# Bucket List Ideas: Sights You Should See on Each Continent ...

The world is filled with amazing sights for you to see. It doesn't matter what continent you live on, because every single one has something impressive located on it. So stop moping about not having the money to travel the world, and start visiting landmarks in your own country. Here's what you should see on each continent:

## 1. Africa

![Victoria Falls,waterfall,body of water,water,water feature,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e7/nk/x9gcn_victoria-falls_waterfall_body-of-water_water_water-feature_600x562.jpg)

If you love nature, then there's plenty to see in Africa. Head to Zambia to see Victoria Falls, which is one of the most beautiful waterfalls in the world. If mountains are more impressive to you, there's Mount Kilimanjaro in Tanzania. And if you'd rather see animal life, you can go on a safari in Kenya Of course, you can't forget to see The Sphinx or the pyramids in Egypt.

## 2. North America

![geographical feature,landform,canyon,cliff,sunset,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/he/ie/cpaga_geographical-feature_landform_canyon_cliff_sunset_600x375.jpg)

Arizona is home to the Grand Canyon, which is one of the most spectacular sights you could ever lay your eyes on. North America also contains Niagara Falls, which you can see from either the USA or Canada. Then there's Mount Rushmore and the Statue of Liberty, which are both impressive, man-made monuments.

## 3. South America

![marine biology,reflection,coral reef,reef,coral,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a1/qi/i401p_marine-biology_reflection_coral-reef_reef_coral_600x450.jpg)

South America has some of the most beautiful scenery in the world. There's the River of Five Colors in Colombia, which will make you appreciate the water in ways you never have before. Then there's the Hand of the Desert in Chile, the Galapogos Islands in Ecuador, and Macho Picchu in Peru. There's beauty all around.

## 4. Australia

![coral reef,habitat,reef,marine biology,coral reef fish,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/ld/r6ay5_coral-reef_habitat_reef_marine-biology_coral-reef-fish_600x399.jpg)

The Great Barrier Reef is beyond beautiful, and will make you appreciate ocean life. Then there's the city of Sydney and the Sydney Opera House. There's Uluru-Kata Tjuta National Park and Blue Mountains National Park. There's plenty of opportunities for you to see wildlife you've never seen before. Yes, that includes Kangaroos.

## 5. Asia

![Taj Mahal,mosque,historic site,landmark,building,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/ry/jg12v_taj-mahal_mosque_historic-site_landmark_building_600x450.jpg)

The Great Wall of China is probably the first thing that comes to mind, because it's so iconic. Of course, Asia also holds the Taj Mahal in India and Mount Everest in Nepal. Then there's the Temple of Heaven in China and Nagashima Spa Land in Japan. There's even a Disney theme park in Tokyo!

## 6. Europe

![Edinburgh Castle,castle,historic site,fortification,building,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u9/hm/aoy5u_edinburgh-castle_castle_historic-site_fortification_building_600x450.jpg)

Europe has so many amazing things to see that it'll take you years to fit it all into your schedule. There's the Eiffel Tower and The Louvre in Paris. Michaelangelo’s famous statue of David is in Florence. The London Eye and Buckingham Palace are in London. The Anne Frank House and Van Gogh Museum are in Amsterdam. And there are plenty of beautiful castles in Edinburgh that will make you wish you were rich.

## 7. Antarctica

![painting,vehicle,boat,sea,bay,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y4/ub/qfa8c_painting_vehicle_boat_sea_bay_600x402.jpg)

There's plenty of amazing pieces of scenery to view in Antarctica. You can go on a cruise in order to get a close up look at some icebergs. You could even visit Deception Island, which was formed by a volcanic eruption. Then you could visit the Port Lockroy museum, which is the only post office on the entire continent. If that's not exciting enough for you, head to Observation Hill to get a good view of Mount Erebus and the Ross Ice Shelf.

There are so many amazing things to see around the world that it's impossible to fit them all on this list. So once you're done visiting all of these landmarks, there's plenty more for you to experience. Where is the most beautiful place you've ever been?
