# 34 Essential Sights of Seattle for Every Visitor ...

Some of the sights of Seattle are famous all around the world thanks to TV's Frasier and Starbucks, but there's so much more to the Pacific Northwest city than a skyline and coffee. Get your ticket because we're going on a photo tour of the sights of Seattle.

## 1. Seattle

![Seattle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bo/ju/nl79rlij_468x765.jpg)

Via [The Perfect Weekend Getaway in ...](https://goo.gl/N1E6qa)
Seattle seems to have it all. An imposing mountain in the background (Mount Rainier), a beautiful harbor and not far away from stunning natural scenery. There are plenty of sights of Seattle for all tastes.

## 2. Freeway Park

![Freeway Park](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/wu/xrdghudq_427x640.jpg)

Via [Concrete & Architecture](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/524950900284954069/)
Freeway Park is a great city park where you can take time to relax from your travels.

## 3. Chocolate Tour

![Chocolate Tour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/az/yl/ye5xn64q_383x640.jpg)

Via [Theo Chocolate](https://goo.gl/1OKPR1)
Did you think Seattle was all about the coffee? Wrong! Chocolate tours are among the popular attractions of Seattle with Fran's, Chocolopolis, Theo and more.

## 4. The Seattle Underground

_ Via Seattle Underground Tour 3_
A network of underground passageways and basements that was ground level at the city's origin in the mid-19th century.

## 5. The Fremont Troll

![The Fremont Troll](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/vm/rmw28hoj_550x368.jpg)

Via [The Fremont Troll - A ...](https://goo.gl/4xktHn)
Under Seattle's impressive Aurora Bridge lurks a huge troll, known to grab small cars passing by.

## 6. Pier 59

![Pier 59](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tb/d1/b0r7fmb2_421x637.jpg)

Via [The world is not in ...](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/173881235587257311/)
The downtown waterfront looks out at West Seattle and the Puget Sound. It's a fun place to walk and explore. You'll find places to sit and take in the view, places to enjoy a quick snack, and places for fine dining. You can watch the ferries and container ships travel across Elliott Bay. There are fun souvenir and novelty shops, art galleries and a family-friendly arcade. You'll see sea mammals and creatures of all kinds, whether at the Seattle Aquarium or along the shoreline. And let's not forget the Great Wheel.

## 7. Suzzallo Library at University of Washington

![Suzzallo Library at University of Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bu/h8/vjucwrfn_364x554.jpg)

Via [49 Breathtaking Libraries From All ...](https://www.buzzfeed.com/mattortile/49-breathtaking-libraries-from-all-over-the-world?sub=2432158_1377362)
One of the most gorgeous libraries in North America.

## 10. Pioneer Square

![Pioneer Square](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fe/n7/epc3txt8_515x918.jpg)

## 11. Christmastime

![Christmastime](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/g3/d3qabcc2_450x677.jpg)

Via [Macy's Holiday Parade & Star/Tree ...](https://goo.gl/2l3mTX)

## 12. Pike Place Market

![Pike Place Market](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j8/jq/adajuc2p_600x899.jpg)

Via [Pike Place Market](https://roadtrippers.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 13. Century Link Field

![Century Link Field](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/o1/u0ashz1j_600x900.jpg)

## 14. Hat 'n' Boots

## 16. Museum of History and Industry

![The Space Needle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z8/v4/wwlclv6i_596x900.jpg)

## 20. The Original Starbucks

![The Original Starbucks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j0/g7/h8tyo414_426x640.jpg)

Via [The Original Starbucks](https://www.flickr.com/photos/fatalysis/3758623495/)
Where it all began. The first Starbucks opened March 30 1971 in Pike Place Market and the rest is history!

## 21. Seattle Burlesque

![Seattle Burlesque](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kc/ko/qizjl42c_296x444.jpg)

Via [in progress](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/167829523584419723/)
Trendy Seattle restaurants regularly feature neo-burlesque and cabaret shows with dinner. Venues like the Can Can Kitchen and Cabaret, Club Noc Noc and The Pink Door all host variations on this classic form of entertainment.

## 22. Woodland Park Zoo

![Woodland Park Zoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/kf/y91ehwr9_336x433.jpg)

Via [Crows Sculpture](https://www.flickr.com/photos/marywit/2461774695/)
A 92-acre zoo with nearly 300 species, a vintage carousel, summer concerts and an indoor play space

## 23. Gasworks Park

![Gasworks Park](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/uf/q0bpr1lt_466x700.jpg)

Via [The Compressor Building At Gasworks ...](https://david-patterson.pixels.com/featured/the-compressor-building-at-gasworks-park-seattle-washington-david-patterson.html)
What makes this park so unusual is the incorporation of pieces of the gas plant that used to operate there, hence the name Gas Works.

## 24. Chinatown

![Chinatown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e5/xo/uujanp4k_550x825.jpg)

## 25. Alki Beach

![Alki Beach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/a1/yrkwcx4n_427x640.jpg)

Via [Alki Beach Sunrise](https://www.flickr.com/photos/10832613@N03/3525571232/)

## 26. Alki Beach Houses

![Alki Beach Houses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zj/bg/zumx76jm_427x640.jpg)

## 27. Olympic Sculpture Park

## 28. Chihuly Glass Museum

Dale Chihuly is probably the world's most eminent glass artist. You'll be amazed at his incredible creations.

## 29. Hiram M. Chittenden Locks

![Hiram M. Chittenden Locks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/i1/tzm9ovbi_337x450.jpg)

Also known as the Ballard Locks, the complex sits at the west end of Salmon Bay, part of Seattle, Washington's Lake Washington Ship Canal.
