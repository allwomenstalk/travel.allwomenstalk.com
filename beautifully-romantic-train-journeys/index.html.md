# 7 Beautifully Romantic Train Journeys ...

Commuting by train daily can really put you off rail travel, but there are some romantic train journeys that will spark a love of riding the rails. Romantic rail journeys will take you through gloriously scenic places you’re sure to fall in love with and maybe kindle a little extra loving in your relationship along the way. And if you want to travel them alone – who says you can’t?

## 1. The West Highland Line, Scotland

![The West Highland Line, Scotland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ya/fn/edu647qt_473x338.jpg)

Scotland's highlands are well known for their majestic beauty, and the West Highland Line takes you through all the best bits, making it one of the most romantic train journeys you could embark on. The railway can take you from Glasgow to Fort William, crossing the Glenfinnan Viaduct, which will surely make Harry Potter fans feel like they’re on cloud nine. Other highlights you shouldn't miss out on include the ancient Dumbarton Castle and the grandiose Arrochar Alps.

## 2. Pretoria to Durban, South Africa

The Blue Train can offer you one of the most luxurious and romantic rail journeys you've ever dreamed of, traversing the country between two great cities. Imagine snuggling up with your loved one in a comfy double bed, watching a movie from your own private suite or admiring the beautiful view from the panoramic windows. You can also relax in your full-size bathtub or pamper yourself even further by taking advantage of the butler service offered on the train.

## 3. Adelaide to Darwin, Australia

![Adelaide to Darwin, Australia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/kh/cacqj272_600x375.jpg)

The route between Adelaide and Darwin will take four days to cover, but when travelling on a luxurious train like The Ghan, you'll never want the journey to end. The train features cabins with large double beds and en-suite bathrooms, offering maximum comfort. Near Alice Springs, the train stops at the Macdonnell Ranges for a while, where you can enjoy a romantic dinner under the stars. What's more, there's an off-train excursion that offers you an entire day to explore the town of Coober Pedy, famous for its underground establishments.

## 4. White Pass Summit Excursion, Canada

![White Pass Summit Excursion, Canada](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q1/qr/oicctdn8_600x450.jpg)

Snuggle up to your loved one in your vintage carriage as you follow the original route to the White Pass Summit, and enjoy the breathtaking view of the glaciers, mountains, gorges, tunnels, waterfalls and historic sites you'll be passing by. One of the most romantic train journeys in the world, the White Pass Summit Excursion passes the Inspiration Point, Bridal Veil Falls, and Dead Horse Gulch.

## 5. Moscow to Vladivostok, Russia

![Moscow to Vladivostok, Russia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/we/il/l6xgl5j4_600x375.jpg)

Enjoy the railway journey of a lifetime taking the luxurious Golden Eagle Trans-Siberian Express Train to Vladivostok, passing by the largest freshwater lake in the world, Baikal, and through the majestic Ural Mountains. The Golden Eagle boasts luxury suites with under-floor climate control, complimentary mini-bars, living areas and en-suite bathrooms equipped with scented toiletries and comfy, comfy plush bathrobes! Can you imagine a more amazing getaway with your loved one?

## 6. Zermatt to Davos, Switzerland

![Zermatt to Davos, Switzerland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/za/oi/ck4rbmb9_472x337.jpg)

The Glacier Express is called "the train to fall in love on" for good reason. Taking you through the Alps from Zermatt to Davos, the train goes through over ninety tunnels and crosses almost 300 bridges, offering plenty of opportunities for stolen kisses. Definitely one of the most romantic train journeys by any standards! If you gather the strength to take your eyes off of your loved one, you'll have the opportunity to admire the Landwasser Viaduct and the Oberalp Pass, as well as the marvelous Swiss mountain scenery that unfolds outside your cabin's panoramic windows.

## 7. Ollantaytambo to Machu Picchu, Peru

![Ollantaytambo to Machu Picchu, Peru](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/kd/xyc6m9hh_550x366.jpg)

If trekking the Inca trail with a huge backpack on your shoulders isn't your exactly your idea of romance, you may want to hop on the luxurious Belmond Hiram Bingham and enjoy an amazing – and so comfortable – journey from Ollantaytambo to Machu Picchu. Bringing the 1920's style back to life, the train is furbished in gleaming brass and polished wood and equipped with large, comfortable armchairs. Make sure to check out the observation car - the best place to relax and take in the magical Peruvian landscape!

Romantic rail travel really isn’t anything like the daily commute or a ride into the city for a day of shopping. The trains are luxurious and the scenery breathtaking. Would you like to take one of the world’s great romantic train journeys?
