# 7 Places to Travel on a Budget ...

If you want to go on a holiday and don’t want to burn a hole in your pocket, this article provides you with 7 destinations where you can travel on a budget. Apart from the usual money saving tactics like traveling during off peak periods, making bookings in advance and staying away from the mini bar, these places are genuinely cheaper to visit, requiring you to spend under $30 a day. Here are 7 places you can travel on a budget.

## 1. Thailand

Image source: [s3prod.weheartit.netdna-cdn.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://s3prod.weheartit.netdna-cdn.com/images/5520175/982596-11-1291577802340_large.jpg?1292342731)

Thailand is a wonderful holiday spot if you’re looking for excellent beaches, mouthwatering seafood and unbeatable shopping. Plan your holiday well, allocating 2 to 3 days in Phuket where you can explore pristine beaches and excellent cave explorations. You can also spend some time in Bangkok, home to some of the best golf courses in Asia and the biggest shopping malls in the world.

## 2. Kenya

![Kenya](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2011/01/kenya_ydidxaxvz_494x492.jpg)

Kenya allows you the luxury of enjoying the spoils Africa without the usual high price. If you’re looking to go on a wildlife safari, Kenya offers some of the best packages available at a fraction of the price you would pay in South Africa. You can enjoy a comfortable stay at some of the better resorts in the country without having to scrounge on your foreign exchange.

## 3. Fiji

![Fiji](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2011/01/fiji_05wjbuvlo_500x375.jpg)

Fiji is one of those astounding places on the planet that has beautiful beaches, lush rainforests, varied marine life and rocky highlands. When you decide to visit Fiji, one of the best places to travel on a budget, you get to experience all types of terrain along with the infectious warmth of the Fijian people. A definite holiday destination for the adventurous and laid back traveler.

## 4. Turkey

Image source: [Darrell Godliman](https://www.flickr.com/photos/darrellg/473386671/in/set-72157623602621784/)

Turkey is a country rich in history and culture offering you many sights to see and exotic cuisine to taste. The Turks are notoriously warm to foreign guests making you feel at home from the moment you step off the plane. The mesmerizing architecture, ancient ruins mingled with modern buildings gives this country a unique flair.

## 5. India

India is an exciting place to visit for Americans especially since so much of it is seen on TV these days with movies like Slumdog Millionaire and Eat Pray Love. The best part about visiting India for Americans is how much stronger the dollar is compared to the rupee. This allows you to enjoy substantial comforts and explore exciting sights on a budget.

## 6. Egypt

A holiday in Egypt is an exciting proposition for any traveler and the fact that you can fully enjoy all the sights on a budget, makes it all the more attractive. Enjoy a cruise on the Nile and take in the majestic pyramids. Explore the desert terrain while enjoying authentic Middle Eastern cuisine. It is definitely a holiday for the memory books.

## 7. China

China is an interesting place to travel on a budget. It provides the winning combination of budget accommodation, food and sightseeing along with mesmerizing cultural experiences. The country is heavily geared towards tourism and foreign visitors; particularly Americans are given a hearty welcome. A word of warning for non-meat eaters, there aren’t many choices available so if you are a pure vegetarian, give China a miss.

Traveling to exotic destinations gives you a chance to explore and experience new and varied cultures. The fact that these are also good places to travel on a budget makes them all the more alluring.
