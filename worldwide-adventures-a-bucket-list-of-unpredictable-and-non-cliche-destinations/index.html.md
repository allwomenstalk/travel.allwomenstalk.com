# Worldwide Adventures: a Bucket List of Unpredictable and Non\-Clich Destinations ...

Paris, London, Rome, New York, Rio de Janeiro, Tokyo … they head up every travel bucket list going. But what if you have been to those places? Or, what if you don’t actually even want to go to these places? As fantastic as these places are, there are so many other destinations around the world that are worthy of a slot on your bucket list. Here are those places:

## 1. Yerevan, Armenia

![Yerevan Cascade,landmark,archaeological site,vacation,tourism,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kf/hk/z4wan_yerevan-cascade_landmark_archaeological-site_vacation_tourism_464x600.jpg)

The Armenian capital is overlooked by Mount Ararat and the city is filled with amazing architecture. Its people welcome you with a huge, friendly hug.

## 2. New Orleans, USA

![color,night,christmas lights,christmas decoration,evening,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ld/dm/sywzu_color_night_christmas-lights_christmas-decoration_evening_490x755.jpg)

Get your jazz on. A multi-cultural and most happening city. Must see Vieux Carré (French Quarter) and Bourbon Street.

## 3. Reykjavik, Iceland

![Reykjavík,town,residential area,neighbourhood,geographical feature,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/ip/g4aam_reykjavik_town_residential-area_neighbourhood_geographical-feature_480x640.jpg)

One of the world's most artistic cities. Spend an evening in a pub where locals entertain with stories and songs.

## 4. Killarney, Ireland

![Ross Castle,sky,reflection,nature,atmospheric phenomenon,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n5/ab/xssx4_ross-castle_sky_reflection_nature_atmospheric-phenomenon_600x900.jpg)

See authentic Ireland. Nature, history and culture combine to give Killarney a special energy.

## 5. Isle of Skye, Scotland

![mountainous landforms,nature,mountain,landform,wilderness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/po/cg/qhf89_mountainous-landforms_nature_mountain_landform_wilderness_600x863.jpg)

One of the most stunning islands of the UK. Must eat fish and chips in Portree.

## 6. Galle, Sri Lanka

![sea,sailing,ocean,wind wave,wave,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sp/pd/sa3xc_sea_sailing_ocean_wind-wave_wave_400x600.jpg)

Beaches, fabulous food and friendly folks - what's not to love? A distinctly colonial feel and a must see is the Portuguese fort.

## 7. Lofoten Islands, Norway

![aurora,atmosphere,PHOTOGR,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pc/jm/se91a_aurora_atmosphere_photogr_534x800.jpg)

One of the most stunningly beautiful places on Earth. Hike, swim, just stand and stare.

## 8. Okinawa, Japan

![beach,shore,sea,body of water,ocean,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mw/ez/q3osm_beach_shore_sea_body-of-water_ocean_479x640.jpg)

Find the secret of long life in the history and culture on a gorgeous island.

## 9. Montreal, Canada

![Notre-Dame Basilica,Notre Dame Basilica,Montreal,Université de Montréal,landmark,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/xb/jbgg5_notre-dame-basilica_notre-dame-basilica_montreal_universite-de-montreal_landmark_478x640.jpg)

Cosmopolitan and urbane with a distinctly European flavor.

## 10. Mestia, Georgia

![mountainous landforms,mountain,landform,geographical feature,wilderness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i4/mc/ijwb7_mountainous-landforms_mountain_landform_geographical-feature_wilderness_479x720.jpg)

Tucked away in an isolated corner of the Caucasus Mountains, this is the place to mingle with the locals. Must drink Georgian wine and hike to a glacier.

## 11. Beirut, Lebanon

![art,wood,flooring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vn/cf/dujzq_art_wood_flooring_600x900.jpg)

Often called the Paris of the Middle East, this is a great place to come and party.

## 12. Gallipoli, Turkey

![Palazzo Sticchi,historic site,landmark,building,palace,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/xp/i8lf7_palazzo-sticchi_historic-site_landmark_building_palace_534x799.jpg)

An amazing site of history (one of the bloodiest battles of World War I), and a gorgeous location on the Aegean Sea.

## 13. Big Sur, California

![sky,afterglow,cloud,sunset,horizon,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/zx/m6s3t_sky_afterglow_cloud_sunset_horizon_400x600.jpg)

A world away from the huge metropolises of California. It's the place to soak in views and hear the crash of waves on cliffs.

## 14. Freetown Christiana, Denmark

![Freetown Christiania,mural,window,art,psychedelic art,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eq/co/zwt3k_freetown-christiania_mural_window_art_psychedelic-art_534x800.jpg)

Indulge your inner hippy in the artsy, free-governing city within a city (Christiania is part of Copenhagen).

## 15. Juqueí, Brazil

![shore,sea,beach,body of water,coast,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/td/j7/cjodn_shore_sea_beach_body-of-water_coast_600x900.jpg)

As well as beautiful beaches, this is seafood heaven, Foodies take note,

## 16. Varanasi, India

![town,tourism,cityscape,waterway,boat,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/rt/v25wn_town_tourism_cityscape_waterway_boat_423x640.jpg)

One of the oldest continuously inhabited cities on Earth. Visit here and experience the real soul of India.

## 17. Broome, Australia

![sunrise,sunset,horizon,dawn,morning,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u8/f4/y2u8n_sunrise_sunset_horizon_dawn_morning_427x640.jpg)

Yep, you really can ride a camel on a stunning beach in Australia.

## 18. Zaanse Schans, the Netherlands

![sky,tree,grass,cloud,windmill,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/v3/oxzmk_sky_tree_grass_cloud_windmill_424x640.jpg)

An easy trip from Amsterdam, this is the land of Dutch windmills and dykes.

## 19. Tulum, Mexico

![coast,nature,shore,sea,landform,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yj/s9/zgl2n_coast_nature_shore_sea_landform_560x850.jpg)

A magnificent mix of history and beaches. Mayan ruins right next to superb stretches of sand.

## 20. Plitvice Lakes National Park, Croatia

![waterfall,body of water,watercourse,water,water feature,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ja/zw/j7r2b_waterfall_body-of-water_watercourse_water_water-feature_600x759.jpg)

This UNESCO site is one of the most magnificent natural wonders of Europe. Discover lakes, waterfalls and caves, and a host of wildlife.

## 21. Cartagena, Columbia

![town,landmark,house,facade,cityscape,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/k6/wpzwz_town_landmark_house_facade_cityscape_471x700.jpg)

This colonial gem will charm you with its architecture, history and beaches.

## 22. Taman Negara National Park, Malaysia

![habitat,tree,vegetation,wilderness,natural environment,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u1/an/dmewc_habitat_tree_vegetation_wilderness_natural-environment_428x640.jpg)

Walk a tree canopy through the jungle and see if you can spot some of the world's most endangered species of tigers, rhinos, leopards and Asian elephants.

## 23. Belgrade, Serbia

![Cathedral of Saint Sava,night,metropolis,darkness,midnight,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ww/f6/i2pm0_cathedral-of-saint-sava_night_metropolis_darkness_midnight_550x864.jpg)

A pretty much undiscovered gem among Europe's capital cities.

## 24. Inle Lake, Myanmar

![boating,sea,vehicle,morning,kayak,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h5/ju/qni5e_boating_sea_vehicle_morning_kayak_500x747.jpg)

Discover a simple way of life among the humble people of the beautiful country around Lake Inle.

## 25. Gothenburg, Sweden

![vehicle,land vehicle,metropolitan area,transport,town,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kj/aj/aasx4_vehicle_land-vehicle_metropolitan-area_transport_town_438x640.jpg)

Known for its artistic and creative side, Gothenburg is one of the friendliest cities in Europe.

## 26. Isla De Vieques, Puerto Rico

![beach,body of water,shore,sea,coast,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cj/fu/ef0rx_beach_body-of-water_shore_sea_coast_600x782.jpg)

Sometimes, all you need is crystalline waters and pristine sandy beaches.

## 27. Rumbur Valley, Pakistan

![mountainous landforms,landform,wilderness,mountain,geological phenomenon,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ud/oq/s0m7y_mountainous-landforms_landform_wilderness_mountain_geological-phenomenon_600x897.jpg)

A real opportunity to escape the modern world and live off the grid without electricity or cell phone signals.

## 28. Ljubljana, Slovenia

![night,reflection,waterway,canal,evening,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bp/r6/vwjwb_night_reflection_waterway_canal_evening_493x750.jpg)

A capital city with plenty to explore. Great nightlife too.

## 29. Eilat, Israel

![bird's eye view,aerial photography,town,marina,residential area,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gk/yq/basec_birds-eye-view_aerial-photography_town_marina_residential-area_600x667.jpg)

Superb resorts and a fabulous place to learn to scuba dive.

## 30. El Chaltén, Argentina

![Monte Fitz Roy,waterfall,mountainous landforms,body of water,wilderness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sj/sk/pwkk6_monte-fitz-roy_waterfall_mountainous-landforms_body-of-water_wilderness_600x900.jpg)

This village with a fab hippy vibe may be small but it sits among some of the most magnificent mountain scenery in the world.

## 31. Bruges, Belgium

![Bruges,reflection,river,water,body of water,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o5/ri/ebbqd_bruges_reflection_river_water_body-of-water_427x640.jpg)

Discover canals, lace, beer and chocolate in a magnificent medieval setting.

## 32. Knysna, South Africa

![coast,landform,geographical feature,sea,promontory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/ng/vqwry_coast_landform_geographical-feature_sea_promontory_380x600.jpg)

Use as a base to discover South Africa's very special Garden Route.

## 33. Monasterio De Piedra Park, Spain

![Monasterio de Piedra,landform,sea cave,cave,water feature,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sb/q2/qry43_monasterio-de-piedra_landform_sea-cave_cave_water-feature_466x700.jpg)

Love history? Love hiking? You'll love this scenic waterfall park that includes the site of a Moorish monastery.

## 34. Ulan Bator, Mongolia

![Genghis Khan,statue,landmark,monument,sculpture,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v2/xo/ft2eo_genghis-khan_statue_landmark_monument_sculpture_600x902.jpg)

One of the remotest destinations of the planet but the journey and the effort is so worth it. It oozes history and culture with masses to see.

## 35. White Sands, New Mexico, USA

![coast,shore,ocean,modern art,painting,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/hn/gbrig_coast_shore_ocean_modern-art_painting_433x640.jpg)

One of the world's most unique landscapes. The gypsum (white sand) dunes cover an area of 275 square miles.

## 36. Rotorua, New Zealand

![habitat,body of water,ecosystem,botany,pond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w1/x3/w01be_habitat_body-of-water_ecosystem_botany_pond_425x640.jpg)

Scenic and relaxing, the mud pools and geysers are a world away from the adrenaline and adventure capital of Queenstown.

## 37. Kazan, Russia

![Kazan Kremlin, Qolsharif Mosque,landmark,building,place of worship,byzantine architecture,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hh/vm/trag6_kazan-kremlin-qolsharif-mosque_landmark_building_place-of-worship_byzantine-architecture_427x640.jpg)

Kazan presents a wonderful combination of Christianity and Islam and a palette of Europe and Asia.

## 38. Corsica, France

![town,geographical feature,body of water,landmark,sea,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/hj/t2ldt_town_geographical-feature_body-of-water_landmark_sea_424x640.jpg)

An incredibly beautiful Mediterranean island with a fabulous coastline and scenic and atmospheric interior.

## 39. Jiuzhaigou Valley Scenic and Historic Interest Area, China

![Pearl Shoal Waterfall,waterfall,nature,body of water,wilderness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/ib/lyoe2_pearl-shoal-waterfall_waterfall_nature_body-of-water_wilderness_496x898.jpg)

The UNESCO Heritage Listed park is home to some of the most photogenic waterfalls in the world.

## 40. Chavin De Huantar, Peru

![mountainous landforms,landform,geographical feature,wilderness,mountain,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/vf/k6w1r_mountainous-landforms_landform_geographical-feature_wilderness_mountain_462x600.jpg)

Slow down a pace and explore the Inca site which is centuries older than Machu Picchu.

## 41. Lake Garda, Italy

![Lake Garda,sea,body of water,landform,geographical feature,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bi/hn/l2a94_lake-garda_sea_body-of-water_landform_geographical-feature_428x640.jpg)

Italy's lake country is the perfect antidote to the cares of the world.

## 42. Isfahan, Iran

![dome,landmark,mosque,estate,water feature,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gi/h5/np0n3_dome_landmark_mosque_estate_water-feature_600x832.jpg)

Some of the most magnificent examples of Islamic architecture you'll find anywhere.

## 43. Ha Long Bay, Vietnam

![landform,geographical feature,sea,rock,coast,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ng/eh/hldrj_landform_geographical-feature_sea_rock_coast_600x900.jpg)

Mysterious and magical, a place to rejuvenate the soul.

## 44. Budva, Montenegro

![Budva the Old Town,water,sea,rock,wood,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/hi/l2w5e_budva-the-old-town_water_sea_rock_wood_480x640.jpg)

One of the smallest countries in Europe has a big capacity to party. Budva is a charming old town perfect for the beach by day, bars by night.

## 45. Atacama Desert, Chile

![mountainous landforms,mountain,geographical feature,landform,wilderness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/de/vq/l8ms3_mountainous-landforms_mountain_geographical-feature_landform_wilderness_600x898.jpg)

Hike by day and be in awe of the amazing scenery and then at night turn your eyes skyward for some stargazing.

## 46. Zanzibar, Tanzania

![sea,ocean,shore,beach,sea turtle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t2/hp/u1b7b_sea_ocean_shore_beach_sea-turtle_600x900.jpg)

The picture says it all - a tropical paradise.

## 47. Seoraksan National Park, South Korea

![atmospheric phenomenon,wilderness,cloud,mist,mountain,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z8/jz/lqpt6_atmospheric-phenomenon_wilderness_cloud_mist_mountain_566x794.jpg)

Find peace with yourself and the world in the tranquility of the mountains.

## 48. Bhutan

![mountainous landforms,nature,mountain,geographical feature,landform,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xu/pm/e3o23_mountainous-landforms_nature_mountain_geographical-feature_landform_600x840.jpg)

There's something to be said for a country that measures its economy on a happiness quota rather than dollars.

## 49. Freycinet National Park, Tasmania, Australia

![Wineglass Bay Beach,landform,sea,coast,ocean,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/q9/x4lmh_wineglass-bay-beach_landform_sea_coast_ocean_366x488.jpg)

Wineglass Bay will seduce into wanting to stay in Tasmania forever

## 50. Isla Margarita, Venezuela

![coast,shore,beach,sea,body of water,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cj/r8/jbgsa_coast_shore_beach_sea_body-of-water_483x640.jpg)

One of the lesser-known Caribbean destinations but there's nothing underwhelming about this gorgeous place.
