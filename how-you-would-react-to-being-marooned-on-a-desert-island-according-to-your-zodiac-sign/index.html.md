# How You Would React to Being Marooned on a Desert Island According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

It’s a conversation game that we all like to play with your friends and family every so often, the game of what would you do if you were marooned on a desert island! We aren’t all Tom Hanks in Castaway; he dealt with it pretty well I think you can agree! It’s the sort of situation that you can’t really predict until you are actually experiencing it, but it’s still fun to speculate anyway! Astrology can even hold some answers for you. Here is **how you would react to being marooned on a desert island according to your zodiac sign**!

## 1. Aries

You are so laid back that you would thrive on not having a phone signal or company! You’d actually enjoy having the time to sort out plans for yourself for once rather than having to follow someone’s lead.

## 2. Taurus

You are naturally very instinctive, which will stand you in good stead when it comes to facing obstacles and dodging bullets in the form of poisonous berries and wild animals!

## 3. Gemini

You don’t get scared by anything, so your time on the desert island would definitely be more Peter Pan than Robin Williams in Jumanji! Your adventurous spirit would lead to overcoming all obstacles.

## 4. Cancer

You would cry for days initially, but your knack for gaining perspective would soon make you see that the only way forward is to be strong and adaptable.

## 5. Leo

You are a sucker for details, so it’s safe to assume that you will come up with the most intricate and foolproof escape plan that the world has ever seen!

## 6. Virgo

Shock will be your primary emotion, but as time goes on you will start to collect yourself again and your best quality will be a talent for building safe and sturdy huts!

## 7. Libra

You are so dependent on your family and friends to supply you with the support to keep going, that the thought of never seeing them again will spur you on to escape from the island no matter what.

## 8. Scorpio

To be honest, nothing fazes you at all. You will probably write HELP in the sand as big as you can, then lay back and get a suntan as you wait for a plane to fly over!

## 9. Sagittarius

You are a totally logical person, so your best route of escape will definitely involve searching the stars at night to work out your directional awareness. A message in a bottle never hurts either!

## 10. Capricorn

If you’re being honest, big crowds and life responsibilities are not your favourite, so you might just flourish in this deserted island and be the happiest you have been in a long time!

## 11. Aquarius

You feel at home near and amongst the water, so your best skill will be to to fashion a raft and try your luck against the waves!

## 12. Pisces

You enjoy your own company more than the company of others, so you would have absolutely no problem talking to yourself and staying alert as you wait for a search and rescue team to arrive!
