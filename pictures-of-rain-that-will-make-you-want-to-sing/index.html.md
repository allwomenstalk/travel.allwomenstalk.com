# 92 Pictures of Rain That Will Make You Want to Sing ...

As I live in Britain and the country is known for its inclement weather, you may wonder why I love to look at pictures of rain. There is, however, a world of difference between the rain lashing down outside, especially in summer when it has maybe upset your plans for a country picnic, and a beautifully arty picture of the perfect rainy moment. I love pictures of rain, whether the rain is the star or a bit player, and here they are. (And I found you lots of lovely quotes about rain and song lyrics too.)
