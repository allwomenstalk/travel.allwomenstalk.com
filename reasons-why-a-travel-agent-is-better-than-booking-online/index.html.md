# 7 Reasons Why a Travel Agent is Better than Booking Online ...

Do you still use a travel agent? It seems so much easier nowadays to book a holiday with using just the internet and your credit card. But in some cases, by not booking your holiday through a travel agent you could be missing out on a lot of time and money saving opportunities. Websites such as CheapTickets.com and Travelocity give customers the chance to book cheaper deals in their own time. However, travel agents are professionals in the fight for the best deals, so are you really saving time and money by not using them? Here are 7 Reasons Why a Travel Agent is Better than Booking Online.

## 1. If You Have a Busy Schedule

Trawling through the internet, trying to find the best deal for you, can be extremely time consuming. Because there are so many options available to you online, sometimes you can get bogged down with the task at hand. Booking via a professional travel agent means they do all the leg work quickly. So you can continue your busy lifestyle knowing that you have a holiday to look forward to at the end of it.

## 2. If You’re Planning a Cruise

Cruise specialist agents have the right facilities at hand to get you the best deals. When they spot them, they can also get you cabin upgrades or discounts with their sharp know-how. With the sheer number of cruise options available, it is extremely handy to hire someone who knows what they’re doing.

## 3. If You’re New to Travelling

Leaving the country for the first time? First time you’ll ever step on a plane? Feeling a bit daunted by it all? Do not worry, because a travel agent knows all the ropes which you might be intimidated by. They will be able to guide you through exactly what you will be doing, how, where and how much. For a first time traveler, an agent is better than booking online because they can prepare you about anything you need to know.

## 4. You’re Planning a Complicated Itinerary

If you’re going to be moving around a lot on your travels, and packaged tours aren’t your thing, a travel agent can be invaluable. An experienced agent will be able to put together your complicated trip quickly and save you a lot of hassle and money trying to do it yourself.

## 5. You Require Special Needs Travel

An elderly or Special Needs passenger will benefit from an agent’s ability to ensure the best care is given to their clients. They can ensure you’re seated near medical equipment, wheelchairs and oxygen masks. An added bonus is that they will search specifically for easy to access and comfortable accommodation.

## 6. You’re Going to a Natural Disaster Hotspot

Hurricanes are no strangers to disrupting trips and losing you a lot of money. Your agent can book you into hotels or resorts that refund part of your costs if they are hit by a natural disaster. They will also help you find a way back home as well as assist in obtaining a refund because they are hired to handle your travel problems.

## 7. For Unforeseen Circumstances

Say your airline goes on strike, declares bankruptcy or the vehicle breaks down. An agent is priceless in these scenarios. They can get you alternative transportation quickly, saving you a lot of time and letting you be on your way. You should always carry your agent’s details in case of emergency.

Working with someone and knowing that there is a professional travel agent out there to help you for your traveling needs will make your holidays or business trips run that much smoother. These 7 Reasons Why a Travel Agent is Better than Booking Online demonstrate how there are many common reasons why you would be saving yourself time and money by hiring a professional. If you have any comments on how a travel agent helped you out in a pickle, please add them below.
