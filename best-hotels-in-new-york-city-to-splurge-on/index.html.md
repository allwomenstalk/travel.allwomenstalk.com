# 10 Best Hotels in New York City to Splurge on ...

The best hotels in NYC are known for being completely luxurious. You get more than the amenities; you get to feel exclusive, pampered, and incredibly rich – even if you're just splurging for a night or two. You might not be able to afford a week long stay, you might not be able to afford luxury hotels all of the time, but if there's any time to treat yourself, it's definitely while you're on vacation – especially if you happen to be visiting New York, one of the most exciting, glamorous cities in the whole world. There are lots of luxurious hotels in New York City to choose from, it all depends on where you want to stay and the kind of atmosphere you want to enjoy while you're there. So, take a look at some of the best NYC hotels and decide what's right for you when you want to indulge!

## 1. The Plaza

![The Plaza](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/1_the-plaza_600x525.jpg)

In addition to being one of the best hotels in NYC, the Plaza is an outright landmark, only partly owing to the fact that it ranges along iconic Fifth Avenue. It has been a scene stealing setting in dozens of movies, but it's even more incredible in real life. Not only are you centrally located when you stay there, you can also take advantage of the many amazing facilities. The hotel offers babysitting, there's a butler available on every floor, it has its own mall, there are bars and restaurants, ball rooms, and it goes without saying that you can take advantage of some of the most fabulous spa services found anywhere.

[theplaza.com](https://goo.gl/WVskkF)

## 2. The Pierre

_ The Pierre is another stunning Fifth Avenue hotel. Its incredible views of Central Park are just one of the reasons it's considered the best hotel in NYC by many. The hotel is absolutely stunning, due in no small part to the fact that it stands in the Historic District of the Upper East Side. The architecture is beautiful both inside and out – you'll be thrilled just by the sight of the rotunda – and it, too, has been featured in a variety of famous films. Guests enjoy all the modern amenities, not to mention classic pampering in every way you can imagine.

tajhotels.com_

## 3. The Ritz-Carlton

![The Ritz-Carlton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/3_the-ritz-carlton_600x525.jpg)

The Ritz-Carlton are now the best hotels the Marriott has to offer, and the Ritz-Carlton New York, located in Battery Park, is utterly amazing. It's huge, with rooms that offer breathtaking views of the harbor – you can even see the Statue of Liberty right outside your window! From plush beds to enormous suites, you'll feel like you've died and gone to hotel heaven when you book a stay at the Ritz.

[ritzcarlton.com](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/new-york)

## 4. The Peninsula New York

![The Peninsula New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/4_the-peninsula-new-york_600x517.jpg)

It seems like all the best hotels in NYC are associated with famous Fifth Avenue in some way, and the Peninsula is no exception. It's a serious luxury hotel and it's considered one of the greatest hotels not just in New York, but in the world. As befitting such a distinguished hotel, it has tons of amazing features, from its own limousine service to gourmet restaurants, lounges, a fitness center, and a huge spa. If you're looking to splurge, this is definitely the place to do it!

[peninsula.com](https://www.peninsula.com/en/new-york/5-star-luxury-hotel-midtown-nyc)

## 5. The St. Regis New York

![The St. Regis New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/5_the-st-regis-new-york_600x525.jpg)

The St. Regis is another best NYC hotel with a gorgeous Fifth Avenue vantage point. It also has a five star rating from Forbes and a five diamond rating from AAA, so you really can't go wrong. It has a very storied history, and was originally built by John Astor (John Jacob Astor IV, to be exact). Naturally, dozens of famous people have stayed there, from beautiful Marlene Dietrich to Salvador Dali, but you, too, can spend time in its luxurious rooms, getting pampered and living the life.

[stregisnewyork.com](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/nycxr-the-st-regis-new-york/overview/?EM=DNM_STREGISNEWYORK.COM)

## 6. Four Seasons New York

![Four Seasons New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/6_four-seasons-new-york_600x526.jpg)

Everyone has heard of the Four Seasons, and there are a variety of hotels at which you can stay. Only at the Four Seasons New York, however, can you enjoy the experience of living it up in Midtown with an astonishing, breathtaking view of Central Park. It boasts one of the most expensive suites in the world, the Ty Warner Penthouse, but all of the rooms are stunning. In addition to taking advantage of the spectacular spy and dining on scrumptious food, you can stay fit, visit the Arcadia Gallery, and enjoy the many family related activities available.

[fourseasons.com](https://www.fourseasons.com/newyork/)

## 7. Mandarin Oriental New York

![Mandarin Oriental New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/7_mandarin-oriental-new-york_600x517.jpg)

The Mandarin Oriental is situated right in the Time Warner Center, and it has five stars worth of luxury appeal. Views include the Hudson River, Central Park, or the spectacular city skyline, depending on your preferences and your price point. Its facilities are nothing short of amazing, and among other things include a fitness center, an incredible spa, a spacious ballroom, the Lobby Lounge, Asiate, and MObar, which is the place to be for anyone who loves the décor of the 1930's and 40's

[mandarinoriental.com](https://goo.gl/QusKRv)

## 8. The Essex House

![The Essex House](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/8_the-essex-house_600x525.jpg)

Another one of the best hotels in NYC is the Essex House, otherwise known as the Jumeirah Essex House. It's right on Central Park and it's been in business, under different names, since 1931. One of my favorite things about this particular hotel has to be its Art Deco décor, which is unquestionably stylish. It has a wonderful restaurant, South Gate, not to mention The Spa which is, of course, a spa, available to guests 24 hours a day. There are lots of banquet rooms and salons which are ideal for weddings and other special occasions.

[jumeirah.com](https://goo.gl/qESu3y)

## 9. Hotel Plaza Athenee

![Hotel Plaza Athenee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/9_hotel-plaza-athenee_600x525.jpg)

The Hotel Plaza Athenee is a stunner, especially if you prefer your hotels with a European flair. It promises all the intimacy and care of a boutique hotel, which is a huge draw for many visits, along with its location on the Upper East Side. Better still, the hotel just underwent a huge reservation, so there are more rooms than ever. There are lots of family friendly activities, but if you're there for a romantic getaway, you can enjoy fine dining, great music, a wonderful lounge, the incredible spa, and the fantastic fitness facilities.

[plaza-athenee.com](https://www.plaza-athenee.com/)

## 10. The Waldorf=Astoria

_ If you're looking for the best NYC hotels, you can't forget about the Waldorf=Astoria. Although it was first located on Fifth Avenue, it is now found on Park Avenue, and it's just as fabulous as it was originally. You really can't do better than the Waldorf; you need only look at its past guest lists to see that. It includes loads of recognizable names, from presidents to postmaster generals, not to mention famous individuals like Marilyn Monroe, Cole Porter, and many more. Given the luxuries you can enjoy at the hotel, it's no wonder it draws so many big names.

waldorfnewyork.com_

As you can see, the best hotels in NYC come in all styles and all levels of luxury. From iconic hotels like the Plaza and the Waldorf-Astora to luxe themed hotels like the Mandarin Oriental and the Plaza Athenee, you'll definitely find something to fit your preferences and make you feel like royalty. What are your favorite luxurious hotels in New York City – or anywhere else in the world?
