# 10 Essentials for a Solo Road Trip ...

You need some **essentials for a solo road trip** if you're hitting the pavement alone. You can certainly have fun and see some great things by yourself, but it's important to consider your safety and general wellbeing so that you don't get lost, victimized or otherwise unhappy. Here are the **essentials for a solo road trip** that you've got to have.

## 1. Great Music

Music sets the vibe for the road trip and keeps your spirits high throughout the long drive. This is definitely one of the top **essentials for a solo road trip**.

## 2. Snacks and Drinks

It's important to stay hydrated and keep snacks for when you're hungry. You'll save money and time by already having drinks and snacks with you.

## 3. GPS/Maps

It's important to know where you are going. Unless of course, you're in Wonderland and the Cheshire Cat says, "If you don't know where you want to go, then it doesn't matter which path you take." I'd recommend bringing a map in case that GPS fails.

## 4. Cell Phone & Charger

It's important to keep in contact with someone that knows where you're traveling in case of an emergency. Also, for listening to music and taking pictures.

## 5. Spontaneous Personality

The best trips are the ones you don't plan. Be open to trying new things and meeting new people along the way.

## 6. Sunglasses

A common item that some people forget, but when that sun is trying to blind you for two hours you will be glad you remembered to bring them.

## 7. Camera

Capture moments from your trip. Wouldn't it be cool to have a group shot from when some skateboarders invited you to shred a pool with them? Then when you get to your destination you have a photo and can brag about how you tried something new and dangerous!

## 8. Comfy Clothing

When you're driving for an extended amount of time you want to be comfortable. Instead of the 4-inch heels opt for some sneakers.

## 9. Emergency Kit

It's best to be prepared in case of an emergency.

## 10. Blankets

Bring blankets in case of an emergency or if you need to pull off and take a nap before continuing your road trip.
