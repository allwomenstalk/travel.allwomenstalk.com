# The Top 10 Most Instagrammable Spots to Check out in Los Angeles ...

What are the **most Instagrammable places in Los Angeles**? LA is a mecca of travel and sightseeing. From celebs to all the gorgeous views, buildings, and neighborhoods, it's no wonder this is one of the hottest tourist destinations ever! Whether you've been to the city before or you're going for your first time, these are 10 absolute **most Instagrammable places in Los Angeles**.

## 1. Griffith Observatory

![outerwear, girl, vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f1/f1/qye9vy0c5a5fb46e30ca1105257498_600x750.jpg)

If you’re into astronomy and the stars, this is a great place to check out. Not only is it FREE to get into the Griffith Observatory, but you get an amazing view of Los Angeles on one side and the Hollywood Sign on the other. It's definitely one of the **most Instagrammable places in Los Angeles**.

## 2. LACMA

![photograph, standing, fashion, tourist attraction, black and white,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ys/m8/pboa2yvx5a5fb4a7608b9997921707_600x750.jpg)

LA is honestly a hot spot for beautiful museums. While there are plenty of exhibits to pose in front of, the Urban Lights sculpture is the most iconic. Try shooting at dusk or nighttime to get the perfect shot!

## 3. Chinatown

![shoulder, leg, girl, thigh, joint,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gd/nh/i9mcwba45a5fb4f2dc9d6809228714_600x658.jpg)

Just a few blocks from Union Station, Chinatown is a must-see (and eat). Snap a pic of the amazing cuisine or the unique architecture.

## 4. The Last Bookstore

![glasses, vision care, inventory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ju/h3/o3kbohhq5a5fb62a50631199611586_600x600.jpg)

Get lost in this music shop/bookstore/art gallery for hours. They have tons of aesthetically pleasing places to snap since they know this is one of the hottest bookstores in LA.

## 5. Little Tokyo

![tree, plant, woody plant, urban area, branch,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/nm/strwm8ah5a5fb6ad4f04c624936538_600x749.jpg)

A cute little spot where you can shop for unique Japanese collectibles, eat sushi, and sing some karaoke. Everything that they sell is amazing and worth sending to your followers.

## 6. Echo Park

![tree, vacation, leisure, plant, tourism,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/ds/n7frsrbf5a5fb6ed517fa809536347_600x519.jpg)

A lake in LA? One wouldn’t think so but this city has anything you can ask for.

## 7. Beverly Hills

![car, road, street, vehicle, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ya/ew/wxpprl3k5a5fb70915d0a100227941_600x749.jpg)

The top two spots to shoot are on Rodeo Drive and The Stair’s at Barney’s New York. Not to mention going is a total callback to late 80’s/early 90’s movies (think Pretty Woman or Clueless).

## 8. Union Station

![ceiling, structure, beam, daylighting, building,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qs/kz/o2jda4oy5a5fb7947951e543252283_600x600.jpg)

Even if you’re not going anywhere, Union Station is definitely worth the visit. It’ll feel like you’ve been transported to the 20s because they’ve kept most of the building looking the same.

## 9. Dodger Stadium

![sport venue, sky, structure, atmosphere, landmark,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s1/dj/xtcyelwn5a5fb7afefb62069395364_600x750.jpg)

One of the biggest draws to L.A in the sports scene. Get a picture of the greenest field in all of L.A. Also try a Dodger Dog - they’re worth it.

## 10. Santa Monica

![sky, cloud, reflection, sea, sunset,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cx/rd/gd71dztq5a5fb7cd4dc12288836479_600x749.jpg)

A little bit of a drive in traffic and you’ll hit the beach. Wait for the sun to go down and you’ll be treated to amazing carnival lights.
