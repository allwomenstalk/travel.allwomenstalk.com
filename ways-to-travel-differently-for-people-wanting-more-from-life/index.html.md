# 6 Ways to Travel Differently for People Wanting More  from Life ...

Traveling is a remarkable experience. It makes you step out of your comfort zone and makes you more open to the outside world. I bet it’s a new step in your life, where you’re not only going to learn about the country you’re visiting, but also embrace your new self in it. As an advocate believer that travelling provides valuable lessons, I recommend this time to travel differently and experience the extraordinary world on your own. Below are 6 ways you can benefit from traveling differently and seeing the world in a completely new light.

## 1. Try to Travel Alone

![rock, canyon, wilderness, geology, formation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jn/hc/w81tcdbn59621ed60bded571146721_540x540.jpg)

You might have been traveling, but, at the same time, you have never left your past life and close ones. As long as you’re with the people who’re close to you, you’ll never get that sense of being reborn. They know everything about you, and you continue to act that way. Nonetheless, it’s high time for you to continue your travels alone. Though this might seem weird, the truth is that sometimes when you decide to travel, the foremost precious companion you’ll ever have is yourself.

## 2. Hang out in the Streets

![photograph, woman, human hair color, beauty, lady,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/f2/ang7lfrl596234a3ab382889437193_600x749.jpg)

Visit the streets where locals go. Eat their food, read their newspapers, watch their series, wear their clothes and act like them. Note that there’s no better travel agency aid than the people in town. Apart from the above, you’ll never receive greater knowledge about the country, it's history, mentality and their every day life than from the locals themselves.

## 3. Avoid Comparisons

![blue, product, advertising, square,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/q0/uvqbgx2459621f02d1d11540644919_600x600.jpg)

Don’t compare a thing – the prices, the people, the food, living standards. Nothin! You’re not travelling to feel superior. You’re travelling to experience a different situation in your life.

## 4. Know You’re Not Alone

![amphitheatre, ancient rome, tourism, arch, archaeological site,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lg/mf/ue8kkzbp5962282810658046507430_600x600.jpg)

It doesn’t matter if you’re travelling alone or not. What does matter is that you will be surrounded by people who will still understand you even when you are only speaking your native language. Body postures, movements and facial expressions are the name of the new game. No matter where you go, people in their majority are kind and you’ll never be alone.

## 5. Take Classes

![girl, photo shoot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/vu/gwbqlbzk59621f45da903259221471_600x603.jpg)

That’s another recommendation. You can invite yourself to classes you did not even know existed before. You can take cooking, dancing, and massage classes. Who knows, maybe this will open the door to a better career in the future!

## 6. Become a Volunteer

![people, child, community, girl, tree,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/ll/vuw8bskm59622a89e4f24599991487_425x282.jpg)

The power of volunteering in a foreign country helps you to develop new skills, teamwork and build experience. It also helps you to cope with different types of people by adjusting to their foreign language, knowledge and daily habits. You will also be sharing your knowledge with people in need. Working so closely with locals for a higher cause will instantly make you feel connected, needed and happy. This kind of travel experience is unique and incomparably precious.
