# 50 Sceneries That Will Make You Fall in Love with New York ...

New York is one of the most exciting states in USA that offers so much history, scenery and social interaction. It has a little bit of every culture! It certainly is a melting pot with a completely equal representation of everyone in the world. New York has a certain 'je ne sais quoi' charm, which gives it such an irresistible quality. If you are not sure what I am talking about, the following points should help you get the feel of it.

## 1. Times Square, New York City, New York

![Times Square, New York City, New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/i6/lys6984y_427x640.jpg)

Via [2008-12-06 New York City - ...](https://www.flickriver.com/photos/68098840@N00/3102630057/)

Times Square is one of the most illuminated places in New York, which looks about the same during the night as it does during the day. It's always busy, lively and filled with constant motion of either the people themselves or the cars!

## 2. Autumn - Central Park, New York City

![Autumn - Central Park, New York City](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zn/oq/k5vnk8ab_375x500.jpg)

Via [Central Park, New York City ...](https://www.flickr.com/photos/vivnsect/5127012165/)

Central Park looks completely different as the seasons change. During the summer it's a social hotspot; during fall it's a completely different environment with the pavement covered in leaves; during winter it's a tranquil and mysterious place; and during spring it's a booming garden!

## 3. Overview of Central Park, New York
