# 7 Best Spas in NYC to Treat Yourself ...

The best spas in NYC will leave you feeling like a brand new person. You may go in stressed out, tensed, fatigued, and generally full of malaise, but you'll come out rejuvenated, relaxed, and relieved of all your stress, with a definite spring in your step. A lot of the best NYC spas are actually located within the city's best hotels, so if you're splurging on a stay in a luxury hotel, you can splurge a little more by indulging at the spa. Take a look at the most fantastic spas in New York City and see which one you'd prefer.

## 1. Caudalie Spa

![Caudalie Spa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/1_caudalie-spa_600x517.jpg)

The Caudalie Spa is more than just one of the best spas in NYC; it's located in one of the city's best hotels as well, the Plaza. Right now, the Vinotherapie Spa is making huge waves, and although it's still relatively new, it is very popular. Other treatments include heavenly body wraps of all kinds – energy wraps, honey and wine wraps, slimming wraps, and more. There are dry body treatments and scrubs, massages of every imaginable type, plus facials, manicures, pedicures, and much more.

[caudalie-usa.com](https://us.caudalie.com/page-not-found)

## 2. Peninsula Hotel Spa

![Peninsula Hotel Spa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/2_peninsula-hotel-spa_600x517.jpg)

The Peninsula Hotel Spa is widely considered the best spa in NYC, and not just because it's part of the fabulous Peninsula Hotel. The spa facilities and treatments are responsible for that reputation. You can, for instance, book a private spa suite, so you can relax in peace or host a truly luxurious girls' day. Whether you take it private or keep it public, the services are to die for. There are holistic massages to go with the body treatments, facials, traditional massages, and specialty wraps, not to mention the ability to go in for head to toe beauty. You can finish your spa experience with a mani/pedi and a wonderful new 'do!

[peninsula.com](https://www.peninsula.com/New_York/en/Wellness/default.aspx)

## 3. Spa Castle

_ If you're looking for something a little more available, Spa Castle is a fantastic choice. After all, the spa promises that, when you go, you get the best of authentic Asian spa treatments and saunas, flawlessly mixed in with wonderful European techniques. There are specialized massages, scrubs, and rubs, and you can get even a scrumptious bite to eat while you're there! It's a one stop shop, great if you want to pack in as many spa treatments as possible.

nyspacastle.com_

## 4. Bliss Spa

![Bliss Spa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/4_bliss-spa_600x516.jpg)

Actually, Bliss Spa is one of the best spas in NYC for lots of different treatments. You're not just looking at standard spa fare here. Don't get me wrong, there are plenty of massages, dry scrubs, and facials from which to choose, however, Bliss also specializes in things like waxing, laser hair removal, and weight loss. And just FYI, if you like the spa but you're only visiting the city, take a look at all the different Bliss spas around the world.

[blissworld.com](http://www.blissworld.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Mandarin Oriental Spa

![Mandarin Oriental Spa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/5_mandarin-oriental-spa_600x517.jpg)

As mentioned, many of the best NYC spas are located in similarly amazing hotels – like this one! The Mandarin Oriental knows what it means to have a fantastic spa. The one there is rated five stars, so you know you'll be getting amazing treatments from skilled individuals. The spa itself includes a fitness center as well as an enormous indoor pool. There are private showers, soaking tubs, private spa suits, an incredible amethyst crystal steaming room, and much, much more.

[mandarinoriental.com](https://www.mandarinoriental.com/new-york/manhattan/luxury-spa)

## 6. Ritz-Carlton Central Park Spa

![Ritz-Carlton Central Park Spa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/6_ritz-carlton-central-park-spa_600x525.jpg)

For many visitors and residents alike, the spa at the Ritz-Carlton is hands down the best NYC spa ever. Called La Prairie, it has everything you could ever want. Considering that it's touted as «Switzerland's first stateside spa,» you can see why it's so popular. You get more than just dozens of fabulous massages, facials, scrubs, and different facilities where you can fully relax and rejuvenate. You get the Ritz-Carlton stamp of approval, and that's saying something.

[ritzcarlton.com](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/new-york/central-park)

## 7. Four Seasons Hotel Spa

![Four Seasons Hotel Spa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/7_four-seasons-hotel-spa_600x526.jpg)

Last but most certainly not least, we have the spa at the Four Seasons. That really says all you need to know, but don't worry, I'm not stopping there! This is another five star spa, which even boasts a Tumblr at FourSeasonsSpas, so you can get up-to-date information and maybe even special deals. Again, at this spa, you get more than just beauty treatments and deep massages – you get the Four Seasons guarantee.

[preview.fourseasons.com](https://www.fourseasons.com/find_a_hotel_or_resort/)

The best spas in NYC are not considered the best because they're located in luxury hotels or because they cater to the rich and famous. They're the best because they employ the best people, the best techniques, and the best facilities. You don't have to be rich or a celebrity to come out feeling like a new person – but you'll feel like you are! Do you know of any other fantastic spas in New York City?
