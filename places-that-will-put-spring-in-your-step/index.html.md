# 7 Places That Will Put Spring in Your Step ...

The places to visit in spring dress magnificently for the season and evoke the visuals, atmosphere and thoughts needed to push the last vestiges of winter from your mind. If you want to kick your winter blues to the curb, these places to visit in spring will ably assist.

## 1. Amsterdam (the Netherlands)

![Amsterdam (the Netherlands)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r4/ti/m7sr2ie7_600x450.jpg)

Amsterdam is one of the most stunning and vibrant cities in the world, providing adventurous international travelers with a myriad of great sights and once in a lifetime experiences. What most people don’t realize, however, is that Amsterdam is also one of the best European holiday places to visit in spring, as beautiful flowers begin to fill the city’s numerous parks, and ducks and geese chauffeur their new hatchlings along the canals. Budget conscious holiday makers will also find that the cooler spring weather generally experienced in the area tends to keep the normally high accommodation prices down, which can make for a very reasonable stay!

## 2. Rocky Mountain National Park (United States of America)

![Rocky Mountain National Park (United States of America)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g8/tf/tjux7owc_600x399.jpg)

One glimpse at the towering peaks and the flora and fauna that comprise Rocky Mountain National Park in Colorado, and it’s easy to see why this breathtaking landscape has inspired many an artist over the years, including John Denver. Travelers looking for a little inspiration of their own will consequently fall in love with the park, especially owing to the fact that it’s one of the most diverse American destinations to visit in spring. Not only does Rocky Mountain National Park show off its stunning variety of spring flowers during the months following its harsh winter, but it is also alive with amazing large animals such as elk, deer, mule, bears, big horn sheep and cougars.

## 3. The Pyrenees (Spain/ France)

![The Pyrenees (Spain/ France)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tl/kb/lo8qawup_600x441.jpg)

One of the most breathtaking places to visit in spring, the Pyrenees mountain range should feature on every seasoned traveler’s bucket list. Straddling the border between France and Spain, the Pyrenees boast stunning spring wildflowers and a multitude of different alpine landscapes, each of which seems to be more beautiful than the last. The tourist attractions, including castles, caves, ruins and amazing churches and monasteries, are also a lot quieter in the spring months as the weather can be a little cool, although cool weather beats waiting in line any day of the week!

## 4. Mt Fuji (Japan)

![Mt Fuji (Japan)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wp/wq/zrls59rl_600x450.jpg)

Japan’s crowning jewel and one of the places to visit in spring, Mt Fuji towers over Honshu Island. Whilst you may not get the clearest view of the mountain during the spring months thanks to the extremely unpredictable weather, the amazing cherry blossoms on display, in addition to the cheaper off-season accommodation, certainly make spring a great time to visit.

## 5. Sognefjord (Norway)

![Sognefjord (Norway)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/i0/fdlnkejx_600x375.jpg)

Sognefjord in Norway provides visitors with some of the most spectacular scenery in the world, whether it be the 1000 meter high sheer cliff faces that skirt the fjord in some places, the beautiful cascading waterfalls that can be found throughout the region or the numerous fantastic hiking trails. Holiday makers that choose to visit the area during spring will also find that the spring fruit blossoms found along some of the smaller branches of the fjord definitely make Sognefjord one of the most magnificent Norwegian spring destinations.

## 6. Halle's Wood (Belgium)

![Halle's Wood (Belgium)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/wo/l778bf1x_600x401.jpg)

A gorgeous 552 hectare forested area in Belgium, Halle’s Wood (or Hallerbos, as it is known in Flemish) definitely deserves to be on a list of the best places to visit in spring, as the region becomes carpeted in stunning bluebell hyacinth flowers, in addition to other wildflowers during April and May. The area is also still relatively unknown and devoid of large crowds, which adds to the beauty of the whole experience.

## 7. South Island (New Zealand)

![South Island (New Zealand)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i4/ed/mp7b4ncb_600x420.jpg)

If you can’t wait for the Northern Hemisphere spring, head down under. New Zealand’s South Island has definitely earned its place at the top of the list of places to visit in spring in the Southern Hemisphere, as the countryside is covered in beautiful blooming plants and vivid green grass, which offer a stunning contrast to the South Island’s mountainous rocky alpine regions. The cooler weather during the spring is also perfect for outdoor activities such as hiking and mountain biking, although rain can roll in at the drop of a hat, so travelers should prepare themselves for all conditions.

When you can’t afford to head off to these marvelous spring destinations, remember the pleasures of spring can be found close to home. You can see Mother Nature come alive in your local park or even in your back yard. As the warmer sun caresses the Earth, life blossoms, so just enjoy it wherever you are. Where are your favorite places that put a spring in your step?
