4 Pink Mountains

Source: The 10 Most Inspiring Natural
Who cares about the cold when the mountains are this beautiful?

***

The pink mountains in the picture are located in the village of Zhangye Danxia in Gansu Province, China. The landscape is made up of red sandstone and minerals that were deposited in the area over 24 million years ago. The vibrant colors of the mountains are due to the oxidation of the iron in the sandstone. The area has been designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 2010 and is a popular tourist attraction. Visitors can explore the area on foot or by bike. The pink mountains make a stunning backdrop for photos and offer an incredible view of the surrounding landscape.
