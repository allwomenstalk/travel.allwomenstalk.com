# 7 Instagram Travel Hacks That Are Borderline Genius ...

I do a lot of traveling ✈️ and throughout all of my travels 🚂 🚆✈️, I've come up with some of the best travel hacks out there! To be honest, these have saved my life on the road! Check them out☝️:

## 1. Use Space Saving Bags 🏬

![vacation, swimming pool, Yongan,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o9/gi/nlyu3ife578bd3c56db71434738647_450x450.jpg)

So, I go on a lot of work-trips and this genius travel hack never popped into my mind! I didn't think that I'd actually be able to pack away everything in the world that I need/want to take … and use space saving bags to do it. You know, those are the bags that you vacuum the air out of? Those ones. They are flat, they are perfect and they keep all liquids out!

## 2. Glasses Case for Phone Charge 📱

![vision care, lunch, bag, glasses, meal,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wo/yh/bpb55y95579086496d35f373270914_500x500.jpg)

OMG – how did I not think of this before? All of my cords constantly get tangled EVERYWHERE because I just throw them into my bag. How did I not think of slipping my cords and chargers in an extra glasses case?

## 3. Binder Clips for Your Headphones 🎧

![electronic component,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h9/gl/slood1at578bd417479fd454945770_600x598.jpg)

How about your headphones? Do you ever go and reach for them and suddenly, they are all tangled up and you can't figure out how to untangle them? I do that all the time (today in fact, on the plane!). All you need is a binder clip and hey presto tangle free headphones.

## 4. Lighter Color Nails 💅

![color, pink, nail, manicure, lilac,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hu/nh/vnq8lzz8578bd5acaeea3466818479_600x647.jpg)

Why the heck would this be a travel hack? Well, that's because the lighter nails don't typically chip and certainly will look fab with anything. With darker nails, you can see every chip or every single crack. Go with lighter and your nails will look beautiful on the plane!

## 5. Contact Cases for Skincare Creams 👱🏼

![hair, hairstyle, footwear, moustache, finger,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ry/pm/p4use1fm578bd7aedab5a559246058_600x600.jpg)

My last trip was to DC and I actually did this and it was great! You know how you can't take a bunch of liquids with you and to take full-size everything of skincare is a hassle? Well, just grab some contact cases and fill them with your expensive skincare products, pop them in your carry on or purse and you won't have to worry about any leaks! Genius!

## 6. Vitamins in a Bottle 💊

![hand, bead, jewellery, nail,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/ci/n9ohegps578bd7d0bcaed245777640_600x602.jpg)

Same thing with vitamins, if you take a ton of vitamins, why do you need to take every bottle with you? Instead, of taking every single bottle with you, which can be really bulky, why not just pop out how many you'll need and stuff everything into one bottle? You can stack the pills on top of each other too, which is perfect!

## 7. Crafting Boxes for Multiple Snacks🍳 🍔 🍟

![meal, dish, food, asian food, osechi,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/l8/p10ulhbp578bd83318d3b148691868_550x550.jpg)

Okay, so if you have kids? This is the perfect travel hack in the world for you! You know those crafting boxes that are all sectioned out? Well, you can use one of those for any small snacks! Grapes, small sandwiches, and all kinds of things can fit into those little cubes and be perfect on the plane or in the airport!

There are so many travel hacks out there, what are some of your favorites? Share 'em in the comments!
