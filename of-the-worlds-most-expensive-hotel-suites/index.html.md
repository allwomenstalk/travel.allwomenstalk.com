# 8 of the World's Most Expensive Hotel Suites You Need to See

One of the perks of being wealthy is having the freedom to stay in the world's most expensive hotel suites. To be honest, you don't just have to be wealthy but exceptionally rich to afford some of these incredible places. Let's not forget they are ultimately just places to rest your head, take a bath, and store your luggage when away from home. The world's most expensive hotel suites are beyond the wildest dreams for most of us, but dreaming costs nothing. If you love discovering lavish getaways, check out our guide to [luxury destinations to visit before you die](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/luxury-destinations-to-visit-before-you-die/).

## 1. The Carlton Suite, The Carlton, Moscow

![The Carlton Suite, The Carlton, Moscow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/03/1_the-ritz-carlton-suite-ritz-carlton-moscow_600x399.jpg)

Previously known as The Ritz-Carlton, [The Carlton in Moscow](https://carltonmoscow.com/) features some of the most opulent accommodations available. For around $18,000 per night, you can expect this 232-square-meter suite to be dripping in luxury. You will find heated flooring throughout the spacious apartment, complete with floor-to-ceiling windows offering a jaw-dropping view of Red Square and the Kremlin. Tickle the ivories on your majestic grand piano or take your tea in your private library.

## 2. The Presidential Suite, Regent Hong Kong

![The Presidential Suite, Regent Hong Kong](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/03/2_the-presidential-suite-intercontinental-hong-kong_600x360.jpg)

Formerly the InterContinental, the newly rebranded [Regent Hong Kong](https://hongkong.regenthotels.com/) offers a Presidential Suite that leaves mortals gasping at the bill. Enjoy a libation on your 2,500-square-foot terrace, do a leisurely lap in your infinity pool on the rooftop, or sink into your Jacuzzi while looking out over your private view of Victoria Harbour. If you can manage it after all of that, you have a private gym and luxury car service at your disposal.

## 3. Royal Suite, Burj Al Arab, Dubai

![Royal Suite, Burj Al Arab, Dubai](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/03/3_royal-suite-burj-al-arab-dubai_600x254.jpg)

When it comes to extravagant hotel suites, Dubai is in a class of its own. The Royal Suite at the [Burj Al Arab](https://www.jumeirah.com/en/stay/dubai/burj-al-arab-jumeirah) has been a global icon since opening its doors in 1999. The distinctive sail shape of this luxury hotel sets the bar incredibly high for ultimate pampering. Personal butlers ensure discerning guests have everything they need in their two-story suite, which includes lavish dining spaces and top-of-the-range whirlpool baths. For more incredible spots in the region, explore these [breathtaking places to visit in the Middle East](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/breathtaking-places-to-visit-in-the-middle-east/).

## 4. The Sultan's Suite, Çırağan Palace Kempinski, Istanbul

![The Sultan's Suite, Çırağan Palace Kempinski, Istanbul](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/03/4_the-sultans-suite-ira-an-palace-kempinski-istanbul_600x338.jpg)

The epitome of world-class luxury and majesty can be found in Istanbul at this breathtaking 16th-century palace, once home to the last Ottoman Sultans. Rebuilt in marble in the 1800s, the [Çırağan Palace](https://www.kempinski.com/en/ciragan-palace) is now an exceptional five-star hotel with a star-studded guest list. With an onsite boat and helicopter service, a private butler, fine art, and vintage furniture, this remains one of the most luxurious suites available.

## 5. Royal Suite, Four Seasons Hotel George V, Paris

![Royal Suite, Four Seasons Hotel George V, Paris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/03/5_royal-suite-four-seasons-hotel-george-v-paris_600x337.jpg)

Rolling in at over $24,000 a night, guests at the Four Seasons Hotel George V are surrounded by stunning French antiques, 19th-century artwork, and crystal chandeliers. There are only two Royal suites at the hotel, both overlooking the statue of the Three Graces from private balconies. Each suite is fitted with a sauna, steam room, marble bathroom, and walk-in dressing room. There is truly nothing like seeing Paris while sleeping in serious style.

## 6. Ritz-Carlton Suite, The Ritz-Carlton, Tokyo

![Ritz-Carlton Suite, The Ritz-Carlton, Tokyo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/03/6_ritz-carlton-suite-ritz-carlton-tokyo_600x400.jpg)

A suite at The Ritz-Carlton in Tokyo has unparalleled views that sweep out over Mount Fuji, the Imperial Palace, and the Roppongi Hills. Located higher than almost any skyscraper in Tokyo, these beautifully appointed private suites offer guests a massive 300 square meters of space. Guests enjoy expansive marble bathrooms, refreshing rain showers, and premium Frette linens that make every night unforgettable.

## 7. The Royal Villa, Grand Resort Lagonissi, Athens

![The Royal Villa, Grand Resort Lagonissi, Athens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/03/7_the-royal-villa-grand-resort-lagonissi-athens_600x398.jpg)

Overlooking a private beach with white sands and a mesmerizing turquoise bay, your private marina awaits at the Grand Resort Lagonissi in Athens. With a private concert pianist playing in the background, a chef whipping up your favorite meals, and a butler handling your calls, it is a wonder you ever have to lift a finger. This resort redefines the very concept of a relaxing Mediterranean holiday.

## 8. Royal Penthouse Suite, Hotel President Wilson, Geneva

![Royal Penthouse Suite, Hotel President Wilson, Geneva](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/03/8_royal-penthouse-suite-hotel-president-wilson-geneva_600x375.jpg)

When it comes to the most expensive hotel suites anywhere, the Royal Penthouse Suite at the Hotel President Wilson in Geneva takes the prize. At roughly $65,000 per night, it is an excellent choice for security-conscious individuals seeking absolute privacy. With bulletproof windows and doors, the 12-room suite takes up the entire top floor of the hotel. There is plenty of space for a private fitness center, library, grand piano, and billiard room, while your private elevator brings you up to panoramic views of Mont Blanc and Lake Geneva.

If you have enough money to travel without a budget, you can easily afford to stay in these luxurious suites and feel like royalty.
