# 7 Most Exotic Destinations for Your Next Vacation ...

We live on such a beautiful planet that offers a lot of stunning landscapes and tropical paradises.Want to take a break from work and go on an amazing vacation that will help you recharge your batteries? There are so many wonderful places that you can visit: beautiful forests and mountains; mist shrouded volcanoes; and unbelievable wild beaches. Just go somewhere far away and exotic and satisfy your wanderlust. Here are 7 most exotic destinations for your next vacation for you to consider:

## 1. The Cook Islands

![The Cook Islands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hm/ux/y8lkj9kg_600x400.jpg)

This is definitely one of the most exotic destinations for your next vacation. The Cook Islands is an island country situated in the South Pacific Ocean. It comprises 15 beautiful islands scattered over a vast expense of seductive ocean waters. It boasts an idyllic climate and is home of a warm and welcoming people. The blue and calm lagoons will mesmerize you, so make these beautiful islands the destination for your next vacation.

## 2. Gozo, Malta

![Gozo, Malta](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y1/mb/fzz9wa3k_600x371.jpg)

Gozo is a beautiful island of the Maltese archipelago in the Mediterranean Sea and it’s well-known for its scenic hills. This island can offer you all the activities that will make your trip perfect. It has a lot of amazing panoramas, many forts, waterfalls, luxurious hotels and beaches that will make your visit a very fun experience.

## 3. Cano Cristales, Colombia

![Cano Cristales, Colombia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xe/zc/jvhg6zzz_600x399.jpg)

Located in Colombia, this river is commonly called “The River of Five Colors” or “The Liquid Rainbow” and it’s even considered to be the most beautiful river in the world due to its striking colors. So, if you are heading to South America, then this Colombian wonder is a must-see. Also, the best time to travel is July – November, because during those months the river bed is full of gaudy colors like yellow, green, red and blue.

## 4. Curaçao

![Curaçao](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vp/gw/jl5p88de_600x400.jpg)

This Caribbean island is a dream destination. Lying not far off the coast of Venezuela, it is known for is gorgeous coral reefs, so it's no surprise the diving here is superb. The food is fabulous, the weather is wonderful and the beaches bewitching.

## 5. Fiji

![Fiji](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vo/hh/e0jbvv8p_600x400.jpg)

Your list of exotic destinations can’t be complete without mentioning Fiji, which is a beautiful archipelago of over 300 islands in the South Pacific Ocean. You’ll not only enjoy pristine white sandy beaches, a visit the beautiful forests or exploring the fish-packed coral reefs but you’ll also have the opportunity to climb a mountain, to soak in a hot spring or even explore a native village.

## 6. South Island, New Zealand

![South Island, New Zealand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t1/k6/pgz7gewe_600x420.jpg)

This is New Zealand’s larger island but it has less than half of the country’s population, so if you want to go to somewhere exotic to relax and recharge your batteries, then this is the place for you. You can visit beautiful mountains, volcanoes and pristine coastlines. You can go skiing, scuba diving, hiking or you could even visit their relaxing spas or luxurious hotels if you need some pampering.

## 7. Tahiti

_ Tahiti is the largest island in the windward group of French Polynesia and is located in the archipelago of the Society Islands in the Central Southern Pacific Ocean. It’s famous for its emerald waters, white-sand beaches and its overwater bungalows. You could take a tour of the small villages, try a banana-tree-leaf body wrap or even go on a shark-feeding expedition.

There are a lot of exotic destinations that you can choose to spend your next vacation. I just mentioned a few in this little article but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. Do you know any other exotic destinations that would make your next vacation a dream come true? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
