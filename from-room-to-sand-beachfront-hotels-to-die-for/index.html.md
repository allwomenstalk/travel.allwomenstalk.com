# From Room to Sand: 7 Beachfront Hotels to Die for ...

One of the most magical aspects of a beach holiday is being able to step straight from your room or hotel onto the sand and be in the ocean in a few steps. Here are some glorious beachfront hotels where that holiday dream comes true:

## 1. Turtle Nest Inn, Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands

![Turtle Nest Inn, Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sl/mz/n55ozbrz554e90f530a8b874516363_600x400.jpg)

Sun-baked, red roofed hotel buildings overlook a dazzling white sandy beach that's mere steps from the hotel's doors. Lush, exotic vegetation and palm trees sway gently in the sea breeze. The sounds and smells of the lunchtime throng in the restaurant may rouse you but don't count on it: this is such a relaxing paradise, you may slip into a dream-like state and never fully wake up again until the end of your holiday. The Turtle Nest Inn on Grand Cayman is one of those beachfront hotels where you can practically fall out of your bed and take a dip in the sea without having to walk more than a few steps.

## 2. Outrigger Reef on the Beach, Honolulu, Hawaii

![Outrigger Reef on the Beach, Honolulu, Hawaii](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yf/rs/jdc81luk554e9107030c8916127676_600x404.jpg)

Although not exactly a high-rise, the Outrigger Reef on the Beach is not exactly a romantic getaway either. But with close proximity to the sea, shopping and dining areas and splendid views over Waikiki Beach, who could possibly grumble at the lack of architectural beauty of this beachfront hotel?

## 3. Galley Bay Resort, St. John's, Antigua

![Galley Bay Resort, St. John's, Antigua](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/xf/fjgt5f4l554e911e16443935064765_600x300.jpg)

Stay in individual bungalows or the main hotel resort building, but wherever you'll hang your straw hat in this Caribbean peach of a resort, you'll never be far away from the sea and beach. Just open your back door, take a deep breath and hurl yourself into the nearest wave. Breathtaking views of verdant hills to the front and the eternal sea to the rear await you. Go hiking, swimming, surfing, diving or simply snooze in a deckchair under a palm tree.

## 4. Blue Lagoon Beach Resort, Yasawa Islands, Fiji

![Blue Lagoon Beach Resort, Yasawa Islands, Fiji](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ng/b9/cj2atfoe554e912de4875116068366_600x399.jpg)

The Blue Lagoon Beach resort is one of those beachfront hotels where you're just a few steps away from the main beach and don't have to worry about wearing shoes for the entire stay. Sit back in your well-padded lounge chair, sip as many cocktails as your diet will permit and enjoy the mountain views!

## 5. Outrigger Phi Phi Island Resort & Spa, Ko Phi Phi Don, Thailand

![Outrigger Phi Phi Island Resort & Spa, Ko Phi Phi Don, Thailand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zv/eb/szeoohg7554e917c26231212620664_600x400.jpg)

This amazing bungalow hotel overlooks a pristine, white sandy beach that makes you believe you're the only human being left on our Blue Planet, it's that tranquil and idyllic. Fishing boats bob gently on the waves as they have done since the dawn of humanity, palm trees provide launch-pads for small children intent on diving into the sea just as you've imagined it from the blazing blue pictures in the brochure. Endless dazzling skies greet you across the breakfast table. Embrace the life of a superstar!

## 6. Cape Panwa Hotel, Phuket, Thailand

![Cape Panwa Hotel, Phuket, Thailand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/te/ip14flm5554e919881fc8638273306_600x427.jpg)

Located in one of the world's most popular party capitals, the Cape Panwa Hotel in Phuket overlooks a tranquil, white sandy beach lined by coconut palm trees, surfboards and small fishing boats. It's an idyllic setting ideal for honeymooning couples, especially those looking for beachfront hotels offering more than the most romantic rooms money can buy - this hotel boasts some of the finest views in Phuket.

## 7. Hamanasi Adventure & Dive Resort, Hopkins, Belize

The holiday bungalows in the Hamanasi Adventure & Dive Resort are open to the elements, allowing a refreshing sea breeze to circulate the warm air in your room. Situated just a few hundred feet from the beach and waterfront, these bungalows afford spectacular views of sunsets and sunrises. It's a fabulous place to come for relaxation, amazing diving and swimming and, of course, holiday adventure of a romantic nature.
