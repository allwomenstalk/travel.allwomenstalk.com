# 9 Beautiful Travel Bags to Make ...

Travel Bags can be very pricey. This is why we suggest that you take a look at these **DIY travel bags**. If you can sew or you know someone who can, check out these **travel bag ideas and patterns**. The projects we have here cover large weekender bags and simple beach totes. I'm sure one of these **travel bags** will become your constant wanderlust-ing companions.

## 1. Pumpkin Patch Weekender

![Pumpkin Patch Weekender](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/1_480x328.jpg)

If you like travel bags that are girly and offer easy access to your things, this is a wonderful tutorial by the ladies over at Kojo Designs. I like this bag because you can sling it across your body, too. Nothing like free hands to peruse flea market and souvenir stalls.

[Tutorial](http://kojo-designs.com/2010/10/kojotutorial-pumpkin-patch-weekender/)

## 2. The Weekender

![The Weekender](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/2_432x336.jpg)

How sexy is this bag? I will probably fit inside this one. Which, of course, makes it a must-sew bag. Don over at Weekend Designer gives us comprehensive instructions on how to make this weekender.

[Tutorial](https://wkdesigner.wordpress.com/2009/01/17/the-weekender/)

## 3. Large Patchwork Bag

![Large Patchwork Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/3_540x470.jpg)

I have just recently discovered the joys of patchwork sewing (15 years after learning to sew *aherm*) and I think this bag will be fun to make. If you happen to have a large collection of fabrics, this is wonderful project for you. I bet this will be lovely in bright summery colors.

[Tutorial](https://www.allpeoplequilt.com/bags-pillows-gifts/bags-and-purses/large-patchwork-bag)

## 4. Cinched Tote

![Cinched Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/4_561x401.jpg)

This** DIY travel tote** is a shoulder bag style. This one is roomy and will definitely be useful on any trip. Even a quick trip to the supermarket.

[Tutorial](http://www.thisbigoaktree.com/2012/02/large-cinched-tote-tutorial.html)

## 7. Canvas Beach Bag

![Canvas Beach Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/04/7a_800x533.jpg)

Boy, I am loving this nautical bag. Looks store-bought, doesn't it? According to Leanne: "...this bag is extra big.
