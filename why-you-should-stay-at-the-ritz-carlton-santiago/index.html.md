# Why You Should Stay at the Ritz Carlton Santiago ...

Wondering **why you should stay at the Ritz Carlton Santiago**? Offering 5-star luxury in the posh Las Condes district, this hotel is located near Santiago’s beaches and ski resorts. Here, the wide range of Chile’s offerings is reflected in details such as a perfectly mixed Pisco sour to a spectacular view of the lush Andes Mountains. The hotel invites guests to experience the following features, which will give you all the reasons **why you should stay at the Ritz Carlton Santiago**.

## 1. Dining

The Ritz Carlton Santiago has four dining venues including healthy farm-to-table dishes at Estro and hand-roasted coffee at Café Ritual. And that's just one of the reasons **why you should stay at the Ritz Carlton Santiago**.

## 2. Spa

The glass-roofed spa and wellness center is an ideal place to get pampered. The amenities and services include an indoor lap pool, a fitness center as well as facial and body treatments.

## 3. Kids Program

The Ritz Kids program is for guests aged 4-12. It offers daily activities, special dining menus and arts and crafts.

## 4. Suites

Choose from luxurious junior, executive or presidential suites. All of them are modern and spacious and have city views. And suite guests have access to the Club Lounge.

## 5. Meeting Rooms

The state-of-the-art meeting rooms are ideal for events and business gatherings. There is space for intimate groups as well as groups as large as 350 people. And catering is available.

## 6. Package Deals

Santiago hotel packages are available. The offers showcase the highlights of the city and the hotel. The Additional Overnight Package includes overnight accommodations, cheese and wine as a welcome amenity upon arrival and a $50 dinner credit at Estro.

For more information about this amazing hotel visit: [ritzcarlton.com](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/santiago/dining#THE%20RITZ-CARLTON%20BAR)
