# 7 Romantic Fall Getaways to Take as a Couple ...

If you want to spend quality time with your man on vacation, you need to pick the right spot to visit. [Redbook Mag](https://www.redbookmag.com/love-sex/relationships/g2916/romantic-fall-weekend-getaways/?slide=8) has some romantic ideas for you, and here they are:

## 1. Cooperstown, New York

![Cooperstown, New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gp/js/m1rrovgs56322159103fa078309141_480x720.jpg)

This is where the National Baseball Hall of Fame is located. Of course, there are also plenty of orchards and breweries.

## 2. Ivans, Utah

![Ivans, Utah](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sy/le/j6qqld8q5632215981966917670843_480x721.jpg)

If you two love to look at beautiful scenery, there are two state parks that you can visit.

## 3. Jackson Hole, Wyoming

![Jackson Hole, Wyoming](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jj/eg/g28bo6bb5632215972fec892930961_480x720.jpg)

If you want to stay at a private ranch that'll make you two feel like the only ones in the world, this is the place to go.

## 4. Natchez, Mississippi

![Natchez, Mississippi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/pl/af53o9zc56322159f1d0c729155823_480x721.jpg)

If you want to stay at a cozy bed and breakfast, you need to visit this town. Then you can take a carriage tour or a rum tour.

## 5. Bristol, Rhode Island

![Bristol, Rhode Island](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bc/po/h5k12lob56322159db1ec325510587_480x710.jpg)

This is a coastal town, which means you'll get a gorgeous view of the water. Plus, it's close to Providence and Boston.

## 6. White Mountains, New Hampshire

![White Mountains, New Hampshire](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p9/wn/phgvj14u5632215a84215895510306_480x720.jpg)

Here, you can explore the White Mountain Trail, or even go zip lining.

## 7. Aruba

![Aruba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iz/ah/ie9lrlye5632215a6eac1961408550_480x720.jpg)

There's no better time for a visit to Aruba. Go now, because the prices will be much higher in winter.
