# You'll Want to Visit These Amazing Ice Hotels ...

It doesn't matter if you get cold easily, because there are some ice hotels that are surprisingly warm. Even though the furnitures, walls, and ceilings are all made out of ice, you won't feel uncomfortable. I mean, how could you complain when you're looking at such beautiful scenery?

If being surrounded by ice doesn't appeal to you, there are snow igloos that you can stay inside of that give you beautiful views of the Northern Lights.

If you're interested in the idea, check out this video by [Ask Men](https://www.askmen.com:443/)

Ice hotels are beyond beautiful! Would you ever stay a night at an ice hotel?
