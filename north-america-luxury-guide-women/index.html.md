# The Discerning Woman’s Guide to Luxury in North America

Traveling as a woman on your own or even as part of a groupcan sometimes be a little bit of a challenge. Not knowing which destinations and experiences are going to give you the **girl’s trip experience** you’re looking for, not knowing which places are the most secure for women traveling together or alone, and not being sure which hotels or Airbnbs are going to offer all night room service for those late night snack moments can really take the fun out of planning.

Your best chance for getting the most out of your **North American luxury trip** is to have options, and that's just what we have to share with you. These are luxury hotspots across North America and our guide for female travelers looking for an exciting escape.

## 1. New York City

![](https://img.allw.mn/392d136e130ed0694fca96e23c305e_1365x2048.jpg)

The Big Apple is one of the best-known destinations on the planet and a must-see for any woman looking to be blown away by theater performances, shopping excursions, and dining fit for a queen. Luxury in New York begins with your arrival: your party will [take a plane charter](https://www.aircharterserviceusa.com/private-charter/private-jets-charter), of course, for the highest level of comfort and service available in the air. Once you land, you’ll have a town car waiting to whisk you away to your accommodation.

📌 **Stays**

Choosing a luxury hotel in New York is like making Sophie’s choice because there are so many high-class establishments available to travelers who like the finer things in life. Selecting one that is close to many of the places you would like to see is the best way to ensure that you spend as little of the trip getting from place to place as possible. The Ritz-Carlton is an institution in New York. Classic elegance, unparalleled Central Park views, and sumptuous dining right in the heart of the world’s favorite city.

📌 **Nightlife**

Sip your cocktails in style at the King Cole Bar at the St Regis - an emblem of style and class for the New York elite. Take in an award-winning show on the great white way with a visit to Broadway. Dance into the wee hours with your girlfriends by your side at Marquee: a blend of industrial and bohemian design.

📌 **Dining**

Le Bernardin, a Michelin reviewer’s dream, waits to welcome you with its European and East Asian Fare. Per Se offers you a fireside seat and a view of the park while you nibble on French-inspired tasting menus.

📌 **Experiences**

Take a carriage ride around Central Park like the queens you are. If that's not for you, there is a plethora of luxury shopping on your doorstep: Bergdorf Goodman, Rockefeller Centre, Bloomingdale’s, and more. If you’re visiting in the winter, you’ll want to experience the magic of ice skating in the park or at Rockefeller Center.

## 2. Miami

![](https://img.allw.mn/77d3e5d240ac5736f28ad30386f153_2048x1366.jpg)

If you and your girlfriends love non-stop excitement and live for a good party, Miami is the place for you. The opulence of life in this bright and colorful city cannot be understated. The blending of cultures, the exuberance of the weather, and the luxury everywhere you look makes Miami an unmissable experience.

📌 **Nightlife**

LIV can be found in the Fontainebleau Hotel: an icon in itself. This nightclub is the jewel in the city’s crown, renowned for its incredible array of DJs and the glamorous crowd that frequents it. E11EVEN is more than just a nightclub: this sprawling complex features an outdoor patio for hot nights (every night is a hot night in Miami), a dance club, a restaurant, and even a bowling alley available for 24/7 parties.

📌 **Stays**

The Setai is located in Miami's most desirable area: South Beach. A blend of traditional Asian hospitality and Miami’s vibrant culture creates a stay you’ll never want to leave. This artfully designed hotel has a lot to offer aside from its exquisite Art Deco exterior: three pools for soaking up the sun, delicious cuisine on demand, and the most wonderfully appointed rooms.

📌 **Experiences**

What better to do in Miami than take advantage of the gorgeous coastline? [Chartering a yacht](https://www.tripadvisor.co.za/AttractionProductReview-g34438-d22679480-Miami_Private_Yacht_Rental_with_Captain-Miami_Florida.html) (or speedboat and jet skis, if you’re a little more adventurous) is no challenge in a city that everyone comes to for beach life. Choose a day trip or longer and use the time on the sparkling blue water to work on your tan, have girl’s night parties abort, or just stare at the scenery.

## 3. Los Angeles

![](https://img.allw.mn/b0e9f70dc605ad10477b86298b63ed_2048x1365.jpg)

LA is known as the [land where stars are made](https://www.getyourguide.com/los-angeles-l179/luxury-experiences-tc287/). This city is positively vibrating with life, excitement, and experiences waiting to be had. Walk the streets where countless movies have been shot, dance in the clubs where the legends have, immerse yourself in gorgeous nature, and eat food cooked by some of the best chefs in the world.

📌 **Nightlife**

Hyde Sunset offers a high-end dinner menu from 6-10 and then transforms into one of the hottest nightclubs in the city. This intimate nightclub is an incredible hip-hop venue and very exclusive, so dress to impress! MainRo is a megaclub to beat all megaclubs- the ultimate in after-dark dancing, eating, and entertainment for over 21s.

📌 **Dining**

Petit Grain Boulangerie offers you buttery, glistening pastries and sweet treats during the daytime - perfect for a breakfast or brunch excursion. Osteria Vera’s open kitchen provides a warm and welcoming atmosphere, and its revamped osteria classics (like cacio e pepe) are incredibly refined yet still incredibly comforting. It’s the perfect place for a glass of wine with friends.

📌 **Stays**

The Santa Monica Proper Hotel is a bastion of cool and calm in a very busy part of the city. The tone matching throughout the building brings nature indoors and relaxes guests. Plus, its close proximity to the beachfront and pier makes it a desirable location for visitors from other cities. The Maybourne Beverly Hills offers a vastly different and incredibly classy experience. Live in the kind of luxury that only Real Housewives stars get to experience in one of the city’s most affluent areas.

📌 **Experiences**

Naturally, no trip to LA would be complete without a walk-up visit to the Hollywood sign! Lace up your sneakers and get into some activewear because it's not a jaunt to the corner store. Take yourselves shopping on Melrose for girls’ trip souvenirs, or spend the day on a VIP tour of Universal Studios.

## 4. Ladies, This Is Luxury

North America has an exciting array of cities perfect for hosting a girl's trip or a solo vacation for a female traveler. Each city has art, culture, natural beauty, and more to share. Ready for your next adventure? These destinations are ready for you.
