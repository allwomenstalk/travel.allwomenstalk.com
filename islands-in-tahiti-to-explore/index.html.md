# 7 Islands in Tahiti to Explore ...

If you are dreaming of turquoise oceans, palm trees, and beaches, visiting the islands in Tahiti will make your dreams come true. The Tahitian islands are located in the South Pacific Ocean, and are part of French Polynesia, an overseas territory of France. These islands are just an eight hour plane ride from Los Angeles, but once you get there you will feel like you are in another world. There are 118 islands in Tahiti, but you only have to explore one of them for your tropical vacation.

## 1. Tahiti

![Tahiti](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/pu/kijty7an_600x450.jpg)

Tahiti is the main Tahitian island. It is the largest and it is very beautiful with mountains and large waterfalls. If you go to Tahiti, you can stay at one of the resorts and dine at fantastic restaurants. You can also shop at the outdoor market, Le Marché, which has local crafts, vanilla, flowers, and fruit. Of course, you can always enjoy the ocean by hanging out at the beach or snorkeling in one of the lagoons. As fabulous as this big island is, it is only one of the many islands in Tahiti you can explore.

## 2. Moorea

![Moorea](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zs/id/a0owra5a_600x450.jpg)

Moorea is also known as the Magic Island, which may be explained by its magical appearance. It has tall green mountains that jut out from the island. Moorea is home to many pineapple plantations, and if you go there, you won’t want to miss out on trying their fantastic pineapple, which is incredibly sweet. You also won’t want to miss out on a chance to swim with the dolphins, which you can do at the Moorea Dolphin Center.

## 3. Huahine

![Huahine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m5/gy/xhr160j5_600x400.jpg)

Huahine is more rugged and remote than some of the other Tahitian islands. It has 8 small villages, and life moves at a slower pace. If you are into history or archeology, Huahine is a wonderful place to travel to, because it has pre-European temples and ancient royal sites that you can explore on archeological tours.

## 4. Tikehau

![Tikehau](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ud/t9/o4i3fa51_600x450.jpg)

Tikehau is a beautiful atoll that has pink sand beaches. Not only will you love lounging on the pink beaches, but if you love fishing, you will be in heaven. The lagoon is abundant with fish, which makes catching one almost guaranteed. If fishing isn’t your thing, you can also snorkel, dive, or jet sky.

## 5. Fakarava

![Fakarava](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/dg/ksr88arq_600x400.jpg)

Fakarava is a relatively untouched island that has rare birds, plants, and crustaceans. The creatures and plants of this island are so rare that the island is a designated UNESCO Biosphere Reserve. Visiting this small island will give you the opportunity to explore a rare and diverse ecosystem. Of course, you will also have plenty of opportunities to lay on the beach and enjoy the beauty.

## 6. Manihi

![Manihi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jg/ip/akx5i0wz_600x399.jpg)

Manihi has crystal clear lagoons that were once filled with mother of pearl. It is also the site of the first black pearl farm in Tahiti. Manihi is still the leading supplier of Tahitian cultured pearls, and it even has a museum devoted to pearls. Aside from looking at all the beautiful pearls, you can also walk along the coral paths and view the colorful homes of the 769 inhabitants.

## 7. Bora Bora

![Bora Bora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/sj/b2b7hk9z_600x450.jpg)

Bora Bora is known as the Romantic Island, and for good reason. It has white sand beaches, fantastic resorts, and wonderful restaurants. While you are there you can feed stingrays or go snorkeling and see tropical fish in the coral reefs. You can also take sunset cruises.

Going to any of these Tahitian islands would be a tropical dream come true. They are all beautiful. Each one has something special to offer, and whatever one you travel to, you will feel like you are in paradise. Have you have been to any of the Tahitian islands?
