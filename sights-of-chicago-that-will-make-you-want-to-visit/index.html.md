# 40 Sights of Chicago That Will Make You Want to Visit ...

It's one of the great cities of the Midwest and while some of the sights of Chicago are well known to many, others are also worthy of attention. Grab your favorite beverage and settle in for a photo album of some of the sights of Chicago.

## 1. Cloud Gate

![Cloud Gate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ir/i7/flu9qypi_600x600.jpg)

Via [Chicago – a locals guide](https://goo.gl/v7qy0O)

Known as "The Bean," Cloud Gate has become one of the new iconic sights of Chicago. It's the work of British artist Anish Kapoor.

## 2. Navy Pier

![Navy Pier](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dq/sm/qsywva81_426x640.jpg)

Via [AngieMcMonigalPhotography-7490](https://www.flickr.com/photos/angiemcmonigalphotography/8323606954/in/photostream)

Where do you go for family attractions, entertainment, shopping, dining, cruises and events? Why, Navy Pier, of course.

## 3. The Magnificent Mile

![The Magnificent Mile](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/iv/afetgx5v_337x450.jpg)

Via [Cruising the Magnificent Mile](https://www.flickr.com/photos/cjsmithphotography/6692668889/in/set-72157626112430001)

The term "The Magnificent Mile" was coined in the 1940s. This shopping mecca is a prestigious section of Michigan Avenue, running from the Chicago River to Oak Street.

## 4. Chicago Lakefront

_ Via Chicago Lakefront_

## 11. Buckingham Fountain

![Buckingham Fountain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/f6/ohkg1j1h_420x600.jpg)

Via [Buckingham Fountain flows against a ...](https://hankphoto.photoshelter.com/image/I00000UY9c66crzs)

## 16. Sears Tower

![Sears Tower](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/gd/dtx4558h_570x891.jpg)
