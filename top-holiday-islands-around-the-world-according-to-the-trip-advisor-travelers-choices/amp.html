Top Holiday Islands around the World According to the 2015 Trip Advisor Travelers' Choices ...

Nosy Be, Madagascar • Main Island, Mauritius • Boa Vista, Cabo Verde • Praslin Island, Seychelles • Nosy Borahah (aka Île Sainte-Marie), Madagascar • More ...

By Neecey

Undecided about where to take your next vacation? Looking for a tropical paradise? You’ll find plenty in Africa, Asia and the South Pacific. Get some help in choosing from fellow holidaymakers who voted for their top islands in the Trip Advisor Traveler’s Choice Awards for 2015. Let’s kick off with …

AFRICA

1. Nosy Be, Madagascar

2. Main Island, Mauritius

3. Boa Vista, Cabo Verde

4. Praslin Island, Seychelles

5. Nosy Borahah (aka Île Sainte-Marie), Madagascar

6. Unguja, Zanzibar, Tanzania

7. La Digue, Seychelles

8. Ilho do Sal, Cabo Verde

9. Mahé Island, Seychelles

10. Rodrigues Island, Mauritius

ASIA

1. Ko Tao, Thailand

2. Bali, Indonesia

3. Ko Phangan, Thailand

4. Ko Lanta, Thailand

5. Gili Trawagan, Gili Islands, Indonesia

6. Boracay, the Philippines

7. Phuket, Thailand

8. Palawan Island, the Philippines

9. Ko Samui, Thailand

10. Langkawi, Malaysia

SOUTH PACIFIC

1. Bora Bora, Society Islands

2. Aitutaki, Cook Islands

3. Moorea, Society Islands

4. Efate, Vanuatu

5. Rarotonga, Cook Islands

6. Waiheke, New Zealand

7. Norfolk Island, Australia

8. Taveuni Island, Fiji

9. Kangaroo Island, Australia

10. Espiritu Santo, Vanuatu

Which island would have received your vote? I’d have picked any of the Whitsunday Islands in Australia.
