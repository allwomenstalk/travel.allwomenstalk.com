# 7 Places to See the Sunrise as the Start to a Perfect Day ...

When I travel, one of the things I consider is whether my destination will have places to see the sunrise. I’m easy to please really and have always found joy in the simple things in life, and the beauty of our world never fails to stir my soul. Among my favorite places to see the sunrise are beaches and mountains, but I’ll quite happily watch it from my hotel balcony too. Here are some of the best places to see the sunrise around the world.

## 1. Haleakala National Park, Hawaii, USA

![Haleakala National Park, Hawaii, USA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/08/1_haleakala-national-park-hawaii-usa_600x450.jpg)

To get the most out of this amazing sunrise, it is important to be in place in plenty of time. There are 2 locations that give outstanding views and it is advisable to reach the peak and be protected from the cold. Wrap up well and you will be able to concentrate on watching the sun come up over the clouds. Of all the places to see the sunrise this should be high on the list if not at the top.

## 2. Angkor Wat, Siem Reap, Cambodia

![Angkor Wat, Siem Reap, Cambodia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/08/2_angkor-wat-siem-reap-cambodia_600x400.jpg)

While it is obvious that there are many reasons to visit Angkor Wat, the stunning sunrise is one that will not be forgotten. In order to discover the history of Hinduism and Khmer, you need to spend a few days in the area, and the sunrise will always be stunning however many times you see it. Angkor Wat is a perfect combination of the magic of nature and the splendor of the past, and one of the outstanding locations to view the sunrise.

## 3. Fiji

![Fiji](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/08/3_fiji_600x399.jpg)

If you want to spend your time resting on a wonderful white beach and then swimming in a calm turquoise sea, Fiji is the place to go. Each day, this is one of the first places that the sun rises and there can be few things better than watching this marvelous event. Around 5.30 am the sky bursts into a mass of purple and orange and clouds frame the sun as it rises. Following this up with scuba diving will be a perfect day as it is one of the best places to watch the sunrise.

## 4. Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania

![Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/08/4_mount-kilimanjaro-tanzania_600x450.jpg)

Once you have travelled up to the top of Mount Kilimanjaro you will be 19,341 feet above sea level and at the highest peak in Africa. As well as the normal view across Tanzania, there is also a beautiful sunrise. On first sight is appears that the sky is on fire, such is the intense red color, and this is one of the most outstanding places to watch the sun rise.

## 5. Stonehenge, England

![Stonehenge, England](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/08/5_stonehenge-england_600x400.jpg)

Mystery has always surrounded Stonehenge in England, and there are still questions asked regarding how and why it was built. It is a meeting place for worship and there are strong beliefs that its existence owes a great deal to the sun. Watching the sun come up here is almost a ritual and tours are arranged. The summer solstice sunrise starts around 4 am and it has been known for 20,000 to be in the area to see it.

## 6. Tulum, Mexico

![Tulum, Mexico](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/08/6_tulum-mexico_600x400.jpg)

Set on the coast of Mexico, Tulum is visited by spiritual beings and travelers who are fascinated by the 3 main things it has to offer. This is a combination of history, scenery and culture. Standing on the shore is the place to be each morning so as you can get the full benefit as this is one of the best places to view the sunrise. Afterwards you can visit the ruins of the walled Mayan city and look out onto the Caribbean.

## 7. Grand Canyon, Arizona, USA

![Grand Canyon, Arizona, USA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/08/7_grand-canyon-arizona-usa_600x402.jpg)

If you have ever seen the sunrise above the Grand Canyon it is clear why it is considered to be the most outstanding sunrise in the country. The colors are stunning as orange and bronze join the purple spectacle that is the sunrise. In order to see the best of the best, it is recommended you go to Maricopa Point then turn your attention to the Colorado River as it reflects the hues of the rocks as they are touched by the morning rays..

All of these are glorious places to see the sunrise but, if you wake early enough, just throw open your drapes and view your very own natural spectacle. There’s no place like home and if you are welcomed to the day with a glorious sunrise, somehow all seems better with the world. When was the last time you sat and watched the morning glory?
