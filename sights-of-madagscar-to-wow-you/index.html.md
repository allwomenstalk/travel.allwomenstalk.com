# 41 Sights of Madagscar to Wow You ...

The sights of Madagascar are among the most unique in the world. The large island off the southeast coast of Africa has some of the most amazing scenery and wildlife, and much of it is not found elsewhere - its separation from the African continent has resulted in isolated evolution. Ready to be wowed? Come with me on a whirlwind tour of the sights of Madagascar.

## 36. Analavory Geyser, Ampefy

![Analavory Geyser, Ampefy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d4/nq/mazyj4u6_500x748.jpg)

Via [Delightfully Manic](https://lalulutres.tumblr.com/post/57819727009/antananarivo-madagascar-it-really-looks-like)
Analavory is a cold geyser, as there is no volcanic activity underground, and is located in the middle of the island of Madagascar. The color of the geyser is caused by large amounts of iron and lime that are dissolved by the water when it gets contact with the outside.

## 40. Andavadoaka

_ Via flickr.com_
Andavadoaka is a small fishing village on the southwest coast. It lies on the edge of a shallow lagoon protected from ocean by submerged barrier coral reefs. Owing to the remoteness and isolation, these reefs are thought to possess a significantly higher abundance and diversity of marine life than other reefs in southwest Madagascar.
