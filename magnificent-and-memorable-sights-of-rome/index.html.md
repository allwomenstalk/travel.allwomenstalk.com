# 39 Magnificent and Memorable Sights of Rome ...

The sights of Rome get us thinking about emperors, gladiators, temples and ruins. But there are also magnificent sights of Rome from later centuries. The mix is truly heady. Take a browse.

## 1. Colosseum

_
Via Travel Pinspiration - 5 Famous ..._

**The Colosseum** is undeniably one of Rome’s most iconic and awe-inspiring sights. A marvel of ancient engineering and a symbol of the power of the Roman Empire, it’s no surprise that this breathtaking amphitheater appears on countless bucket lists worldwide. For travelers visiting Rome, [**Colosseum tours**](https://colosseumtoursinrome.com/) are an experience not to be missed. Walking through its grand arches and standing in the arena where gladiators once fought brings history to life in a way that photos alone never could. By taking a tour, visitors can fully immerse themselves in the **Colosseum’s rich history**, uncover fascinating stories about its construction, and learn about the daily lives of Romans who gathered here for entertainment. The **deeper insights offered on a guided tour** make it an unforgettable experience, adding so much more to your Roman adventure.

## 37. Arch of Constantine

![Arch of Constantine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/ew/qzd9u03y_473x710.jpg)

Via [dwf-5954_0002](https://www.flickr.com/photos/30207234@N07/3618480221/lightbox/)

The triumphal arch is located between the Colosseum and Palatine Hill (one of the 7 hills of Rome) and commemorates Constantine I's victory at the Battle of Milvian Bridge in 312. The arch spans the Via Triumphalis, the route taken by the emperors when they entered the city in triumph.
