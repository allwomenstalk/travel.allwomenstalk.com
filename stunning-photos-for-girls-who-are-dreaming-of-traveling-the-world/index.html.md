# Stunning Photos for Girls Who Are Dreaming of Traveling the World ...

I never get bored of looking at travel photos. They stir my wanderlust more than anything else. I've picked out a selection of pictures that showcase the diversity of our beautiful world. Enjoy!

## 4. Mirror, Mirror

![sky, reflection, nature, atmosphere, atmospheric phenomenon,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/e9/lh4plfm2583da96f0815b240372902_600x398.jpg)

## 5. Distant Peak

![habitat, mountainous landforms, plain, geographical feature, landform,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mg/pk/b3wciy5i583eed71127a4564671848_600x398.jpg)

## 6. Deep Blue

![Lake-Melissani Cave, geographical feature, landform, sea cave, cave,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v2/sa/by9fjasz583ef0fe6995a029410299_600x401.jpg)

## 7. Standing out

![Trolltunga, landform, geographical feature, cliff, geological phenomenon,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/yt/j6qxbbnd583efc7dd9f23216989874_600x342.jpg)

## 17. Moody Day
