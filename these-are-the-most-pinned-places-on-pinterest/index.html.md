# These Are the 20 Most Pinned Places on Pinterest ...

I have been an avid follower of a number of travel boards on Pinterest pretty much since they introduced Place Pins two years ago. The boards are an inspiration for destinations and just looking at fabulous travel photos. Accordingly, I was extremely interested when the list of the most pinned places on Pinterest was published. Let’s go on a whistle-stop tour of the destinations:

## 1. POSITANO, CAMPANIA, ITALY

![POSITANO, CAMPANIA, ITALY](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gu/ny/an74hc9f56dddff25e2f8430771007_600x392.jpg)

A favorite among the A-list and jetsetters, this charming little town on the Amalfi coast is as much a destination for foodies as well as beach lovers.

## 2. MUSHA CAY ISLAND, BAHAMAS

![MUSHA CAY ISLAND, BAHAMAS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/eu/qi2xleam56dde03958d35701464783_575x388.jpg)

So many lagoons and cays and atolls and tiny islands around the world but among them all, the one that features in the most pinned places on Pinterest is Musha Cay with its exquisite sand and pristine water.

## 3. HANOI, VIETNAM

![HANOI, VIETNAM](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/qm/j00sg8l656dde061e9e7a194095484_600x401.jpg)

Running on an energy as frenetic as the thousands of motorbikes that whizz around the city streets, Hanoi has ancient colonial hotels, traditional sites and a brilliant if somewhat typical South Asian culture.

## 4. HAVASU FALLS, GRAND CANYON, ARIZONA

![HAVASU FALLS, GRAND CANYON, ARIZONA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jt/tx/ktkncxgd56dde0a9d5c3c851272012_600x400.jpg)

There are just so, so many parts of the Grand Canyon to visit and photograph, but Pinterest users have put Havasu Falls in pole position.

## 5. PETROHUE FALLS, CHILE

![PETROHUE FALLS, CHILE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jo/u4/wzg0py7f56dde0d1df376877305505_600x450.jpg)

The falls cascade from Osorno Volcano in the Vincent Perez Rosales National Park.

## 6. JARLSHOF, SHETLAND, SCOTLAND

![JARLSHOF, SHETLAND, SCOTLAND](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jb/hj/jyn7zr5156dde0fa10f63335945038_600x400.jpg)

It seems pinners love ancient ruins and remote places. Shetland, along Scotland's rocky coast, is sometimes called the end of the world. The rewards are great for those who make it there.

## 7. KYOTO, JAPAN

![KYOTO, JAPAN](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n1/z0/u7i9lyed56dde185339cb022115280_600x398.jpg)

Once the Japanese capital, Kyoto can boast 1200 years of history. More spiritual, sedate and serene than Tokyo, photographers can lose themselves among beautiful shrines and stunning gardens.

## 8. PORT FAIRY, VICTORIA, AUSTRALIA

![PORT FAIRY, VICTORIA, AUSTRALIA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/zo/aj61jghh56dde21beb0bd969224746_600x400.jpg)

This fishing village, less than 200 miles west of state capital Melbourne, packs on the charm with its 19th-century cottages, stone churches and delightful inns. It seems few can resist the photogenic lighthouse.

## 9. GLACIER NATIONAL PARK, MONTANA

![GLACIER NATIONAL PARK, MONTANA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/pk/rdiyintf56dde243ce508261507224_600x398.jpg)

American beauty is on full display in this Montana visitor's spot with forests, mountains, lakes and alpine meadows.

## 10. TULUM, MEXICO

![TULUM, MEXICO](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uo/oc/t64xegj656dde26da181c658312861_600x375.jpg)

Forget about Cancun-Tulum, with its Mayan ruins, underground cenotes, eco-friendly food, and gorgeous beaches, is the Mexican destination most beloved on Pinterest.

## 11. LISBON, PORTUGAL

![LISBON, PORTUGAL](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/gb/n3rsxxc556dde2c3da95d887516463_600x375.jpg)

Portugal is often overshadowed by neighboring Spain, but its capital, with its tiled streets and buildings, has no shortage of pretty vantage points and is packed with history.

## 12. NEW ORLEANS, LOUISIANA

![NEW ORLEANS, LOUISIANA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xe/eb/jc5cfrp456dde3224c7a2742399358_600x450.jpg)

New Orleans is one of America's most popular cities for tourists, and not just for its amazing food scene and its drinking culture. The French Quarter, Latin Quarter, and Garden District all provide popular nature and architecture photo opportunities.

## 13. KJERAGBOLTEN, NORWAY

![KJERAGBOLTEN, NORWAY](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dp/bt/e1d0gf5x56dde380254e2326139349_600x397.jpg)

Surely one of the most incredible places for a selfie, you will stand on a wild glacial deposit, suspended over an abyss more than 3,000 feet deep. Scared?

## 14. MARRAKESH, MOROCCO

![MARRAKESH, MOROCCO](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/le/nprc955a56dde3c0814c4876516559_600x400.jpg)

Whether steaming in a hammam, indulging in flavorful foods or wending their way through the markets, many revel in the dream destination that is Morocco. Speaking from experience, it is all that it is cracked up to be,

## 15. CAPE TOWN, SOUTH AFRICA

![CAPE TOWN, SOUTH AFRICA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ey/ui/fh4g6jv956dde405ddb88124333148_600x420.jpg)

One of my favorite places, Cape Town is full of stunning pin-worthy scenery, from hiking trails to striking coastal views, including of course, Table Mountain. The trendy V&A Waterfront is a must-visit. .

## 16. SOMOTO CANYON, NICARAGUA

![SOMOTO CANYON, NICARAGUA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/or/j9y5eily56dde45511518967505994_600x400.jpg)

With Little Corn Island hitting the top of upcoming destinations and the natural beauty of geography like the Somoto Canyon, it seems a lot of us are falling in love with Nicaragua.

## 17. LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA

![LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a3/nn/tiyl84d156dde53659ffb638128479_600x337.jpg)

Los Angeles is so much more than Hollywood. With its beaches, hiking trails, multiethnic culinary scene, entertainment and cultural landmarks, L.A. has it all going on.

## 18. MILFORD SOUND, FIORDLAND NATIONAL PARK, NEW ZEALAND

![MILFORD SOUND, FIORDLAND NATIONAL PARK, NEW ZEALAND](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tw/jo/n62zq3lu56dde566e2f31486023315_600x389.jpg)

Carved out by glaciers, this phenomenally beautiful area on the southwestern coast of New Zealand's South Island is one of the country's most famous tourist destinations.

## 19. REYKJAVIK, ICELAND

![REYKJAVIK, ICELAND](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a6/ng/a0clzk2j56dde5939c382830188226_600x339.jpg)

Iceland is a stunningly beautiful country and visitors are often surprised by how hip and happening the capital is. It’s stylish with colorful houses, excellent restaurants, and friendly bars.

## 20. NEW YORK CITY, NEW YORK

![NEW YORK CITY, NEW YORK](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cx/n4/ajdloer556dde5cfebf1e335964042_600x400.jpg)

What’s not to love about the USA’s biggest city? There’ so many attractions, heaps of culture and let’s not forget the iconic skyline and photogenic Central Park.
