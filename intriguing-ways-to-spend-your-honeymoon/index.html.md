# 7 Breathtaking Honeymoon Destinations for Your First Great Adventure

Honeymoons are meant to be exciting and romantic. It is during this trip that you take your first grand adventure as a married couple, so choosing a destination that is fun and thrilling for both of you is essential. If you are looking for the [most romantic destinations for your honeymoon](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-most-romantic-destinations-for-your-honeymoon/), finding a place with the right blend of culture and excitement is key. To help you plan, let's explore seven intriguing ways to spend your honeymoon.

## 1. Parlez-vous Francais?

![Parlez-vous Francais?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uf/wk/mzs45sjo_600x400.jpg)

Paris is still one of the most intriguing European locales, and visiting is an excellent way to spend your honeymoon. The [Eiffel Tower](https://www.toureiffel.paris/en) remains a personal favorite. I love it so much I even have Eiffel Tower lamps. The wondrous architecture sends my steampunk loving heart aflutter. If you venture into France to visit this beautiful city, make sure you go to the [Orangerie museum](https://www.musee-orangerie.fr/en). It is an absolute must-see.

Paris offers endless romantic experiences. A few essentials for your itinerary include:

- Walking hand-in-hand along the Seine.

- Enjoying fresh pastries at a local café.

- Exploring hidden art galleries away from the crowds.

## 2. Picturesque Waterways

![Picturesque Waterways](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/oo/0fk2iff3_600x481.jpg)

In Venice, an impressive choice is to travel through the waterways in a classic gondola. Imagine securing a view of this exquisite city curled up in the arms of the one you love, as you both enjoy everything passing beside you. Do not forget to eat some of the impeccable cuisine offered near the historic Ca d'Oro. The regional Italian food is simply incredible.

## 3. Do You Want to Take My Picture?

![Do You Want to Take My Picture?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s5/tw/p5phn1q4_600x450.jpg)

In Bora Bora, you must venture down the beautiful photo lagoons. You and your partner can take a scenic boat tour and capture gorgeous photos to share with your grandchildren. Trust me, this area is breathtaking. The immaculate beaches and up-close marine life encounters here will not disappoint.

## 4. Virgin Islands

![Virgin Islands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/0z/t1odu5w1_600x400.jpg)

St. Croix is my top suggestion when it comes to the Virgin Islands. You will find pristine beaches, live entertainment, and fascinating museums, and the food is incredibly delicious. If you seek culture mixed with a tropical vibe, St. Croix is right up your alley. I promise you, it is nothing short of amazing.

Here is a quick look at some highlights you can expect on the island:

| Feature | Vibe | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Pristine Beaches | Relaxing | Morning walks |
| Live Entertainment | Energetic | Nightlife |
| Museums | Educational | Afternoon trips |

## 5. Cancun

![Cancun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/b3/gtuqtfy4_600x396.jpg)

If you adore marine life like I do, you must go to Cancun. There are large-scale aquariums, lush nature reserves, and incredible opportunities to swim with dolphins. You will learn a lot about Mexican culture and have the chance to eat authentic, mouth-watering cuisine. [Xcaret Park](https://www.xcaret.com/en/) is impressive as well, and you should definitely visit the eco-archaeological theme park if you have the time.

## 6. St. Lucia

![St. Lucia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/av/gig4jo6d_600x400.jpg)

If you love to hike, you must visit the Pitons in St. Lucia. They provide a generous workout with a rewarding view. Paradise Ridge is also an absolute must. In this locale, you can experience the stunning [Ladera Resort](https://www.ladera.com/), an estate completely dedicated to romance. You and your partner can get a soothing couple's massage or spend the entire day poolside. If you are looking for [relaxing honeymoon destinations](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/relaxing-honeymoon-destinations/) where you can enjoy ultimate privacy, this is your destination.

## 7. Antigua

![Antigua](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gx/rt/xcjf99yz_600x450.jpg)

If you are ready for the ultimate adventure, Antigua is awesome. It is a fantastic choice if you are researching [offbeat honeymoon destinations](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/offbeat-honeymoon-destinations/) that offer a bit more thrill. You will have the opportunity to swing through the trees like you are Tarzan, and he can be Jane this time. Devil's Bridge, despite its tumultuous history, is an amazing natural formation. If you love geysers and rocky shores, this is a great option for you both. You can also visit Nelson's Dockyard and sail on a beautiful yacht. The views are simply breathtaking.

Your honeymoon is meant to be the beginning of a lifetime of adventures. Choosing an idyllic location is just the start of your first big journey together. How did you, or how will you, spend your honeymoon?
