# Imagine a Train Ride Where Wellness Takes Center Stage...

Imagine being nestled within the majestic Scottish Highlands, cared for by top-notch wellness experts, while soaking in the luxury and history captured in the carriages of the Royal Scotsman. This is not just travel; it’s a **sensational voyage of relaxation and discovery**. Aligning with this opulence, [**Belmond**](https://www.instagram.com/belmond), in collaboration with **Dior Beauty**, offers an exceptional getaway tailored for those yearning for peace and rejuvenation amidst nature’s finest surroundings.

_Picture this_: At dawn, a soft mist caresses the rolling hills outside as you awaken to the gentle motion of the train. A crisp breeze invites you to step outside and breathe in serenity. Remain in awe as you engage with nature through luxurious wellness treatments right on board. Sound dreamy? Belmond is bringing this rare opportunity to life with the **Dior Wellness Journey** set to depart on June 20, 2025.

Let’s delve deeper into this extraordinary retreat and explore how it masterfully blends luxury, wellness, and the unrivaled natural beauty of Scotland.

## A Landmark Partnership

**Belmond's** decision to collaborate with **Dior Beauty** adds a layer of elegance and prestige to this memorable journey. **Dior’s global wellness experts**, Rose Ferguson and Bryony Deery, curate a program enriched with nutritional guidance and bespoke treatments designed to invigorate the soul and rejuvenate the body. The charm of the Scottish Highlands, with its peaks and valleys, forms the perfect canvas for this transformative experience.

## The Luxury of the Royal Scotsman

The Royal Scotsman itself is a **marvel of luxury** A beautifully designed train, it offers an indulgence that few travel experiences can parallel. Walking through its carriages is like stepping back into a grand era where elegance knew no bounds. Each compartment, wrapped in opulent fabrics and polished wooden details, instantly infuses a sense of calm and luxury.

![image](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/225a12ef-98ff-4f1d-b2f7-ddd38074aa25_1440x1800.jpg)

**Comfort and sophistication blend effortlessly** in each cabin, which are equipped with plush bedding and decorated in Dior’s signature style. At rounded prices that reflect the quality and uniqueness of this journey, guests find themselves in a cocoon of warmth and style.

These treatments, available at exact prices reflecting their luxury, are grounded in holistic approaches, promising a refreshed you at journey’s end.
