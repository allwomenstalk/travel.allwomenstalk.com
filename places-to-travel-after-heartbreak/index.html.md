# 8 Breathtaking Places to Travel to after Heartbreak ...

Looking for some **places to travel after heartbreak**? When you are recovering from sadness and loss you can feel detached from the world. One way to feel human again and fulfill your soul is by traveling. Going somewhere can feel refreshing. It is an escape and you can see the world from a new perspective. Where should you fly, drive, or get a boat to? Check out these inspiring **places to travel after heartbreak**.

## 1. A Place Where You Can Relax

![sea, ocean, horizon, sky, wind wave,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mp/xh/g5al8w885aa197db835c9654280346_600x600.jpg)

A relaxing location is one of the best **places to travel after heartbreak**. If you are looking for a place where you can feel calm with inspiring views, try traveling to an island beach. The island of Saint Martin in the Caribbean has warm water mixed with the bright sun. Whether you are in the sparkling water or soaking in the sand, you will always have an incredible view.

## 2. A Place Where You Can Feel Physically Moved

![reflection, nature, wilderness, mountain, mountainous landforms,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kv/v5/en22wt8j5aa1983cdcf98963638401_600x600.jpg)

Another way to combat your stress and cortisol levels is to go to a place where you can have a fulfilling exercise. Skiing is a calorie burner. Be sure to check out the slopes and sights in Aspen, Colorado. Aspen comes with highly rated reviews and challenging, yet beautiful runs.

## 3. A Place That Takes Your Breath Away

![sky, blue, sea, snow, vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/q2/xv5lkexh5aa198bca1d92888180029_600x600.jpg)

Look for a place that is unique and different. For example, the Greek islands have incredible architecture mixed with a stunning landscape.

## 4. A Place Where You Can Travel without a Schedule

![nature, wilderness, ecosystem, water, tree,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xc/tp/vm4asliu5aa19932a744a765249062_600x600.jpg)

Sometimes the best plan is to decide at the moment. Go where you can make your own path. For example, a forest may bring you closer to nature and help you feel better. The Redwood National Park in California has some of the tallest trees in the world. If you are traveling through the United States, make sure to check out the national parks.

## 5. A Place Where You Can See Something for the First Time

![nature, aurora, atmosphere, sky, phenomenon,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pk/aj/hbp27bjk5aa1997de78e8252240025_600x600.jpg)

Another place you can go is where you can see something new. You may find yourself somewhere people you know have never even been before. One incredible destination would be to see the Northern Lights. You can try heading north to Finland or Norway for the best view.

## 6. A Place Where You Can Connect

![property, floor, furniture, deck, flooring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ui/qj/jygst3mq5aa199a36312f132766877_480x480.jpg)

A retreat can be a rewarding experience for you to overcome what has you feeling upset. You may find yourself expressing your feelings. Retreats are often found in beautiful locations around the United States and the world. The goal of a retreat is to help you feel better.

## 7. A Place Where You Can Explore

![girl, wood, long hair, computer wallpaper, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v9/xg/cm1i3vzd5aa199ea8fb89367490562_600x600.jpg)

Feeling adventurous can help you feel alive. One place that is especially interesting to travel through is Antelope Canyon near Page, Arizona. Here you will find light shining down as you walk through captivating rock formations.

## 8. A Place You Have Loved

![fashion, shoe, jeans, girl, joint,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o5/p5/l8dkjhyi5aa19a11ac153557229026_600x641.jpg)

Do you always have that one place in your heart where you know traveling there was your favorite? Try taking a trip back to that place. Going to this place could bring you happiness when you are feeling down, which is important for your journey.
