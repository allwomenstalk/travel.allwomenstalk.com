# Hotels Designed by Doyens of Haute Couture: Where Fashion Meets Luxury

Fashion designers possess an incredible ability to make our jaws drop or leave us shaking our heads in awe with their stunning catwalk creations. Their mastery of fabric, silhouette, and color is nothing short of legendary. But does that keen eye for style and design extend beyond the high-fashion runway? Let’s explore what happens when these visionary fashion icons apply their boundary-pushing creativity to the world of luxury travel and boutique hotels. Whether you are looking for [the most beautiful hotels in the world](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/the-most-beautiful-hotels-in-the-world/) or a unique designer stay, these properties offer a glimpse into the minds of fashion's elite.

## 1. Hotel Du Petit Moulin, Paris, France

![Hotel Du Petit Moulin, Paris, France](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/e1/r93c8xgl556a903d31144262261531_600x329.jpg)

Located in the heart of Paris's famous Marais district, this [Christian Lacroix](https://www.hotelpetitmoulinparis.com/) decorated boutique hotel boasts 17 luxuriously appointed bedrooms and suites that pay homage to old Paris in their interior design. Offering a unique flair and charm among Europe's designer hotels, the Hotel du Petit Moulin is surrounded by art galleries, street artists, and high-fashion boutiques. Every floor tells a different story, reflecting the whimsical and theatrical nature of Lacroix’s couture work.

## 2. Palazzo Versace, Queensland, Australia

![Palazzo Versace, Queensland, Australia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/ot/g5i2wmy6556a9060a9932341410869_600x407.jpg)

Donatella Versace's signature bold designs are legendary, though they are not necessarily every traveler's cup of tea. Contrasting patterns and vibrant colors in wallpaper and bespoke fabrics decorate every luxurious room. Gold features heavily throughout the property, from the moldings to the fixtures. It may be regarded as a temple of elegant opulence, dripping with Italian flair and a hint of Renaissance architecture, but waking up to Donatella's intense creations may not be everyone's idea of a relaxing vacation. Still, there is the wonderful [Palazzo Versace restaurant](https://www.palazzoversace.com.au/restaurants) to look forward to for an evening of culinary excellence.

| Amenity | Highlight |
| :--- | :--- |
| Decor | Gold and Medusa-head accents |
| Location | Gold Coast, Australia |
| Vibe | Pure Italian Opulence |

## 3. Claridge's, London, United Kingdom

![Claridge's, London, United Kingdom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/k0/wrufrffu556a91d47a69b087301581_600x329.jpg)

Claridge's is the Art Deco jewel in the crown among designer hotels. Boasting twenty luxury rooms and suites in central London, Claridge's timeless elegance has attracted fashion designers and dignitaries for more than a century. This iconic landmark has provided a temporary home for silver screen legends like Audrey Hepburn, Cary Grant, Yul Brynner, Bing Crosby, and Katherine Hepburn during their time in Britain. The [Claridge's official site](https://www.claridges.co.uk/) showcases its stunning suites designed by the likes of Diane von Furstenberg, adding a modern twist to its historic grandeur.

## 4. Lloyd Hotel and Cultural Embassy, Amsterdam, the Netherlands

![Lloyd Hotel and Cultural Embassy, Amsterdam, the Netherlands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/zt/awjkc6qc556a91f2aea68418895595_600x400.jpg)

Just like every traveler is unique and has distinct needs, the Lloyd Hotel's rooms are all different, each one decorated by several popular Dutch designers. It doesn't matter if you're on a budget or wealthy enough to order a genuine Rembrandt with your morning meal. Rooms ranging from one to five stars are predominantly classified based on size here, ensuring there is a space for everyone. It's an art lover’s home away from home and a true [incredible place to stay in the Netherlands](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/incredible-places-to-stay-in-the-netherlands/).

## 5. Heidelberg Suites, Heidelberg, Germany

![Heidelberg Suites, Heidelberg, Germany](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n2/yh/uuertulh556a9345a16bb323413944_600x394.jpg)

Considered to be one of the world's most romantic cities, historic Heidelberg in southern Germany has attracted travelers from all four corners of the globe for centuries. The Heidelberg Suites were decorated by Florentine architect and designer Michele Bonan, who wanted to pay homage to the city's long history and German romanticism through her meticulous designs. The former mansion now boasts a neoclassical decor with contemporary gray walls, a very tempting spa, and spacious, well-appointed rooms that overlook the Neckar River. You can explore more about the property on the [Heidelberg Suites website](https://www.heidelbergsuites.com/).

## 6. G & V Royal Mile Hotel, Edinburgh, Scotland

![G & V Royal Mile Hotel, Edinburgh, Scotland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/dn/qqazq771556a935d46c74691894231_600x379.jpg)

Thankfully, there's little evidence of traditional tartan at the G&V Royal Mile Hotel, though the staff do wear plaid, so be prepared for a stylish nod to tradition. There may even be bagpipes heard in the distance later! This designer hotel was decorated by the Italian fashion house [Missoni](https://www.missoni.com/), with each room being as unique as Scotland's many types of wild heather. Bold, colorful patterns and iconic zig-zags make every guest room a visual feast for the fashion-forward traveler.

- Location: Edinburgh’s Old Town

- Designer: Missoni

- Highlights: Vibrant colors and Scottish charm

## 7. Armani Hotel, Dubai, United Arab Emirates

![Armani Hotel, Dubai, United Arab Emirates](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/uo/hxaisoyj556a93e5ee3da341791014_600x338.jpg)

No list of designer hotels would be complete without a mention of Armani. Dubai is home to the tallest building in the world, the Burj Khalifa, and naturally, its dimly lit hallways and spacious guest rooms should ooze luxury. Every corner reflects Giorgio Armani's personal style, featuring dark wood, Italian leather, and opulent textiles. Calming hues of mushroom, cream, and pearl gray make each room a haven of tranquility in this bustling metropolis. You can eat your way around the globe at the hotel's renowned restaurants, which serve a curated selection of Indian, Italian, and Japanese dishes. Don't forget to treat yourself to a pre-dinner cocktail or a nightcap from the world's finest selection of wines and spirits in this larger-than-life hotel.
