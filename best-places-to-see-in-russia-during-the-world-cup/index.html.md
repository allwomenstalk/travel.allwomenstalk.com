# 10 Best Places to See in Russia during the World Cup ...

The FIFA World Cup 2018 Russia has started. Are you planning to visit Russia and be a part of major football Tournament? If yes, and you are thinking about what **places to visit in Russia during the World Cup**, here are the best.

## 1. KREMLIN

Moscow is the capital of Russia and Kremlin is the heart of Moscow. You can see sky-high buildings, shopping centres, cafes, restaurants all at the same time.

## 2. HERMITAGE MUSEUM

It is situated in Saint Petersburg, the second largest city in Russia. Saint Petersburg is also called the city of art and the city of bridges. It's a great museum with hundreds of rooms. If you want to enter you should cut your nails. It is forbidden to enter with long nails. It is the best place to see the famous White Nights.

## 3. TRANS SIBERIAN RAILWAY

Russia has a huge area and 9 climate zones. Having one day travelling with TSR, you can see nature changing from a cold arctic zone to warm and subtropical. It is also the best way to see East and West in one day. From Russia to Mongolia.

## 4. MOUNT ELBRUS

The highest mountain of the Caucasus mountain range. It is a great time and opportunity to be a part of football, but also have a rest or do other kinds of sports like climbing and hiking.

## 5. VALLEY of GEYSERS

Another adventure to have in Russia. Valley is situated in the Kamchatka Peninsula.

## 6. KIZHI

Meet 14th century wooden structures, churches and explore an open-air museum.

## 7. ST SOPHIA CATHEDRAl

Again if you want to meet history, St. Sophia Cathedral is the best option for you. Although Novgorod means a new city, you can see wooden structures, museums and churches.

## 8. LAKE BAIKAL

The world's deepest lake and a nature paradise all around it.

## 9. SOCI

If you go once, you will return next time. You become addicted to its nature. There are hundreds of resorts to visit here.

## 10. KAZAN

The city where European and Asian culture meets. You can see mosques for Muslims and churches for Christians.
