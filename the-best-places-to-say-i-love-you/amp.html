The Best Places to Say I Love You ...

The Pont Neuf in Paris, France • Overlooking the Lagoon in Venice, Italy • The Waipio Valley in Hawaii, USA • Overlooking Big Sur in California, USA • Deserted Sand Spit in Fiji • More ...

By Neecey  · Mar 30, 2026

Saying "I love you" should always mean something but some things can make it even more meaningful. Whether it's an anniversary, a celebration or a prelude to a proposal, there are some extremely romantic places to say I love you that add that extra special touch:

1. The Pont Neuf in Paris, France

Paris has the well deserved reputation for being one of the most romantic places to say I love you, but there are certain locations that are especially poignant. The Pont Neuf Bridge, which translates to “New Bridge” but is actually the oldest in Paris, has been a lover’s point since the bridge was built in 1579. The best and most romantic atmosphere occurs around sunset.

2. Overlooking the Lagoon in Venice, Italy

If Paris ranks high in most romantic places to say I love you, Venice is a close second and some would argue it’s even more romantic. Among the gondolas and narrow, cobbled streets and picturesque bridge put even the cold-hearted in the mood for love. Lovers flock to Giudecca, an island in Venice with a panoramic view of the lagoon.

3. The Waipio Valley in Hawaii, USA

Hawaii offers several special places to say I love you. However, the Waipio Valley, named for ancient Hawaiian royals buried there, boasts beauty that takes your breath away and makes your heart swell with love. Say I love you while walking along black sand beaches, or while watching one of many waterfalls. Allow yourself to become enchanted.

4. Overlooking Big Sur in California, USA

Big Sur is a 90 mile stretch of road that runs along California’s coast between Carmel and San Simeon. Enjoy the rugged beauty by turning out into one of several viewpoints. Whisper “I love you” into your lover’s ear while being lulled by the ocean waves and wowed by the beauty of the cliffs and mountains.

5. Deserted Sand Spit in Fiji

Fiji is made up of a few large islands and many, many small ones. Many of the resorts of the area can arrange a romantic rendezvous on one of the private islands, and in some cases can even offer a champagne picnic. High tide washes all the footprints away, so when you’re there you’ll feel like you’re the only ones on the island.

6. A Hot-air Balloon Ride above the Masai Mara in Kenya, Africa

Rise up into the air in a hot air balloon in the misty morning from an African game reserve, then whisper “I love you” as dawn breaks. There are several vacation packages that include balloon rides as part of their package or on request. Some even include a breakfast on the plains.

7. The Vertigo Restaurant in Bangkok, Thailand

This restaurant is located on a former helipad on the 61st floor of the Baynon Hotel, so it’s not like any ordinary rooftop restaurant. Far up from street level pollution, you’ll see Bangkok spread out before you in glittering lights. Ask the concierge if you’d like a private table, special wine, flowers, or any other staging to go with your “I love you.”

8. The Mulia in Bali, Indonesia

The Mulia Resort villas offer resort services while making you feel you’re on a private isle away from everything. Each villa is surrounded by a privacy wall and includes a plunge pool, lush tropical gardens and an outdoor shower, plus a view of the ocean and a Hindu temple set on a clifftop. If your “I love you” turns into a proposal, there’s even a glass chapel in which to vow your undying love.

9. Santorini in Greece

Santorini (or Thira) is one of many postcard-beautiful islands in Greece. Santorini, however, is well known as a lover’s paradise, with its picturesque whitewashed villages and Oia sunsets, it proudly bears the nickname Honeymoon Island. The people are inviting and views are stunning, which will certainly make you feel like holding that special someone near.

10. The Mayan Ruins in Tulum, Mexico

The ruins, set on a hilltop overlooking the turquoise waters of the Caribbean, these ruins are remarkably well preserved for a city built in the 13th century. When you’re done exploring, take the winding staircase down to the white sand beach, where you can swim or bask in the sunshine.

11. On a Sailboat in Bora Bora, Tahiti

While the over water bungalos are mesmerizing, nothing quite beats a sailboat ride as the perfect scenario for exchanging “I Love Yous.” Set sail at sunset on a traditional Polynesian boat, called a pirogue, and sip champagne as the sun dips behind the Otemanu Mountains, with your beach attendant being your only other companion (and he won’t peek).

12. The Castle in Prague, Czech Republic

The castle is stunning; parts of it built as far back as the 9th century and it’s listed as a national UNESCO treasure. Head out to the manicured gardens for a stroll. Set on the steep slopes right below the castle, the gardens were recently restored to their previous glory. Looking out over the red roofs of Prague, what a great place to declare your love.

13. Hyams Beach in Australia

Why go to an overpopulated and overhyped beach with your loved one when you can go to a gorgeous white sand beach that flies under the radar and is gloriously absent of tourist traps. The beach is so white, in fact, that it’s listed in the Guinness Book of World Records as being the world’s whitest. And as you’re luxuriating on that sand looking out on the blue Jervis Bay, what a great time to whisper “I love you.”these three little words.

14. Looking out over the Pitons in St. Lucia

The Pitons are huge volcanic peaks that jut dramatically out of the crystal blue water. Am UNESCO World Heritage site, these peaks rise nearly 3,000 into the air. Take a sunset cruise around the island to add drama to your love declarations, then rent a little cottage and have the staff set up a romantic dinner for two.

15. The Ashford Castle in Cong, Ireland

Built in the 13th century, the renovated castle is now an elegant hotel, surrounded by a manicured landscape fit for royalty. And you’ll be treated like royalty! Take a cruise around the lake, with a stop on Inchagoill Island, the site of St Patrick’s fifth century monastery. As the bagpiper plays traditional music, take advantage of the romantic atmosphere with your love.

16. Bow Bridge in Central Park, New York City, New York, USA

Spanning the lake at Central Park, the Bow Bridge is one of the oldest cast-iron bridges in America. Because it’s surrounded by tranquil waters and lush greenery, it’s a favorite for photographers and those who are romantically inclined.

17. Fern Grotto, Kauai, Hawaii, USA

This fern-covered natural grotto formed from lava is located on the eastern side of the island and because it’s a natural amphitheater, many musical groups perform there. Accessible by boat, the grotto is considered the most romantic site on the island and weddings are often performed there. Could yours be next?

18. Puerto Mosquito in Vieques, Puerto Rico

A national natural landmark, this bay is best observed at night, when bioluminescent organisms create an eerie but completely wonderful blue glow along the waterline. The glowing shore is caused by micro-organisms that glow when the water is disturbed. Walk along the shore hand in hand to observe to phenomenon and share a special moment.

19. Old Quebec in Quebec City, Canada

This quaint historical area of Quebec City boasts a citadel, ramparts, and century-old houses. Old Quebec, and UNESCO world heritage site, is the only walled city north of Mexico, and seems like a section of old Europe transported to the Americas. See the city via horse drawn carriage for a romatic adventure.

20. The Top of Table Mountain, Cape Town, South Africa

Whether you choose to hike up or ride the cable car, the effort it worth it. Looking down on the incredible city with its trendy waterfront and across the ocean, taking in the islands, let it take your breath away and then say "I love you".

21. The Space Needle in Seattle, Washington, USA

An icon of Seattle, the Space Needle was built for the 1962 World’s Fair and has remained an attraction ever since. Soaring 605 feet into the air, see Seattle’s skyline from the observation deck or have a romantic dinner in the SkyCity Restaurant, which revolves to show you the entire city every 47 minutes.

22. Winvian Farm in Morris, Connecticut, USA

Set in the Litchfield Hills, you’ll enjoy bucolic scenery and New England hospitality. Known for its unique themed cottages, you could find yourself staying in anything from a treehouse to inside an old Coast Guard chopper. Not only do you feel hidden away from the world, but if you are inspired to say “I love you,” the resort offers elopement packages.

23. The Taj Mahal, Agra, India

Probably the most well known public declaration of love, the stunning marble palace sits in serenely beautiful gardens that provide the perfect backdrop for an expression of love.

24. Under the Northern Lights

Whether you choose Canada, the Northern USA, Scotland or Scandinavia, the Northern lights are a spectacular sight and will surely stir any romantic soul to whisper those magic words. Personally, I would choose Iceland for an extra special touch of romance.

25. The Lake District, Cumbria, England

With sixteen major lakes squeezed between England’s highest mountains – and set within a mere thirty-mile area – the Lake District deserves all its romantic hype. This is the place for long walks, picturesque villages and breathtaking scenery – and fantastic pubs in which to cosy up at the end of a day exploring.

26. Overlooking Neuschwantstein Castle, Bavaria, Germany

The stunning castle looking like it belongs in a fairytale is one of the most romantic sights in Europe. It's easy to imagine you are a princess in the arms of your Prince Charming when drinking in this view and hearing those three little words.

27. On the Shores of the Bosphorus, Istanbul, Turkey

Straddling Europe and Asia, Istanbul has been irresistible to travelers for centuries. Boasting a staggering wealth of attractions – from Byzantine churches to the minarets of its Ottoman mosques – complemented by superb food and exciting nightlife, Istanbul is undoubtedly one of the world’s most beguiling cities.

28. Riding the Cable Car up Sugar Loaf Mountain, Rio De Janeiro, Brazil

With a view that takes in the sprawling city, the world famous beaches and the stunning mountain scenery, the experience is topped off with an utterance of "I love you".

29. Patagonia - Chile and Argentina

The romance of adventure is evident in travelling through Patagonia; this immense area is both legendary and extreme, from the breathtaking natural wonder of the craggy Glacier Perito Moreno, to the savagely beautiful peaks of the Fitz Roy massif. Marvel at whales, penguins and elephant seals, and feel as though you’re travelling to the edge of the world. It can only be bettered when accompanied by a declaration of love.
