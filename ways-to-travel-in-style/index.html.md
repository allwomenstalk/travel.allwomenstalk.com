# 7 Ways to Travel in Style ...

Travelling is part of almost every one’s lives and you want to enjoy your break as best you can. Holidays are ideal to take yourself away from your everyday life and the stress that it can bring. The most any of us hope for and look forward to is an annual vacation that is somewhere nice, where we can relax and just maybe we get a bit of pampering. For others though, travelling is something they can afford to do often and lavishly. Wouldn’t it be delightful to ensure your stay is not only relaxing but filled with luxuries? Here is a list of 7 Ways to Travel in Style

## 1. 1st Class Cabins

Step into the very front of the aeroplane and enjoy a first class cabin. Enjoy excessive leg room and an air hostess tending to your every need. Some luxurious airlines even offer comfy double beds where privacy blinds mean you can tuck up and hide away. This will make any long haul flight more than bearable as well as being a great way to travel in style.

## 2. Travel in Style to Your Own Island

Isolate yourselves from civilization and become a castaway on your own island! There are websites that rent and sell islands in different locations all around the world. Rent one in the Bahamas and enjoy the hot weather and sandy beaches with up to sixteen of your loved ones. With the island there’s also a few boats and jet skis so you can explore the ocean as well. You won’t feel uncomfortably cut off from the world as you still have the option to use the internet and phone, but why would you want to when everything you could ever need is on the island.

## 3. Take a Maid/butler

Make sure your travelling experience is the most relaxing it could possibly be with your own maid or butler! They can do all the dreaded packing and unpacking as well as making sure you are hydrated and well fed when you are lounging on the beach. They can eliminate the stress of making sure everything is in order such as tickets and passports and ensure that you get the rest you desire. And, what better way to travel in style than having nothing to worry about except looking good on the beach?

## 4. Hire a Privet Jet

Why risk getting that seat with the crying child behind who keeps kicking your seat when you could hire a privet jet? Take a seat in the reclining chairs and don’t worry about who’s legs you may be crushing as all that is behind you is space. Travel in style to your destination with only the people you want to spend those precious hours with and sip champagne as you pass over the clouds.

## 5. Luxury Car Hire

Whip your loved ones around in a luxury car. You can race around in a Lamborghini or hire a chauffeur and cruise in the back of Royals Royce or Bentley. You can really travel in style relaxing in the comfortable back seats of a Maybach Exelero being driven to any given destination.

## 6. Rent a Yacht

Relocate yourselves out at sea. Wake up in a stunning cabin surrounded by the man made beauty of the interior and then enjoy the breathtaking views outside. You can travel in style in a yacht; forget damp smells, soggy cushions and wooden interiors. Here you can have plush white leather sofas, mood lighting and spiral staircases. Relax on top deck with a glass of freshly squeeze orange juice and work on your tan while sailing the seas.

## 7. Luxury Hot Air Balloon Ride

Take yourselves up, up and away in a unique way to travel in style. You can fly over the land and see all the beauty from a birdseye view. If you are an animal enthusiast you can find trips which can take you over forests and grass plains filled with monkeys and other wild animals. If you prefer a scenic route you can fly over volcanoes and famous land marks and see them from a new perspective.

There are plenty of ways to travel in style. From hot air balloons to privet islands there is something to suit everyone. With an imagination, your money can ensure that you get that relaxing break that you desire and have worked so hard for.

Has anybody got any more ideas of Ways to Travel in Style?
