# 8 Amazing Things to See in California ...

California is all about an enviable lifestyle and living life to the fullest, and when it comes around to things to see in California, you are literally spoilt for choice. There are some major attractions in California from the north to the beaches, to its national parks and in and out of the cities. If you are looking for wonderful things to see in California, and are struggling to choose, you are not alone. Here are just 8 Amazing things to see in California.

## 1. Golden Gate Bridge

![Golden Gate Bridge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/10/16_800x529.jpg)

Notably declared one of the modern wonders of the world, the Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco is one of the most photographed bridges in the world. It has become an iconic heritage symbol of California. Visit the Golden Gate National Conservancy Park to experience the history, the building, and the meaning of this magnificent structure and its glorious setting of San Fran Bay.

## 2. Redwood National Park

![Redwood National Park](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/10/25_1024x768.jpg)

A stunning coastal conservation park, with an abundance of ancient Redwoods, glorious marine reserves, beaches and walking trails. Streams, rivers, horse riding, cycling, camping, kayaking, rock climbing, whale watching and the legendary Banana Slug festival, make this an outdoor lover’s true slice of paradise and one of the true attractions of California. You can enjoy overnight horse camping trails, snorkelling and so much more, immersed completely in untouched nature.

## 3. Alcatraz Island

![Alcatraz Island](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/10/35_2100x1406.jpg)

This world famous penitentiary has a legendary reputation of being a formidable prison. But its history goes back much further than that, and it is one of the most poignant sites integral to modern American history. Known as The Rock, the prison was eventually closed and replaced with an on-land facility due to massive financial constraints in the mid 1960s. This is definitely one places to see in California.

## 4. San Diego Zoo

![San Diego Zoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/10/47_1200x686.jpg)

Started after an exotic animal showcase where the animals were abandoned in 1915, the San Diego Zoo is now home to almost 40,000 animals, including the legendary giant panda. Privately owned and cared for, it is one of the most exciting animal conservatories in the world. The zoo is usually at the top of tourist’s lists of places to visit in California. Ride the gondola and get a bird’s eye view of the different habitats right up in the tree tops. An African safari park, polar bears and the world’s largest open air aviary are just some of the incredible features found here.

## 5. The Hollywood Walk of Fame

![The Hollywood Walk of Fame](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/10/54_1041x733.jpg)

The iconic walk of fame is a public monument to the highest achievers in the entertainment industry, and runs over 2 km east to west, or 15 blocks on Hollywood Boulevard in Los Angeles. Millions of tourists say this is the very first thing they want to see when they vacation in California. See the stars of musicians, actors, producers, directors, theatre and Broadway groups, cartoon characters and much more.

## 6. Napa Valley

![Napa Valley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/10/64_639x499.jpg)

Possibly one of the most famous wine regions in the world, and definitely one of the top places to see in California. Amazing scenery, gorgeous weather, sweeping vineyards, luxurious accommodation, a laid back lifestyle, glorious wines and some great festivals throughout the year are just some of the things that make this a number 1 tourist destination in the world.

## 7. Lake Tahoe

![Lake Tahoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/10/75_800x531.jpg)

Lake Tahoe is one of the most popular visitor attractions in California, all year round. This massive freshwater lake is home to a number of winter sport ski and snowboarding resorts, complete with golf courses, casinos and luxury hotels. During the summer it is a glorious water sport playground, with skiing, swimming, jet skiing, kayaking, sailing, stand up paddling, boat racing, hiking and cycling taking place. The perfect place for the ultimate family holiday in California.

## 8. Yosemite National Park

![Yosemite National Park](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/10/83_1600x1200.jpg)

Of the many national parks in California, Yosemite is a real treat. It’s a vast wilderness that is so very far removed from the massive metropolitan sprawls of LA, San Fran and San Diego. The park is full of peaks and valleys, rivers and lakes, and also home to 3 of the 10 tallest waterfalls in the world. It also has the biggest granite monolith anywhere on earth and the land is populated with some of the world’s tallest and largest trees. Forget the activities, just stand in awe of Mother Nature.

When it comes to things to see in California the contrast is huge. Attractions in California cater for everyone, whether its star spotting, adventure holidays or luxury you are after.
