Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I visit these 50 cities before I die?
These cities are unique and offer unforgettable experiences. Each one has its own culture, history, and beauty that you really shouldn't miss out on. Life is short—why not make the most of it by exploring the best the world has to offer?
Is this list suitable for solo female travelers?
Absolutely! This list is curated with safety and enjoyment in mind. Many of these cities are known for being friendly to solo female travelers. Of course, always do a bit of research and stay cautious, but these are generally well-loved destinations by women traveling alone.
Are there affordable options among the 50 cities?
Definitely! While some cities might be a bit pricier, there are plenty of budget-friendly places you can explore too. From charming small towns to vibrant cities with affordable accommodations and food, there's something for every budget.
Can I visit some of these cities in a short time frame?
Yes, you can! Some of these cities are perfect for a long weekend trip or a short vacation. It gives you a taste of the place and you can always plan a longer trip if you fall in love with a particular city.
Are these cities good for family vacations too?
Absolutely, many of these cities are great for families! They offer a mix of activities that children and adults will enjoy, from beautiful parks to interesting museums and family-friendly accommodations.
