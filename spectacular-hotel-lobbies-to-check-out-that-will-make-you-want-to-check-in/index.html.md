# 46 Spectacular Hotel Lobbies That Will Make You Want to Check In

Hotel lobbies are often the first chapter in your travel story, setting the stage for an unforgettable stay. They offer that crucial first impression, confirming you’ve made a great choice for your getaway. As the central hub of any property, the lobby is where you arrive, meet, and start your adventure. Here are some of the most spectacular hotel lobbies that will definitely make you want to check in.

## 1. Armani Hotel, Dubai, UAE

![Armani Hotel, Dubai, UAE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a0/nk/q33wh5lo_499x750.jpg)

The [Armani Hotel](https://www.armanihoteldubai.com/) occupies 10 floors in the Burj Khalifa, the world’s tallest building. The curved lobby atrium features four interlocking steel arches that rise almost 40 feet against a refined backdrop of creams and grays. Every guest is greeted by a personal lifestyle manager, ensuring a seamless arrival.

## 2. Hotel Lincoln, Chicago, USA

![Hotel Lincoln, Chicago, USA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ht/cp/mje0qkne_520x738.jpg)

Check out that front desk at the [Hotel Lincoln](https://www.hotellincolnchicago.com/)! This boutique spot in Chicago captures a unique local charm that makes it a favorite among visitors. If you are planning a trip to the Windy City, check out our guide on [how to spend a perfect weekend in Chicago](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/best-tips-for-a-weekend-in-chicago/).

## 3. San Giorgio Hotel, Mykonos, Greece

![San Giorgio Hotel, Mykonos, Greece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pb/vu/fccqe4uf_600x900.jpg)

The [San Giorgio Hotel](https://www.sangiorgio-mykonos.com/) in Mykonos offers a relaxed, bohemian vibe. Those swinging chairs in the lobby provide the perfect spot to take in the spectacular views. For more Mediterranean inspiration, read our tips on [beautiful Greek islands you need to visit](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/beautiful-greek-islands-to-visit-this-summer/).

## 4. Royal Livingstone, Zambia

![Royal Livingstone, Zambia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b6/on/d1q41lz8_600x599.jpg)

This stunning property offers an experience like no other. See more at the [Royal Livingstone Victoria Falls Zambia Hotel by Anantara](https://www.anantara.com/en/royal-livingstone).
