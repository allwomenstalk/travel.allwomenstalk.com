# Bucket List Travel Destinations You Don't Want to Miss ...

Are you searching for clean and incredibly scenic travel **destinations that should be on your travel bucket list**? Finding pristine places on our planet is becoming increasingly difficult, but if you want to participate in a memorable and eco-friendly trek there are still some pristine spots worth traveling to. Here are 5 options for **destinations that should be on your travel bucket list**.

## 1. The Galapagos Islands

![coast, sea, headland, coastal and oceanic landforms, sky,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mj/qf/bjdw4agu5a7d915b0439d451992308_480x480.jpg)

Following in the footsteps of Charles Darwin, visiting these islands will allow you to come face to face with amazing wildlife such as giant tortoises, iguanas, sea lions and penguins. Galapagos Islands are an archipelago in the eastern Pacific Ocean off the west coast of South America. It is a province of Ecuador and is definitely one of the best **destinations that should be on your travel bucket list**.

## 2. The Antarctic

![nature, reflection, sky, glacial lake, atmosphere,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oe/tx/lw2q59355a7d919db426e447950825_600x600.jpg)

Believe it or not, Antarctica is a continent and the site of the South Pole. This ice-covered landmass is off the beaten path, but its isolated terrain shelters unique animals such as seals and albatrosses. You can take an eco-tour that includes travel by ship along the Antarctic coast.

## 3. Clayoquot Sound, British Columbia, Canada

![water, nature reserve, loch, coast, vegetation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ms/cl/kqb0iesr5a7d929293983081449495_600x600.jpg)

This huge sound is on the west coast of Vancouver Island. It is comprised of breathtaking beaches, lakes, fjords, mountains and forests. Enjoy the biosphere reserve from the Clayoquot Wilderness Resort.

## 4. Namibia

![sky, sand, sunrise, horizon, shore,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tg/i3/lgqmnzmg5a7d92d3eb30c727807497_600x750.jpg)

This African nation is one of the most sparsely populated countries in the world. It is home to the largest number of cheetahs anywhere! Visit Namibia to feast your eyes on the giant dunes, ancient petroglyphs, craters, and waterfalls.

## 5. Papua New Guinea

![nature, loch, lake, sky, nature reserve,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n6/cf/unh7zmrb5a7d9403065b2217776371_422x422.jpg)

I wanted to serve as a Peace Corps volunteer here but was assigned to a different country. Papua New Guinea is one of the most rural and least explored places on earth. According to scientists, many of the world’s undiscovered species of plants and animals exist in the jungle interior of this exotic and intriguing island.
