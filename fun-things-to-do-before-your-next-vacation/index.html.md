# 7 Fun Things to Do Before Your Next Vacation to Feel Your Best

Getting ready for a vacation is about more than just packing a suitcase. It’s about the anticipation, the preparation, and ensuring you feel like the best version of yourself when you finally hit "out of office." Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or a long-haul adventure, taking a few extra steps before you leave can make all the difference. Here are 7 fun and essential things to do before your next vacation to get you in the spirit of travel and ensure a stress-free start to your [trip](#3)!

## 1. Get Your Hair Done

![Get Your Hair Done](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/03115003_4051_600x900.jpg)

One of the things to do before your next vacation is to get your hair done so you feel at your best during your trip! Since I only get it done about twice a year, I'll wait for upcoming trips for a new cut and color so it's fresh and easy to manage while being away from home. Lots of times the water and the atmosphere are different when you travel, so you can expect your hair to act differently, too! Check out these [hair care tips for travelers](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/hair-tips-for-travelers/) to keep your locks looking great on the road.

## 2. Shellac Your Nails

![Shellac Your Nails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/03115040_1947_540x720.jpg)

Another thing I love to get done before any trip is a Shellac mani! For some reason I can't justify paying for it any other time because my polish tends to last a week as it is if it's a good brand. It does get dull and chips a tiny bit at the very ends of my nails, which is why I love [Shellac](https://www.cnd.com/products/cnd-shellac) for vacations! It lasts 2-3 weeks and looks great the whole time. If you love getting your nails done, right before any trip is a perfect time to go. You could also add in a pedi if you're going someplace tropical! This is one of those essential things to do before vacation beauty routines that make life easier.

## 3. Decide What to Pack

![Decide What to Pack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/03115249_2572_334x500.jpg)

While I hate packing for trips, I love deciding what to bring, especially shoes, because you always need to be prepared while away from home. I seem to always pack the wrong things, whether it's shoes that aren't meant for walking long distances (yes, I've had to buy shoes while on trips!) or jackets that aren't warm enough. So make sure you research the weather and know what activities you'll be doing that require your walking shoes! Don't you wish you could just pack up your whole closet? Then we would never be unprepared!

**Quick Packing Checklist:**

- Weather-appropriate outer layers
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Travel-sized toiletries
- Portable power bank

## 4. Go Shopping!

![Go Shopping!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/03115409_0630_550x723.jpg)

Sometimes deciding what to pack leaves you needing something you don't already own. The fun part is that you then get to go shopping! Maybe it's a new coat, a comfortable and stylish pair of boots, or perhaps it's a new bikini if you're going someplace warm! Even if you don't "need" something, shopping is always fun for any occasion!

| Destination Type | Must-Have Item | Why You Need It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Tropical | High-SPF Sunscreen | Protect your skin from intense rays |
| City Break | Stylish Sneakers | Comfort for miles of exploring |
| Mountain | Moisture-wicking layers | Stay dry and warm during hikes |

## 5. Pick up a New Book or Two

![Pick up a New Book or Two](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/03115613_7069_540x720.jpg)

I'm not much of a reader (although I would like to be), but on trips where we fly or drive long distances, I love to bring a new book to get lost in! It's also great for when there's downtime at your new destination. If there's a book you've been dying to read, then before your next trip, pick it up and dive in! It also makes travel time go by faster. Browse [Goodreads](https://www.goodreads.com/) to find the perfect vacation read.

## 6. Grab Some Magazines

![Grab Some Magazines](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/03115701_9030_600x800.jpg)

I also only buy magazines before taking off in an airplane. I generally hate wasting money on magazines but for getaways, I can justify it! I love [Vogue](https://www.vogue.com/) and other fashion magazines, as well as Real Simple and home decor magazines. They are fun and a good escape!

## 7. See Your Friends

![See Your Friends](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/03120523_5480_500x750.jpg)

If you're planning a long trip away, a good thing to do is to see friends and catch up. Grab coffee or brunch and just have a fun women's time together. Your friends will appreciate the time and effort you took to be with them and you'll have some cool memories to take with you on your trip! Check out these [fun brunch ideas](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/fun-girls-brunch-ideas/) for your next meetup.

Most of these things I do before any trip and it's always so much fun! We're planning a 6 day trip this December for Washington DC and I can't wait! If you're anticipating an upcoming vacation, what ways do you plan on preparing for it? Do you like to get your hair or nails done right before?
