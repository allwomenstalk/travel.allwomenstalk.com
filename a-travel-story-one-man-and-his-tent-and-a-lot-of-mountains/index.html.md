# A Travel Story: One Man and His Tent and a Lot of Mountains ...

Polish photographer Karol Nienartowicz spent 6 years traveling around the mountains of Europe. Naturally he chronicled his trip in photos. What he chose to do to make his stunning pictures different to other stunning pictures of the same locations was that his little tent featured in each one. Let's join him on a journey up a mountain - through his gorgeous photos, of course.
