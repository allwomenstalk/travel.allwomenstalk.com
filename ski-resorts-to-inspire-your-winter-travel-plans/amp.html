31 Ski Resorts to Inspire Your Winter Travel Plans ...

Whitefish, Montana • Courchevel in the French Alps • Vail, Colorado • Aspen, Colorado • Lake Tahoe • More ...

By Eliza

Even if you prefer warmer climates, you might change your mind once you see the beauty of ski resorts around the world. I typically imagine my vacation on a sunny beach, book in one hand, margarita in the other, but I've spent some time in various ski resorts and they have a draw that makes me want to go back again and again. If you need a break this winter, consider heading to somewhere snowy and cold for a few days of skiing, snowboarding and soaking in the hot tub. Check out these ski resorts and I'm sure you'll have all the reasons you need to book a trip right now.

1. Whitefish, Montana

Via Whitefish, Montana - Winter Wonderland

Maybe Montana isn't the first place you think of when you plan a vacation, but the state has some great ski resorts.

2. Courchevel in the French Alps

Via The Best New Ski Resorts ...
With a mountain backdrop like this one, you know that the skiing is going to be epic. Plus, you're in France. Enough said.

3. Vail, Colorado

Via Ski Vacation Destination Guide: Vail, ...
I can personally attest to the awesomeness that is Vail. Great skiing, wonderful shopping and tasty food all meet up here.

4. Aspen, Colorado

Via Best Destinations for Christmas Travel
Of course, you can't go wrong visiting Aspen either. You might even see a celebrity here.

5. Lake Tahoe

If you're up for more than just skiing, you'll find everything you could ever want at Lake Tahoe.

6. Vail Village

Via Ski Vacation Destination Guide: Vail, ...
The photo of the skiing at Vail above was probably enough to get you there, but this night view won't hurt either.

7. Perfect Snowy Resort

Via Whistler Ski Resort
This ski resort has so much to offer. You can see the great ski trails in the background, but the adorable village is pretty great too.

8. Michigan's Crystal Mountain Resort

Via Midwest Ski Resorts We Love
Talk about great snow. You'll wish you could come here all the time after just one visit.

9. Park City, UTah

Via Top 20 ski resorts
You haven't really experienced skiing at its finest until you've hit the slopes in Park City.

10. Hyatt Beaver Creek

Source: Coolest ski resorts in America
I have actually stayed here and the amenities are outstanding. The hot tub was awesome!

11. Whistler, BC

Via The Four Seasons Whistler
Who wouldn't want to go here? After a cold day of skiing, warm up in the hot springs.

12. Crested Butte, COlorado

Via Top 10 ski towns in ...
This rustic destination has a lot to offer in addition to the ski slopes.

13. Jackson Hole, Wyoming

Via Ski Resort, Jackson Hole, Wyoming
Jackson Hole is probably one of the most well known ski resorts. And for good reason - it's a great place to ski and stay.

14. Breckenridge, Colorado

Via Village at Breckenridge Resort in ...
Been here too, and the skiing will bring you to Breckenridge, but the dining, lodging and fun will keep you coming back summer and winter.

15. Niseko, Japan

Via The World’s Top Ski Resorts ...
Most people don't think Japan when they think skiing, but apparently they have some of the best powder in the world.

16. Snowbird Ski Resort

Via Ski Challenge at Snowbird | ...
If you can see yourself in this photo, Snowbird resort is the place you belong.

17. L'Apogée Courchevel, France

Via Three New Stylish Ski Resorts ...
Imagine hitting the slopes, then relaxing to this view. Nothing would beat this for a winter vacation, wouldn't you agree?

18. Transalpina Ski Resort, Romania

Via Home Page - Romania Friends
Romania boasts some of the most breathtaking views in the world and this one is no exception.

19. Alyeska Ski Resort, Alaska

Source: Alyeska Resort, Girdwood, AK
Of course Alaska will have good skiing. Check out this lovely lodge.

20. Telluride COlorado

Via Top 20 ski resorts
Get a load of that powder! Telluride is the epitome of small skiing towns. You are going to love it!

21. Bláfjöll Ski Resort, Iceland

Via Bláfjöll
No doubt Iceland has some of the best skiing in the world. Look at that view!

22. Arosa Ski Resort, Switzerland

Source: alpineanswers.co.uk
What a beautiful place to ski. I bet you need some experience but it would be so worth it to check this place out.

23. Night Skiing in Keystone, Colorado

Via White Nights in Keystone, Colorado
Have you ever been night skiing? Keystone is a great place to give it a try.

24. Photo Ops

Via Color Outside the Lines
No matter which ski resort you choose, the amount of great photo ops will amaze you.

25. Avoriaz, France

Via Avoriaz,France﻿
This cute little ski resort nestled in the French mountains is magical and wintery.

26. Chateau Lake Louise, Canada

Via Lookout From the Top: Resorts ...
Check out those mountains! There is no lack of great skiing here.

27. The Banff Springs Hotel

Via BANFF, Canada - Vintage Ski ...
I know this is just an advertisement, but Banff tops my list of must see destinations.

28. Ski Dubai's Indoor Ski Slope

Via Photos of Dubai’s Indoor Ski ...
No matter what time of year it is, you can get in a few runs at this indoor ski resort.

29. Sandpoint, IDaho

Via Top 20 ski resorts

30. Wolf Creek

Via The 24/7 Family Business

31. Gstaad, Switzerland

Source: aujourney.info
What an epic ski resort. It looks like something you'd see on a postcard.
