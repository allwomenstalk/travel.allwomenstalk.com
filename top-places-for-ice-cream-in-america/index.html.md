# 9 Top Places for Ice Cream in America ...

For the list of best ice cream places in America there’s going to be plenty of contenders. Sweet, creamy, cooling and refreshing – who wouldn’t like ice cream? Americans sure do love it, but the long list of the best ice cream places shows that they also know how to make it properly. The American top spots for ice cream have found the way to put perfect flavours into perfect textures, in order to provide you an ultimate creamy pleasure. Take a look at the following list of the 9 best places for ice cream in America and you might find one close to home.

## 1. Missoula, Montana: Big Dipper Ice Cream

These masters of ice cream claim that they can make any flavour you can imagine. Judging by the list of ice creams and other desserts offered, we don’t have a reason to doubt it. Beside the usual tastes of vanilla, chocolate and strawberries, you can also try some exotic flavours like Cardamom, El Salvador Coffee, Coconut, Bubblegum or Green Tea. Depending on the season you are visiting, you can also taste frozen desserts made of pumpkin, peanut butter, saffron, almond or mango. My pick is Huckleberry ice cream, made with local berries.

## 2. Portsmouth, New Hampshire: Annabelle’s Natural Ice Cream

Located on the city’s waterfront, this extremely popular ice cream shop offers more than thirty inspired regular flavours, along with additional seasonal frozen desserts. The shop with over a 30-year long tradition is famous for uncompromising usage of exclusively high quality natural ingredients. Beside the fabulous ice creams, you can also try some frozen yoghurts and fruit sorbets. The opulent list of ice creams includes Ginger Fruit, Minty Mint Cookie, Peanut Butter Fantasy, Swiss Chocolate Avalanche and many more. It is quite difficult to make a choice, but you would probably like to try Mint Summer Night’s Dream, made of mint chocolate and enriched with chocolate chunks. Annabelle’s is definitely one of the top places for ice cream in America.

## 3. Portland, Oregon: Cool Moon Ice Cream

The Portland’s ice cream alchemists have been experimenting and combining some peculiar ingredients just for one reason – to find the holy grail of ice cream. According to their sumptuous list of frozen desserts, they are at least on the right way. Among numerous variations on the theme of chocolate, vanilla and fruits, there are also some seemingly impossible combinations like Salty Caramel, Coconut Sesame Crunch, Summer Rose or Spicy Thai Peanut. Taste the magic of the Bing Cherry Almond Chip, made of fresh Oregon Bing cherries.

## 4. Kansas City, Missouri: Glacé Artisan Ice Cream

One of the top spots for ice cream in America is found Kansas City. If you stay paralysed standing in front of number of known or less known flavours and you are unable to make a choice, feel free to ask for samples. Open your mind and try something new, even if it seems jarring at the first sight. You might find out that French Lavender, Goat Cheese or Rosemary & Honey ice creams taste way much better than they sound.

## 5. Sharon, Massachusetts: Crescent Ridge Dairy Bar

Crescent Ridge is both a dairy and creamery, producing miracle ice cream flavours that have earned the shop the title of one of the best ice cream places in America. The shop boasts ultimate-creamy textures of ice cream that come together in harmony with inspiring flavours, and are made of the finest natural ingredients. Combine some of numerous delicious flavours of ice cream with some of fantastic toppings for a true creamy pleasure.

## 6. Seattle, Washington: Molly Moon Ice Cream

Happy, hormone-free cows of Western Washington provide the best quality milk that is used as a basic ingredient for happy homemade Molly Moon Ice Cream. Other ingredients added for flavour are also organic and carefully cultivated mostly in the local area. The Molly Moon menu rotates with the seasons and according to the availability of ingredients. Balsamic Strawberry, Honey Lavender and Vegan Coconut Chunk are only some of amazing flavours found in this shop.

## 7. Bainbridge Island, Washington: Mora Iced Creamery

If you want to recall the flavour of the ice cream made by your grandma, Mora Iced Creamery is place you want to go. Only purely natural ingredients make part in these ice creams – real eggs, fresh milk and cane sugar make an ultra-creamy base for your sweet, creamy satisfaction. Tasting the ice cream made in old, traditional way in this ultimate spot for ice creams will assure you that life is, after all, sweet and exciting.

## 8. Brooklyn, New York: Blue Marble

New York, New York… Everybody knows what this splendid metropolis offers to its visitors – there are so many sights to see and so many things to do. But did you know that it also has one of the top spots for ice cream in America? Award-winning ice cream of the Brooklyn’s Blue Marble is based on local organic milk and other natural ingredients. In order to avoid adding unhealthy additives, Blue Marble’s ice cream masters tend to keep the flavours elemental and simple, but yet with some surprising elements. Arouse your senses with Peppermint Stick Ice Cream.

## 9. Traverse City, Michigan: Moomers Homemade Ice Cream

An impressive list of flavours shows that Moomers offers much more than “just a little taste of country”, as their motto suggests. While you taste your delicious scoops chosen with great difficulty among more than twenty flavours prepared for that day, you can enjoy the window view of the happy grazing dairy cows Thanks to them, you are about to experience the extreme creamy texture of amazing Moomers ice cream.

After this list of top places for ice cream in America my mouth is watering. Considering the infinite variety of possibilities, do you know which flavours are you going to choose?
