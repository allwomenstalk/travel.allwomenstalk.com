# Insanely Surreal Places Fairy Tale Addicts Need to Add to Their Bucket Lists ...

Our world is full of gorgeous sights: snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, rolling sand dunes, palm-fringed beaches, waterfalls ... the list goes on. But we are also blessed with places that are so surreally beautiful it's hard to imagine they weren't conceived in a fairy tale. I'm sure you're going to love seeing this collection of pictures of wonderful places on Earth and they'll have you lunging for your passport.

## 1. Wave Rock in Australia

![Wave Rock in Australia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tt/d4/dsl4bdql573af226b99e9814001567_600x400.jpg)

Natural folds of rock have created an amazing wall.

## 2. Salar De Uyuni in Bolivia

![Salar De Uyuni in Bolivia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e3/fq/oo53xqx6573aeb2e724c4373998097_600x418.jpg)

The salt flats are so mirror-like the meeting of earth and sky is seamless.

## 3. The Colored Earth of Chamarel in Mauritius

![The Colored Earth of Chamarel in Mauritius](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/p7/sh0hr6gw573b3221b67bf590679617_600x401.jpg)

Formed by volcanic lava cooling at different levels.

## 4. Rio Secreto Cave in Mexico

![Rio Secreto Cave in Mexico](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tt/kq/vbnx2g2t573aebcc6f2fc743819476_600x399.jpg)

The stalactites form amazing shapes.

## 5. The Northern Lights in Multiple Locations

![The Northern Lights in Multiple Locations](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vl/dt/ooysbfyy573b3f6877404129612923_600x401.jpg)

The spectacular display of colors is created by ionized particles in the atmosphere.

## 6. Five Colors River in Meta, Columbia

![Five Colors River in Meta, Columbia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u9/ad/ysarztus573af12908c2a867728212_600x399.jpg)

The colors come from the various species of riverweed.

## 7. Kjeragbolten in Norway

![Kjeragbolten in Norway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g0/sv/yog6plby573b356622918269753987_600x400.jpg)

The boulder is on Kjerag mountain, over a drop of 3,228 feet.

## 8. Namib-Naukluft Park in Namibia

![Namib-Naukluft Park in Namibia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/em/kl/z1uj8e06573af20e03b10415068949_600x400.jpg)

The charred looking remains of trees have led to the area being known as the "Dead Marsh".

## 9. The Giant's Causeway in Northern Ireland

![The Giant's Causeway in Northern Ireland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g8/jy/p96q4xam573b3aa326a2e775468901_600x389.jpg)

Formed from hexagonal columns of basalt.

## 10. Zhangjiajie National Forest Park in China

![Zhangjiajie National Forest Park in China](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oq/nb/gl4y2om5573b411bd4d1f349583091_600x452.jpg)

You might recognize this as the inspiration for Pandora in Avatar.

## 11. Laguna Salada De Torrevieja in Spain

![Laguna Salada De Torrevieja in Spain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/ad/sos2s6s0573b002d228c2117114062_600x400.jpg)

The color is created by algae.

## 12. Cappadocia in Turkey

![Cappadocia in Turkey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jg/hc/rjicevre573b420434530060363940_600x400.jpg)

Formed by soft tufa rock. Homes and hotels have been burrowed into the rocks.

## 13. Hitachi Seaside Park in Japan

![Hitachi Seaside Park in Japan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/u9/q0638ai1573b540227390767784931_600x402.jpg)

The park is in bloom all year round with all colors of the rainbows varying season to season.

## 14. The Great Blue Hole in Belize

![The Great Blue Hole in Belize](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pg/g8/qmvbzsug573b340fcefd7547122880_600x400.jpg)

A huge sinkhole that offers incredible diving experiences.

## 15. The Danakil Depression in Ethiopia

![The Danakil Depression in Ethiopia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/e1/jacnduhp573b54d8c9d3f737431107_600x390.jpg)

The Danakil Depression is the hottest place on earth year-round.

## 16. Yehliu Geopark in Taiwan

![Yehliu Geopark in Taiwan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/hw/zq60fq99573af29a04026500929512_600x400.jpg)

A number of the "voodoo stones" have been given names according to their shapes.

## 17. Tsingy Stone Forest in Madagascar

![Tsingy Stone Forest in Madagascar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ot/kz/frot85qk573b3d9e4a847631755986_600x400.jpg)

The forest is actually high spiked towers of eroded limestone.

## 18. Lake Baikal in Russia

![Lake Baikal in Russia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uc/iv/b4a624yt573b53a26cc8a511567607_600x399.jpg)

When the world's largest lake freezes the ice formations are incredible.

## 19. Rice Terraces in Vietnam

![Rice Terraces in Vietnam](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lh/d0/pd5hr6lf573b4024acce7849417748_600x343.jpg)

Examples of this form of rice cultivation can be found elsewhere in South East Asia.

## 20. Sea of Stars in the Maldives

![Sea of Stars in the Maldives](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sb/x1/cndya2ry573afde463a2c830652936_600x398.jpg)

The lights come from bioluminescent plankton.

## 21. Wisteria Tunnel in Japan

![Wisteria Tunnel in Japan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mu/eb/f4q2hsa0573afe63c7df6344173268_600x400.jpg)

The plants have been trained to grow over a frame and now they create an amazing canopy.

## 22. Plitvice Lakes in Croatia

![Plitvice Lakes in Croatia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/to/jh/pumrmfdt573b52e1f29c2136510093_582x388.jpg)

The cascading lakes are simply stunning.

## 23. Glacier Ice Caves in Iceland

![Glacier Ice Caves in Iceland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cm/gl/zif14pqi573afec12d4b9600420549_600x400.jpg)

The caves are big enough for a stroll.

## 24. The Door to Hell in Turkmenistan

![The Door to Hell in Turkmenistan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bs/dk/xybsel6a573b3cfc8cfa2691593435_600x337.jpg)

## 25. Antelope Canyon in USA

![Antelope Canyon in USA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zr/hl/dpp3xfu8573b4ddf18f5a797190169_591x394.jpg)

The amazing erosion was caused mainly by flash floods.

## 26. Dragon Blood Trees in Yemen

![Dragon Blood Trees in Yemen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/ye/wwr3y4uy573b007407cf6763025532_600x400.jpg)

The trees on the island of Socotra have red sap, hence the name.

## 27. Bigar Waterfall in Romania

![Bigar Waterfall in Romania](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/wy/a8orza5l573b500d51b6f887663335_600x400.jpg)

The water runs through moss which created the unique pattern.

## 28. The Cave of the Crystals in Mexico

![The Cave of the Crystals in Mexico](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fr/uh/a0vbrgw6573b5583c4936364864421_600x429.jpg)

The home to some of the world's largest known crystals.

## 28. Grand Prismatic Spring in USA

![Grand Prismatic Spring in USA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oc/z3/d8435nk0573b3ebc79602764601803_600x399.jpg)

The largest hot spring in the USA is in Yellowstone National Park.

## 29. Pamukkale in Turkey

![Pamukkale in Turkey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/qg/gqhatxtp573b33d415a00456761670_600x399.jpg)

The name translates as "Cotton Castle" - it's easy to see why.

## 30. Danxia Landscape in China

![Danxia Landscape in China](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/h1/rasae6v0573b3c8ae4633782573592_600x400.jpg)

Formed from red sandstone and mineral deposits.

## 31. Trolltunga in Norway

![Trolltunga in Norway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vz/hg/p5b63irg573b4d590c1ee688316400_600x390.jpg)

## 32. Abraham Lake in Canada

![Abraham Lake in Canada](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/ek/meandgm9573b5079392d0708674256_600x400.jpg)

## 33. The Chocolate Hills in the Philippines

![The Chocolate Hills in the Philippines](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nr/uf/ot7z31xq573b50da48ff7250220347_600x375.jpg)

## 34. Glendurgan Maze in England

![Glendurgan Maze in England](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b6/ue/s4i4n88q573b51aee6d36068652219_600x400.jpg)

## 35. Mount Roraima in Venezuela

![Mount Roraima in Venezuela](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xm/n0/tzyx2qtu573b5618a8e6d311563408_600x391.jpg)
