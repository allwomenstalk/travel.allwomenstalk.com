# Where You Should Vacation This Year According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

If you are anything like me, then one of your favourite activities of the entire year is planning the destination for your big summer vacation. I love the whole process, the long list, then the short list, then the research that goes into resorts and towns, and activities, the whole thing gives me something to be motivated and excited about! However, what if you wanted to take a different approach and leave it up to the stars? Here is **where you should go on vacation this year according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

![Bridge, Water, Landmark, River, Waterway,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u5/ii/khmn5ltg5c671e73c181c921844354_600x612.jpg)

You need a place that is going to satisfy all of your varied interests, so somewhere like London, a multicultural and multi activity playground, is perfect for an Aries!

## 2. Taurus

![Photograph, Tourism, Travel, Eyewear, Sunglasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dz/go/leyuaynt5c671e9053b3c715449483_600x750.jpg)

Food and fashion are two of the things that you love most in the world, and nothing embodies those things more than the Italian capital, Rome. Trust me, you’ll never want to leave!

## 3. Gemini

![Street, Town, Neighbourhood, Sky, Building,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dk/al/r1u16nnq5c671eaece882181237004_600x750.jpg)

Barcelona is the perfect place for a Gemini. It's a mixture of amazing history and culture, with some world-renowned nightlife to indulge your partying side.

## 4. Cancer

![Sky, Cloud, Morning, Horizon, Ocean,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b2/gl/ab0vpm445c671ef8164b8021273082_600x600.jpg)

You want clean and classy at all times, and there isn’t a place more fitting for those two descriptors than Martha’s Vineyard! Amazing beaches, amazing fresh food, and of course, amazing wine to sample!

## 5. Leo

![Sky, Resort, Water, Tropics, Vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/xk/uwlocllo5c671f1df2385290774433_600x600.jpg)

You want a place that is going to make you feel like you are the only person that matters on this entire planet, and the extreme beauty and serenity that Tahiti provides certainly fits the bill.

## 6. Virgo

![Iron, Sculpture, Sky, Design, Architecture,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sa/xj/d6yn6xs45c671f3cc0c66016943043_600x600.jpg)

You like things to run a smooth and slow pace, no big surprises or loud noises to ruin your mellow. Napa Valley is the perfect location for you, with hundreds of mountain trails to casually complete and surround yourself with beautiful nature.

## 7. Libra

![Arch, Triumphal arch, Architecture, Landmark, Monument,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m6/m4/qxhsmn555c671f7e8dd09876964835_600x750.jpg)

You are a hopeless romantic, so there is only one true destination for you: the city of love itself, Paris! From the museums to the boutiques to the cafes, there is something that feels a little more special about Paris.

## 8. Scorpio

![Windmill, Reflection, Mill, Waterway, Water,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ji/l3/x3g944dh5c671fa325cdc916313329_600x750.jpg)

You like hustle and bustle mixed with a little bit of danger and excitement, which makes Amsterdam the perfect location. The intrigue and energy of your trip can be determined by which neighbourhoods you visit!

## 9. Sagittarius

![Marketplace, Market, Public space, People, Town,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fr/qz/h18lo9ps5c671fd2ccaea171093252_600x749.jpg)

You are here to do one thing and one thing only, party! And trust me, no city parties quite like New Orleans! Try to get there for Mardi Gras, it’s a once in a lifetime experience.

## 10. Capricorn

![Mountain, Natural landscape, Mountainous landforms, Nature, Reflection,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/no/m5/p8z7qu8w5c672016c6535850082987_600x750.jpg)

You like to live in luxury but you also like to be active when you can, so a skiing holiday in Aspen covers those two desires perfectly.

## 11. Aquarius

![Beauty, Lighting, Fashion, Architecture, Outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i7/sd/x67fh0a05c672032c61c7176681829_600x750.jpg)

You are a sign full of creativity and imagination, and somewhere like Berlin with its modern artistic history is a place where you will feel right at home.

## 12. Pisces

![Boardwalk, Sky, Bridge, Tourism, Walkway,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/pm/dh5nvsl25c672046c32dd651128145_600x669.jpg)

You want to be a small part of a huge place, that is what makes you feel comfortable. How about the city than never sleeps? You can lose yourself in the joyful chaos of New York, no problem!
