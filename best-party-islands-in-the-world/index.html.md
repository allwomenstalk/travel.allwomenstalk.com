# 7 Best Party Islands in the World ...

To some, holidays are for relaxing and sniffing up culture, but to others, a holiday to one of the best party islands sounds more interesting. These islands cater to party animals who seek to splurge on nightlife, from low budget bars to the most exclusive clubs that only serve the best champagne. These are the best party islands if you’re up for an exotic destination with a vibrant nightlife.

## 1. Mykonos

![Mykonos](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j9/cb/uzgdt6ld_600x424.jpg)

Mykonos is a great island when it comes to nightlife and it’s especially fun if you’re part of a younger crowd. There are also beautiful beaches, religious sites and archaeological monuments, and you can even do some shopping on this Greek island. Mykonos has so much to offer and is one of the best party islands in the world.

## 2. Aruba

![Aruba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ey/ii/rrjpn5le_589x388.jpg)

An incredible clubbing scene, casinos, bars… What more do you want? Nightlife on Aruba goes on seven days a week, 24 hours a day and naturally, beach parties are common here. The island is suitable for every budget, so spend whatever you’re comfortable with. Aruba is also a favorite party destination for numerous celebrities.

## 3. Bali

![Bali](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qx/tx/mlu4sime_600x400.jpg)

If you’ve come to Bali to party, make sure to stay along the south coast of the island. The ambience here is more vibrant than in other parts of the island, where culture and religion are more prominent. Bali has numerous stylish nightclubs that cater to every budget. Some have strict door policies, while others are more relaxed.

## 4. Ibiza

![Ibiza](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nc/l3/rich005n_600x399.jpg)

If you’re a part time party animal, Ibiza may not be your thing, as parties go on until the wee hours. Ibiza is home to the world’s most famous clubs and bars, including Pascha, Space and Café del Mar. But not to worry: Ibiza also has beautiful beaches, sceneries and Spanish history and culture. A great combination for a getaway.

## 5. Miami Beach

![Miami Beach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jl/hz/gedjislp_600x381.jpg)

Miami Beach offers an interesting mix of the elite, the famous and regular folks like you and me. Put on your best outfit, because nightlife in Miami Beach is taken seriously. Trendy nightclubs and stylish bars with strict door policies and dress codes are a guarantee for great parties with lots of beautiful people.

## 6. Corsica

![Corsica](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w1/i6/xmztv660_600x423.jpg)

Corsica is somewhat pricier than other destinations in Europe; however, it’s a great mix of relaxing beaches and vibrant towns that offer plenty of nightlife. Here you’ll find nightclubs and bars with live music, as well as exclusive piano bars and casinos. Corsica tends to draw sophisticated crowds that have a bit more to spend, so if you want to party in style, Corsica is your destination.

## 7. Boracay

![Boracay](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/lq/hx1vvijg_600x328.jpg)

Nightlife is a big thing on this island in the Philippines, and it has even had a bad reputation because of it. Nightclubs, bars and beach parties make Boracay the ultimate place to be if you like to party night and day, but thankfully, the island is also home to beautiful beaches, making it a great combination of partying and relaxation.

Everyone goes on a party holiday once or twice in their life, so if you can, why not splurge and travel to an exotic island? Nightlife is definitely what attracts the crowds to these islands, but don’t forget there are plenty of other things to do, making these destinations suitable for just about any age group. What is your favorite party destination?
