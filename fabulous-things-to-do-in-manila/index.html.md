# 7 Fabulous Things to Do in Manila for a Memorable Trip

Manila, the vibrant capital of the Philippines, boasts an eclectic personality. Often described as a collection of unique neighborhoods rather than one singular city, it offers endless opportunities for adventure. Exploring these diverse areas is the best way to get acquainted with Manila's many faces.

## 1. Visit Historic Intramuros

Intramuros, the city's historic walled area, is a must-see. You can join a walking tour or explore it at your own pace to admire the colonial architecture that has stood the test of time. Learn more about the city's rich past by visiting the [official Intramuros administration website](https://intramuros.gov.ph/). For a traditional experience, consider a tour via a calesa, a horse-drawn carriage.

## 2. Shop the Vibrant Street Markets

A trip to Manila isn't complete without exploring its bustling street markets. Unlike the modern malls, these markets offer an authentic glimpse into local life. Soak up the lively atmosphere and discover unique, affordable finds. Remember to bargain politely, as it is a common practice here. For more shopping inspiration, check out our guide on [shopping tips in Manila](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/tips-for-shopping-in-manila/).

## 3. Ride a Jeepney

The iconic jeepney is a true testament to the resourcefulness of Manila's residents. These colorful, crowded vehicles are more than just transport; they are a cultural symbol. Riding one is an essential experience for any traveler. For tips on navigating the city, you might also find our article on [how to get around Manila](https://travel.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-get-around-manila/) useful.
