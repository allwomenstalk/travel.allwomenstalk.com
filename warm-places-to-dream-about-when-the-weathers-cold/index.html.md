# 7 Warm Places to Dream about when the Weathers Cold ...

Warm places are infinitely more enticing when the weather is cold. It’s a fact of life. Unfortunately, for most of the world, it’s beginning to be that time of year again. Whether you’re snuggled up under a blanket next to the fire, or trudging through the snow on your commute, you may find yourself daydreaming about warm temperatures and sandy beaches. Next time you’re dreaming of paradise, add these warm places to your dream bucket list.

## 1. Jamaica

![Jamaica](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/06084314_0419_550x412.jpg)

Jamaica seems like everyone’s go-to dream destination. Personally, it’s one of my favorite warm places to dream about. I’ve been fortunate to go there, and it’s a beautiful country. Whether you experience the “resort version” or see the real Jamaica, you will fall in love with the country. The weather is always beautiful and the people are (almost) always friendly. One of my favorite parts about Jamaica is that it’s almost like the country runs on a different time. Our lives get so busy that we forget to slow down. In Jamaica, everyone slows down. Especially when we’re in the thick of things during the winter, it’s nice to dream about slowing down and relaxing.

## 2. Florida

![Florida](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/06084408_5801_600x402.jpg)

It may be filled to the brim with older people during the winter, but Florida is definitely a great place to dream about during the colder months. Whether you have a trip planned for the near future or just need to take your mind off of things, Florida is a great place to daydream about. It’s such a big state, so there’s so much to do there, and it’s relatively close if you’re in the United States. It’s a vacation without the hassle of a big vacation!

## 3. The Bahamas

![The Bahamas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/06085815_5785_600x375.jpg)

The Bahamas are a dream travel destination for almost everyone, I would assume. If you’ve seen videos of The Atlantis Resort, you understand what I mean. If you haven’t, I suggest you watch them and start daydreaming. Atlantis is supposedly a sunken city, and the Atlantis Resort definitely sticks to that theme. There are above water aquariums, huge slides, spas, and theatres, among other activities. It’s the ultimate destination to dream about when you’re dealing with a foot of snow.

## 4. California

![California](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/06090043_3416_600x400.jpg)

No matter the type of vacation you’re looking for, California can give it to you. If you want a relaxing vacation by the sea, go to California. If you’re looking for a big city vacation, go to California. California has an endless possibility of vacation opportunities, making it one of the most fun places to dream about.

## 5. New Zealand

![New Zealand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/06090123_6860_600x405.jpg)

New Zealand and Australia are on the opposite end of the spectrum from most of the world, meaning that it’s summer there right now. The outdoor activities are endless. If you don’t want to do anything, lying back and relaxing is also a more than viable option. New Zealand is a crazy country with a lot to do, making it a fun place to daydream about for the daredevil in all of us!

## 6. Hawaii

![Hawaii](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/06090245_2639_600x400.jpg)

If you’re looking for the typical relaxing, warm place to daydream about during the cold winter months, look no further than Hawaii. It’s got out of this world scenery and beaches to die for. It’s a place that you’ll spend weeks and months dreaming about during this cold, winter season.

## 7. Arizona

![Arizona](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/06090423_8544_600x450.jpg)

Whether you’re looking for a warm, dry place to dream about this year, or looking to do a little sightseeing in a place that’s not absolutely freezing, Arizona is the place for you. You can relax by a pool, do some shopping, or maybe check out the Grand Canyon! There are so many options when it comes to traveling to Arizona!

I think I’m ready for a vacation now! Where do you like to dream about during the winter season? Let me know in the comments!
