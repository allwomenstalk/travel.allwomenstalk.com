# Genius Ways to Find Cheap First Class Tickets ...

Want to learn **how to find inexpensive first class tickets**?
Some of the trappings of first-class flights are free cocktails, spacious seats, [gourmet food](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/easy-gourmet-dinners-for-tonight/) and even private suites. The standard of service in first class is incomparable, with highly trained crew able to anticipate a passenger’s needs and not be fazed by any celebrities they might encounter. Although more expensive than economy class, first-class tickets do not have to break the bank. Here are some smart shopping tips on **how to find inexpensive first class tickets**.

## 1. Sign up for Airline Newsletters

This is one of the best tips for **how to find inexpensive first class tickets** because you receive word of sales, coupons and discounts.

## 2. Become a Frequent Flyer

Fly a particular airline repeatedly to build frequent-flier miles. Accumulated travel equates to loyalty. And many airlines reward loyalty with free or cheap first-class upgrades!

## 3. Low-price First Class

Choose an airline that has a low-price first-class section. Low-cost carriers have cheaper flights than legacy or luxury carriers.

## 4. K-up or Y-up

Purchase this type of economy fare that lets you travel in first-class if there is space available. You can also get a full-fare economy ticket that comes with the option of a complimentary upgrade if space allows.

## 4. Credit Cards with Travel Benefits

Get a credit card that grants elite status as a perk. When you book a trip using this type of card, the airline might give you an automatic upgrade or award substantial miles on your purchase. A couple of good options are the Citi/AAdvantage Platinum Select World Elite Mastercard and The Platinum Card from American Express. Happy travels!
