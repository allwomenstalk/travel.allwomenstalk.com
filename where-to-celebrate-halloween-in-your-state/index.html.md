# Where to Celebrate Halloween in Your State?

Have you decided where you’re celebrating Halloween? A house party? Trick or treating? At home with a scary movie? How about something different this year? Every state has haunted happenings, ghoulish galas and frightening festivities where you can get your creep on. I’ve got a list of Halloween hullabaloos here for all to enjoy. You’re welcome!

October 23
