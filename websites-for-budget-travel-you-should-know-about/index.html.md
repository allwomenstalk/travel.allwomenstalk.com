# Book a Visit to the Best Websites for Budget Travel ...

Nowadays you don’t have to spend a fortune to travel since you can go almost anywhere you want on a limited budget. You don’t have to break the bank to reach your dream destination when you know which budget travel websites to head to. The Internet can make things easier for travelers, since there are plenty of websites that will help you find the best vacation for your budget. There are also a lot of discount travel websites that offer travelers the best deals and rewards not only on airfare and hotels but also on travel packages loaded with all kinds of perks. Here are 7 websites for budget travel you should know about, since they will help you spice up your travel experience while making your life much easier:

## 1. [Couchsurfing.com](https://www.couchsurfing.com/)

This is one of the most important websites for budget travel you should know about if you are a passionate traveler who wants to meet different people all over the world to share their experience and culture. Just sign up and start searching for hosts who are willing to offer you free accommodation. If you’re a little worried about sleeping at places of unknown people, you shouldn’t be because everyone who is serious has filled out a profile, has a lot of recommendations and they provide references.

## 2. [Momondo.com](http://www.Momondo.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is such a wonderful website that you should visit if you are a passionate traveler. If you don’t know where to go, this site can help you since it allows you to set up your money limit, the airport you are flying from and it will give you a list of all the places you can fly within your budget. You can also find all kinds of deals and advice about the best time to buy a ticket.

## 3. [Staydu.com](http://www.staydu.com/)

This website is actually a network for budget travelers (who need a place to stay) and for hosts (who want to meet all kinds of people around the world). You will be able to get accommodation from a host for free, for a small fee or even for some help that you may be able to offer them.

## 4. [Stay.com](https://stay.com/)

This website has around 100 destinations listed where you can create your personal itinerary. All you have to do is to pick the place and select the attractions that you would like to visit and they will create your itinerary. You can download it as a .pdf and print it, or you can even download their app for Android or iOS and just use it on the go.

## 5. _Lastminute.com_

On this website you will find all kinds of amazing offers for last minute train tickets, airline tickets, hotels or different attractions. You can even find deals on theatres, spas, restaurants and entertainment available in the US, in the UK or even in some western European countries.

## 6. [Workaway.info](https://www.workaway.info/)

On this amazing website you’ll be able to find jobs in more than 135 countries all around the world. This way, you’ll be able to stay for short periods of time in different parts of the world. You’ll live with the locals and you’ll learn new skills. You should know that the standard volunteer rate is five hours a day for five days a week.

## 7. [Hipmunk.com](https://www.concur.com/en-us/concur-hipmunk-faq)

This is basically a search engine for airline tickets that compares all kinds of offers from travel and airline sites. Also, their hotel-search tool has some built-in color-coded heat maps that will display the best places for dining, shopping or nightlife. This way, you’ll end up in a neighborhood that fits your specific needs.

Thanks to all kinds of websites, it’s easy for travelers to find the best deals. Do you know any other websites for budget travel? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
