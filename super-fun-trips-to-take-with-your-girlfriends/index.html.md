# 9 Super Fun Trips to Take with Your Girlfriends ...

If you’re looking for some fun and adventure, check out these ideas for fun trips to take with your girlfriends! No matter where you live, you can find lots of laughs, action and excitement and get away from it all! Has it been awhile since you took a good ol’ girls' trip somewhere? Maybe you just want to get everyone together so you can catch up while you take in some new scenery. Whatever the case, round up your pals and consider one of these fun trips to take with your girlfriends this summer!

## 1. Palm Springs, CA

![Palm Springs, CA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/1_palm-springs-ca_550x364.jpg)

Fun trips to take with your girlfriends don’t always have to be super trendy locations! I consider Palm Springs to be one of the more low key places to visit in Southern California but it doesn’t make it less interesting! Spend a weekend with your girls at a chic hotel such as the Parker, or try taking a trip back to the 1950s at the Desert Riviera, which was voted top pick as the Traveler’s Choice Award. Don't forget to stroll through the Palm Springs Art Museum!

## 2. Denver, CO

![Denver, CO](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/2_denver-co_600x399.jpg)

Feeling a little adventurous? Why not gather your friends for a girl trip to the Mile High state?! Colorado is clearly a super cool destination for those who love the outdoors. But even if you prefer to spend your girlfriend getaway indoors, there's still plenty of fun to be had! Eat your way through the artsy downtown district, tour a brewery, visit Elitch Gardens Theme and Water Park or there are always lots of options in shopping!

## 3. Charleston, SC

![Charleston, SC](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/3_charleston-sc_550x412.jpg)

If you and your pals consider yourself to be foodies, a fabulous trip to take with your girlfriends would be to Charleston! You and your group can indulge in some super tasty fare, go on a culinary tour or even take in some cooking classes once you recover from your food coma. You can also visit one of SC’s gorgeous beaches for horseback riding and fun in the sun or go kayaking at the Horse Creek Water Trail!

## 4. Las Vegas, NV

![Las Vegas, NV](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/4_las-vegas-nv_500x325.jpg)

Las Vegas is one of my favorite places for a girlfriend getaway because you can do a little bit of everything! Dine in one of the many fine restaurants, dance your cares away at one of the uber trendy nightclubs, shop at one of the hundreds of chic stores, zip line through the old strip, try your hand at gambling, drive an exotic car on Las Vegas Speedway or go rock climbing!

## 5. Oahu, HI

![Oahu, HI](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/5_oahu-hi_600x330.jpg)

Oahu is another great place to take a girl trip! Who can resist a trip to one of the fabulous islands of Hawaii, where the weather is nothing less than stellar pretty much all year long?! Bond with your girlfriends over surf lessons, or while boating, hiking, enjoying a Hawaiian-style luau supper or while kicking back at Rum Fire, the only beachfront nightclub on Waikiki Beach!

## 6. Boston, MA

![Boston, MA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/6_boston-ma_600x450.jpg)

Take a trip to hip yet historic Boston with your girlfriends this year! You know you’ve got to indulge in some yummy seafood, go on a historic walking trail, take one of the many tours such as a trolley, ghost or harbor tour or take in a concert at the Boston Symphony Orchestra. Finish the night off at one of the many watering holes or nightclubs in the city for a truly memorable experience with your closest friends!

## 7. Orlando, FL

![Orlando, FL](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/7_orlando-fl_600x344.jpg)

If you and your girlfriends are really kids at heart, taking a girl trip to Orlando is a great choice! Orlando is home to tons of attractions like SeaWorld, Universal Studios, Magic Kingdom Park and you can even visit the Wizarding World of Harry Potter. If theme parks aren’t your thing, try Miami Beach, where you can visit the super cool Ocean Drive in South Beach for people watching, live music, food and dancing!

## 8. New York City, NY

![New York City, NY](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/8_new-york-city-ny_600x402.jpg)

New York is another place to experience a little bit of everything! Plan your girlfriend getaway here and plan your day around the many art museums, shopping spots and dining establishments! Check out notable spots like the Empire State Building, Central Park, Times Square and Rockefeller Center, as well as the countless tours on foot, car or boat! Think of all the fun memories and photo ops!

## 9. Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

![Punta Cana, Dominican Republic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2013/07/9_punta-cana-dominican-republic_600x398.jpg)

The last destination and trip to take with your girlfriends is in the Dominican Republic! Gather your girls for a truly luxurious experience at a resort in Punta Cana. Take in the sun, sand, sights, sounds and most importantly, the food and drinks of this beautiful white-sand beach during the day. At night, hit one of the many bars and nightclubs and relax!

I hope you got some ideas on places to go for your next girlfriend getaway. Leave all your cares at home while you and your BFFs blaze some trails! You don’t even have to go very far for your girl trip; as long as you have good company, anyplace can be fun and an adventure! Where have you gone on a girlfriend getaway before?
