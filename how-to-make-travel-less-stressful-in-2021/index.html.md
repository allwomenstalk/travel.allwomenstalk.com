# How to Make Travel Less Stressful ...

Travel is a source of great pleasure. People love the chance to meet others and see new places. In the last year, travelers have often found traveling stressful. The rise of Covid-19 has created all kinds of issues for anyone making travel plans. These issues are likely to continue well into the new year. If you plan any travel in the coming year, there are ways to make it much more comfortable.

## 1. Pack Lightly

Inspectors are likely to have a close up look at everything you are taking with you. With that in mind, you'll want to pack as lightly as you can to avoid those long lines. Bring in only what you need. A few changes of clothing are all you need. Things can be washed when you're at the hotel. Leave the paperbacks at home and grab your library card. You can access lots of library books from your laptop no matter where you are. Everything you have on hand should be kept a minimum as you head off to a new destination.

## 2. When You're out of Town

Keep things secure when you're not at home. Thieves might know you're not home and pounce. You want to head them off at the pass with the right kind of preparation. All areas of your home should be secure. Buy a timer and set it up next to a lamp in front of a window. Have in turn off and on at a set schedule. The same is true for outside parts of your home. For example, you can install a personal parcel locker. It’s like [apartment package lockers](https://www.parcelpending.com/markets/multifamily-parcel-lockers/), but for your house. Doing so provides a place for mail carriers to securely store packages while you’re out of town.

## 3. Reduce Distractions

It can be hard to concentrate as you travel. Planes are noisy. Small children can make it hard for you to focus and enjoy your time on the road. As the vaccine goes through a rollout in the next few months, you're likely to find bigger crowds than last year. That means spaces that have more people with more to say. Get a [good pair of noise-canceling headphones](https://www.cnet.com/news/best-noise-canceling-headphones-for-2020-sony-bose-more/) and bring them along with you. Instead of hearing hours of loud noises, you can sit back and relax as you hear music. This is also an excellent way to catch up on your sleep on the road.

## 4. Little Things

There are lots of little things that will make it easier for you to have a good trip. Many people bring electronics with them as they travel. If you are traveling overseas, you'll likely face different outlets than those you use at home. Slip a universal adapter into your bag. You can plug in anytime. The same thing is true of your electronics. You want to have a handy charger around as you continue to travel. This way, you're not stuck with a cellphone that isn't working.

## 5. Well in Advance

In 2020, travelers were facing all sorts of restrictions. The new year is likely to see people doing a lot more traveling as the world gets in front of the pandemic. Anyone who is going to travel in the coming year should keep this in mind. That means making reservations well in advance. This is particularly true of [highly popular destinations](https://www.lajolla.com/guides/). If you are heading to Paris or New York City, you'll want to make plans well in advance. Get your housing choices in order. Keep in mind that conditions might change, so have a backup plan before leaving.
