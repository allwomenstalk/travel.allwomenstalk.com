# 7 Fun Cruise Trips ...

With so many places to go and things to see, ideas for fun cruise trips are practically endless. Are Hawaii’s tropical beaches calling your name? Have you always dreamed about exploring glaciers in Alaska? Whatever your dream, here are seven fun cruise trips you’re sure to love.

## 1. Hawaii

Sailing around the Hawaiian Islands for my honeymoon was by far one of my all-time favorite fun cruise trips. Whether you sail across from the mainland or depart directly from Honolulu the way we did, there’s definitely nothing more romantic than traveling from island to island with your sweetheart. And the best part about getting a taste of all Hawaii has to offer is that you’ll gather plenty of ideas about which islands you’d like to come back and visit again in the future.

## 2. Alaska

While many people think of cruising as a way to visit tropical destinations, there are also plenty of exciting Alaskan cruises departing each summer from locations like Seattle and Vancouver. Plan a whale watch, explore glaciers and attend a salmon bake while taking in the beautiful landscape and scenery. Even if you can’t wear a bikini, you’re still sure to have the time of your life!

## 3. Bahamas

Looking for a quick tropical getaway? Cruises to the Bahamas offer the perfect chance to relax and beat the winter blahs while getting away from it all, even if you can only spare a long weekend. Of course, just because your cruise is short doesn’t mean there aren’t still plenty of fun things to do. Plan an excursion to go snorkeling, swim with dolphins or experience parasailing before kicking back at the beach.

## 4. Caribbean

Ready for a mojito or two? From Jamaica to St. Maarten to Puerto Rico, Caribbean islands are popular destinations for cruisers any time of year. Whether you’re hoping to shop, learn about an island’s history or simply chill out at the beach, Caribbean locales definitely offer something memorable for everyone.

## 5. Australia & New Zealand

Talk about the trip of a lifetime! Cruising in Australia and New Zealand offers an amazing opportunity to tour the land down under and take in everything from the Great Barrier Reef to unique wildlife species. And since these cruises are often more than a week long, you’re certain to get the absolute most out of your vacation.

## 6. Canada & New England

Want to take in the East Coast’s beautiful fall colors? Fall cruises throughout New England and Canada allow you to do just that while still enjoying all the best food and entertainment. With stops in cities like Boston, Portland and Halifax, you’re sure to be awed by stunning foliage and breathtaking scenery every step of the way.

## 7. Europe

Whether you journey to the Greek isles, sail to Venice and Rome, or visit any other region that strikes your fancy, European cruises are incredibly popular choices for those looking to experience the best of the Old World. Companies like Viking River Cruises also offer unique options like sailing along the Rhine, Main and Danube, so don’t forget to check them out when planning your trip. Whether you travel by river or stick to the sea, history buffs and adventurers alike are sure to have a wonderful time.

What fun cruise trips have you been on? Are there any locations you particularly love that aren’t already listed here?
