# 7 Romantic Places for Valentines Day Walks ...

**Romantic places for Valentine’s Day** should immediately promote evocative thoughts of «lurve». There is nothing quite like the feeling of being in love, and when you find that special someone, you want everyone to know how you feel about them. When Valentine’s Day gives you a proper excuse to do something special for the person you love, it is not only about what you do, the setting needs to be just perfect. Finding romantic places for Valentine’s Day, needs to show the love of your life just how much you care, and it always makes a difference when the other person can see just how much effort you have put into making the day extra special. Doing something extraordinary, such as taking your loved one on a romantic walk on Valentine’s Day, will certainly create special memories that will last forever. Here is a list of 7 Romantic Places for Valentine’s Day Walks, for you to choose from.

## 1. Kirstenbosch Gardens, Cape Town, South Africa

![Mountainous landforms, Highland, Mountain, Nature, Sky,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h2/i7/jwe6ysvj5c5c1726120b2448700928_600x750.jpg)

Nestled at the foot of the majestic Table Mountain, this incredible botanical garden has the backdrop for some of the most **romantic walks on Valentine’s Day**. Meander along the winding footpaths into secret floral hideaways, throw a picnic blanket out on the pristine rolling lawns, and languish along the lily-filled ponds, while the bird life totter about around you. Pop a bottle of champagne and watch the sun go down behind Table Mountain, and you will be guaranteed to create a day to remember.

## 2. Lapad Bay, Dubrovnik, Croatia

![Coast, Rock, Water, Sky, Sea,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gg/we/e631lguz5c5c17a6dcae4840671099_600x750.jpg)

The gorgeous promenade walkway, in Lapad Bay, winds leisurely along the incredible coastline of Croatia. Stroll along the walkway at dusk, and pass by all the quaint cafes and bustling pubs on the way, stop in for a candlelit sundowner, and experience the romance of the twinkling lights across the bay, where you can see the sunset over The Elaphite Islands. Surrounded by a picturesque combination of untouched nature and gorgeous coastline on one side, and historic architecture on the other makes this one of the world’s most picturesque and **romantic walks on Valentine’s Day**.

## 3. Kensington Gardens, London

![Garden, Botanical garden, Flower, Property, Plant,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lm/e9/fg6xpoxg5c5c1840489f4708188646_600x600.jpg)

Whether you come here in winter, where the snow-covered trees and benches come alive into a white wonderland, or summer, where the lush green, rolling lawns bask in the glory of the waterways and fountains, Kensington Gardens are a setting out of a fairytale storybook. As the most beautiful setting for a romantic walk on Valentine’s Day, you can stroll through the Italian Gardens and the backdrop of the glorious fountains will add that regal touch to your scenery. Don’t forget to make a turn at the statue of Peter Pan.

## 4. Sunrise, Hanalei Pier, Hanalei Bay, Kauai, Hawaii

![Sky, Afterglow, Horizon, Sunset, Cloud,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ax/mx/gniun29h5c5c187ac3751826636110_600x749.jpg)

The world famous Hanalei Pier didn’t get to make its debut in a Roger and Hammerstein movie for nothing. This incredible pier has unparalleled views of the bay, and the surroundings waterfalls that cascade along the mountains around the bay, make this not only one of the most **romantic walks on Valentine’s Day** but also one of the most dramatic. Watch the sun come up over the mountains beyond the bay and be surrounded only by the lapping of the waves against the shore.

## 5. Sunset, Hayman Island, the Great Barrier Reef

![Body of water, Sky, Sea, Beach, Coast,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/in/sb/tywocv205c5c18dc0a14e192485420_600x600.jpg)

This private island paradise, perched beautifully in the heart of the Great Barrier Reef, is without a doubt, the most romantic destination in the world, and certainly the most romantic place for Valentine’s Day. If you propose in the month of February on their island, Hayman Island will actually give you a complimentary wedding, in their exquisite setting. Stroll along the pure white beach at sunset, as the crystal clear waters twinkle in the sunset, and be prepared for an unforgettable experience.

## 6. River Bank, Guangxi Province, China

![Sky, Body of water, Nature, Afterglow, Reflection,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/ai/jgpkutji5c5c199cc29fc100143850_600x750.jpg)

Colorful caves, lush rugged landscapes, theatrical rice terraces, and the lazy winding river, on the border of Vietnam, makes this destination one of the world’s most **romantic places for Valentine’s Day**. Drift along the river with ancient pagodas and dramatic mountain landscapes as your backdrop, or take a leisurely stroll at sunset, along the banks of the river, as it winds its way to Yangshuo, and take home treasured memories of ancient China.

## 7. Dusk, Nassau Paradise Island, Bahamas

![Sky, Tree, Sunset, Palm tree, Afterglow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/k8/zs45n21y5c5c19da883d5729824816_600x600.jpg)

Dusty pink sands that drift off into mesmerizing, crystal clear turquoise waters, make the Bahamas one of those unbelievable destinations, that are certainly the perfect spot for the most romantic walk on Valentine’s day. Whether sunrise or sunset, you can be assured of a panoramic ocean view that will heighten your romantic experience and take you to another level. Grab a cocktail and let the sand sink between your toes, as the sun takes a bow, over the majestic Bahamas at sunset.

Valentine’s Day is the most romantic day of the year, and when looking for somewhere super special to take your beloved, the backdrop of incredible scenery always goes a long way in making the day absolutely perfect. No matter where you take the person you love for a romantic walk on Valentine’s Day, you will create precious memories for years to come. Some of the most **romantic places for Valentine’s Day**, don’t have to break the bank, some of the most glorious settings around the world, are just waiting there for you to explore, and don’t cost a thing. Even though these are just 7 Romantic Places for Valentine’s Day Walks, from around the world, where would be your most romantic place for Valentine’s Day?
