# 7 Best Budget Honeymoon Destinations ...

What couple does not look forward to their honeymoon – an opportunity that allows them to embark on the most intimate and magical period of their lives. Honeymoons are full of romance, physical togetherness, and sweet nothings. However, what do you do when you are strapped for cash and can’t take your pick of honeymoon destinations from all over the world? Well, I can tell you what you shouldn’t do – despair. There are plenty of options for couples who have a restricted budget. To start with, you could consider the 8 best budget honeymoon destinations listed here.

## 1. Hawaii Islands

Hawaii islands have an honorable mention on the list of best budget honeymoon destinations in the world. Every year hundreds of couples looking for a reasonable honeymoon option flock to the Hawaii islands. It’s the perfect combination of great scenery, fantastic food, pristine beaches, and plenty of extra-curricular activities for adventurous couples. What more could you possibly ask for?

## 2. Anguilla

Never heard of Anguilla? This tiny isle is located east of the British Virgin Islands. Here, the accommodations are hospitable, the beaches flawless, the food delectable, and the sunsets absolutely spectacular. Whether you want to lounge lazily on the beach or indulge in some adrenalin pumping water sports, Anguilla will have something to offer everyone.

## 3. The Philippines

The Philippines is a collection of 7,107 fabulous islands giving you 7,107 reasons to rejoice with your partner. And, the best part is, Philippines gives you great bang for your bucks. Which means you can allow yourself to spend extravagantly on accommodation, shopping, sightseeing, and eating out. For once, you can chuck budget concerns out of the window.

Some of the more famous islands on the Philippines are the Boracay Island in the Western Visayas region, the El Nido Island which is famous for its biological diversity, the Cebu Island, the Pear Farm, and the Bohol Island which is home to the famous Chocolate Hills.

## 4. Tahiti Islands

If you are craving for a tropical honeymoon, the Polynesian island of Tahiti in the South Seas is a paradise with beautiful beaches replete with palm trees and tropical fauna. The town of Papeete satisfies the cravings of the metropolitan man with plenty of cafes, shops, and boutiques offering delightful local curios and cuisine. You could also take a cruise through the islands of French Polynesia to add a little more romance to the whole experience.

## 5. The Caribbean Islands

Johnny Depp isn’t the only reason why the Caribbean Islands are popular. They also count among the best budget honeymoon destinations in the world. With top tourist destinations like the Barbados and Aruba, the Caribbean islands are sure to delight any newly married couple with their wide range of white sand beaches, beautiful waters, stunning scenery, delicious food, wonderful culture, and fun activities.

## 6. Fiji Islands

The Fiji Islands count among the most beautiful destinations in the whole world. Why would you not want to spend your honeymoon here? And for reasonable prices, you can even spend a whole day on a private island - just you, your spouse and the untamed nature at your command. Who can say no to such an option?

## 7. India

India also features prominently among the best budget honeymoon destinations in the world. Few other countries can boast of such geographical variety as India. From the golden deserts of Rajasthan to the lush backwaters of Kerala, from the Taj Mahal in the North to the Ajanta and Ellora caves in the West, this country celebrates its diversity with panache. You will find it really hard to resist the mystical beauty of the land called India.

With this list of best budget honeymoon destinations at your service you must be feeling slightly better for the abundance of choices available to you. Now all you need to do is chalk out the plans to spend some fabulous time with your partner and get to making the relevant arrangements. Do you need any help with that?
