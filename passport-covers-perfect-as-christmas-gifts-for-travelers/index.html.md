# 9 Passport Covers Perfect as Christmas Gifts for Travelers ...

Passport covers make great gifts for the travelers in your life. They come in so many styles that you can match the love of travel with the recipient’s other personality traits. From covers embellished by great travel quotes to girlie Barbie pink, passport covers range in price to make them perfect as stocking fillers or a special statement gift. Check out these babies!

## 1. In the Pink

![In the Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074303_6743_390x463.jpg)

Passport covers should be fun. Got a friend who has a permanently packed suitcase in her closet? Maybe a maiden aunt who spends more time on the road than at home and you never know what to buy her? Who wouldn’t love this darling pink number? Buy it for your travel queen at goplanetgo.com

## 2. Animal Print

![Animal Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074302_6040_570x462.jpg)

Animal prints made quite a splash on the catwalks this season, so why not passport covers too? Your animal loving traveler will adore this zebra print one from Etsy. The great thing about this one is that it is eco-friendly, made from upcycled materials.

## 3. Class Act

![Class Act](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074302_6313_400x400.jpg)

Got a couple of hundred bucks to drop on your travel buddy? I love the color. I love the classic style. I love the classic tooling. I love the leather. I love the addition of the sliding clip. I, however, do not love the price. This printed calf leather passport cover is available from Smythson at £140.00 (~$230). Smythson is a London Bond Street establishment that has been providing luxury goods since 1887. Bond Street quality, Bond Street prices.

## 4. Penguin Classic

![Penguin Classic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074303_0560_599x548.jpg)

Oh how cute is this? For the traveler who loves to read, the iconic original style of Penguin Books lends itself perfectly to being a passport cover. The covers have different books featured so you can find one to match your recipient. They are sold in various places online. Matching luggage tags are also available.

## 5. Traveling Pair

![Traveling Pair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074303_4072_599x590.jpg)

Does your favorite couple love to travel? Always stuck with thinking what to buy them – something they will both appreciate? Going to a winter wedding and need a gift? How about a pair of Mr and Mrs passport covers? I’ve never been one for his and her gifts but I quite like these. They are smart, functional and affordable. Buy from Amazon at $15.00

## 6. Perfect Pink

![Perfect Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074302_4468_500x500.jpg)

Need a stocking filler? Need something very budget friendly? Need something that looks more expensive than it is? The Daditong New Ribbon Passport Holder fits the bill perfectly. And if you aren’t taken with the bubble gum pink, it comes in apple green, black or baby pink too. Best thing – it costs just $2.50 from Amazon. A-ma-zing!

## 7. Bucket List

![Bucket List](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074307_4673_600x430.jpg)

It’s fun. It’s funky. It’s recycled. I love it! It’s made from recycled shopping bags and it’s printed with inspiration for the next trip. If you know someone who wants to race cockroaches in Australia or turn water into wine in Israel, these passport covers from yeilty.com are ideal. Costs $20.00,

## 8. Superhero

![Superhero](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074304_8421_445x431.jpg)

On the whole, I found the passport covers with men in mind to be a pretty boring bunch, but this one stood out. The male traveler in your life may not be able to fly like Superman or swing between buildings like Spider-Man, but he can find his inner superhero on his travels with this passport holder adorned with Marvel Comics' favorite characters. Available from Amazon.

## 9. Vintage

![Vintage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/02074303_0325_499x465.jpg)

What traveler doesn’t like to tell people of where they have been? Adorned with stamps of major cities, this charming holder has a nice vintage feel. I love the air mail envelope styling of the edging. The manufacturer, Miamica, swears it’s perfect to secure you that seat upgrade! Available at Amazon.

There are passport holders in every color, every pattern and every price range. They make nice gifts and are great if you really have no idea of what to buy. What have you bought or are thinking of buying the travelers in your life this year?
