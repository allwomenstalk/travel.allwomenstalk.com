# 41 Sights of San Francisco, the City on the Bay ...

Some of the sights of San Francisco are iconic and known around the world, others are less familiar but still help make it a great city. San Francisco is a brilliant place to visit and has a vibe all of its own. Here are some of the sights of San Francisco to put on a visitor's itinerary.

## 1. Bay Bridge

![Bay Bridge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o8/xr/oyeedato_400x500.jpg)

Via [Happy New Year!](https://www.flickr.com/photos/mikeoria/6628307775/)
Everyone knows the Golden Gate Bridge but the Bay Bridge is one of the great sights of San Francisco.

## 2. Golden Gate Bridge

_ Via Believe in Yourself._
One of the most well known bridges in the world. It must have been blown up in the movies so many times.

## 3. Golden Gate Park

![Golden Gate Park](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kr/ib/ih8lmdh4_428x640.jpg)

Via [Golden Gate Park, San Francisco, ...](https://www.flickr.com/photos/matbest/2421647956/in/set-72157605970475393)
The park is huge (20% bigger than Central Park) covering more than 1,000 acres. It has so many features you can easily spend a whole week in the park alone. Some pics of a few of the features follow.

## 4. Huntington Falls

_ Via wildnatureimages.com_
The falls cascade down Strawberry Hill into Stow Lake.

## 5. The Japanese Tea Garden

![The Japanese Tea Garden](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rr/ot/a3ymm3kh_376x759.jpg)

Via [30 | November | 2011 ...](https://thegracefulgardener.com/2011/11/30/)
This is the oldest Japanese garden in the US and was designed in 1894.

## 6. Koret Children's Quarter

![Koret Children's Quarter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d2/ce/eugj41xk_360x480.jpg)

Via [How to spend a day ...](http://pitstopsforkids.com/2012/07/how-to-spend-a-day-in-golden-gate-park-with-kids/)
Fun things for the kids to do.

## 7. Flower Conservatory

![Flower Conservatory](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bh/db/m4yuh505_430x640.jpg)

Via [flower observatory](https://www.flickr.com/photos/sathishcj/112211170/in/photostream)
One of the world's largest conservatories, it's built of traditional wood and glass panes

## 8. Golden Gate Park Lakes

![Golden Gate Park Lakes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/sw/sjf0foxp_375x500.jpg)

Via [Heaven's Gate](https://www.cntraveler.com/stories/2008-02-14/heaven-s-gate)
There are nine lakes in Golden Gate Park and they have boating and model boat facilities.

## 9. Golden Gate Park Windmill

![Golden Gate Park Windmill](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ms/ra/p6icb1fy_260x390.jpg)

Via [20 Things to See in ...](https://discover.hubpages.com/travel/20-Things-to-See-in-Golden-Gate-Park)
The North Windmill is one of two in the park.

## 10. Palace of Fine Arts

![Palace of Fine Arts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w6/dr/tynlpoqu_424x640.jpg)

One of the most photogenic attractions of San Francisco.

## 11. California Academy of Sciences

![California Academy of Sciences](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ng/bf/rgh3jlu7_300x400.jpg)

Via [California Academy of Sciences / ...](https://www.archdaily.com/6810/california-academy-of-sciences-renzo-piano)
One of the largest natural history museums in the world, the building covers 37,000 square meters of Golden Gate Park.

Time to leave the park now and see some other sights of San Francisco.

## 16. The Green Columbus Building

## 18. Ferry Plaza Marketplace

## 19. Fishermen's Wharf

## 21. Seward Street Slides

![Seward Street Slides](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kv/s9/kphk1op0_236x354.jpg)

Via [Seward Street Slides](https://maps.roadtrippers.com/us/san-francisco-ca/points-of-interest/seward-street-slides)

## 22. The Port

![The Port](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qk/gk/gci5itkw_426x640.jpg)

Via [Sunrise Over the Port of ...](https://www.flickr.com/photos/big_pixel_pusher/6037134923/)

## 23. Pier 7 Lantern Dock

![Pier 7 Lantern Dock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/x0/hhxr9m6k_600x900.jpg)

## 24. Palace Hotel

![Palace Hotel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ts/od/a85fnkuy_333x500.jpg)

Via [Palace Hotel, San Francisco](https://www.flickr.com/photos/atelier79033/4126599126/in/faves-16592809@N03/)

## 25. The Transamerica Pyramid

![The Transamerica Pyramid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vk/ub/a6n00g9t_401x900.jpg)

## 26. The Walt Disney Family Museum

![The Walt Disney Family Museum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zd/g4/g6yn7w9y_300x300.jpg)

Via [The Walt Disney Family Museum](https://www.sunset.com/travel/insider-guides/walt-disney-family-museum-san-francisco)

## 35. Coit Tower

![Coit Tower](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/kw/ndhqkrbb_446x669.jpg)

Via [Coit Tower](https://www.ranker.com/writer/pics)
The art deco tower serves no purpose other than to beautify the city. Coit Tower was paid for with money left by Lillie Hitchcock Coit, a wealthy socialite

## 36. Telegraph Hill

_ Via Top 10 highlights of San ..._
Coit tower stands at the top of Telegraph Hill, one of the original Seven Hills of San Francisco. Did you know there are 44 hills in San Francisco?

## 37. Aquarium of the Bay

![Aquarium of the Bay](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/en/z5bifj7c_546x640.jpg)

Via [Aquarium of the Bay #66](https://www.flickr.com/photos/andertho/2988103088/)

## 38. Pier 39

![Pier 39](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/u9/nhcimris_479x640.jpg)

Via [San Francisco - Pier 39 ...](https://www.flickr.com/photos/the-o/1518562931/in/set-72157601266355933/lightbox/)

## 39. Haas-Lilienthal House

![Haas-Lilienthal House](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/nq/qf7orrti_350x526.jpg)

One of the wonderful Victorian houses that managed to survive the Great Earthquake and the Great Fire.
