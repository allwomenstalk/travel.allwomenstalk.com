## 21. Krakatoa, Indonesia

![Krakatoa, Indonesia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pb/b6/elehq0r3_500x704.jpg)

Via [Untitled](https://www.pixoto.com/images-photography/news-and-events/world-events/18855580)

Krakatoa is a volcanic island in Indonesia which erupted in 1883. The eruption is believed to be the loudest sound in human history and was heard 3,000 miles away. The eruption blew most of the island away and caused tsunamis resulting the deaths of more than 36,000. The remaining islands/calderas are still violently active.
