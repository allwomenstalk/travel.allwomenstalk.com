# 10 Great Ideas for a Girls Get Away ...

Sometimes there is nothing better than getting out of town with your girlfriends. You can relax, talk about your men, and just enjoy the girl time you've been craving! When it's just the girls, there are no preconceived notions of romance, just pure relaxation and basking in the sunshine of friendship. In planning your girl's get away, here are some ideas you should check out. Here are 10 great ideas for a girl's get away!

## 1. Back to Nature

![Sky, Reflection, Nature, Natural landscape, Body of water,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vk/nm/a8qqe6yr5c79a5058f3b2547019624_600x594.jpg)

If you're a country girl, or a city girl looking for adventure, this may be a great idea for you. Head to the Canadian Rockies for some caving, rock climbing, skiing, hiking, and horse back riding. It is a great place for adventure and thrill seeking!

## 2. Take a Road Trip

![Transport, Vehicle, Vacation, Truck, Sea,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ne/jb/vapq9gox5c79a23818129512784055_600x600.jpg)

Pick up the girls and head across the States for exciting adventures at every destination, ending up in California. If it's always been a dream to take off with no cares in the world, driving a convertible with your hair in the wind, this is the perfect choice. Life is short; go for it!

## 3. Anti-stress Get Away

![Resort, Swimming pool, Property, Sky, Vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s1/k4/eop9o2g45c79a53ecca21030835329_600x600.jpg)

Let’s face it, we all need stress relief. We are no longer in the “Little House on the Prairie” days, when all things were laid back and slow paced. We live in a frantic stress-filled environment, and we all need a break at some point. Take the girls on a spa weekend getaway. If you want the ultimate in luxury, try the Ritz Carlton Grand Cayman, in the Cayman islands.

## 4. Culinary Amazement

![White, Eyewear, Fashion, Footwear, Sitting,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/sd/img6olgy5c79a28356ace698802796_600x750.jpg)

I don't know about you, but I love food. A great way to get away from all your stress is to go to a place where the food is almost as amazing as the geographical oddities that can come with exotic destinations. For example, let’s look at Italy. The food in Italy is simply a masterpiece of art and culinary genius. Get the girls together and enjoy a wonderful girl's get away enjoying the best culinary creations the world has to offer.

## 5. Let the Waves Wash Away Your Fears

![People on beach, Clothing, Swimwear, Undergarment, Vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qs/o5/bttag3xa5c79a2b296dc2655629529_600x750.jpg)

Nothing beats the beach. It's the ultimate vacation experience no matter who you are. If you want to bump up your beach experience, go to Thailand for a beach trip to exotic Phuket or Krabi.

## 6. Say Aloha

![People in nature, Nature, Sky, Natural landscape, Wilderness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ta/ht/o54awqys5c79a44a9e5c0967810001_600x750.jpg)

Really, who could say no to Hawaii? It will always be a great vacation spot for all adventures so it is a natural choice for a girl's weekend. Take some hula lessons or just lounge by the ocean. The Hawaiian islands plus your girlfriends is the perfect recipe for relaxation!

## 7. Calling All Shopping Experts

![White, Clothing, Photograph, Street fashion, Black,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kt/wj/dhd47hta5c79a4631532c252932370_600x706.jpg)

If the girls want a get away that is all about shopping, consider New York City to vacation. You will never run out of shops in the famous city that never sleeps.

## 8. Musical Adventures

![Flag, Advertising,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tb/c2/kl16lfj05c79a47c956ee020863242_600x749.jpg)

If you are into music, check out the homes of some famous singers. Nashville is the starting point for so many musicians, both country and secular. Take some tours, hit up a concert or two, and have a blast!

## 9. Party Time

![Sky, Tree, Palm tree, Daytime, Sea,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/rw/l712v1d55c79a4b99428c317331289_600x720.jpg)

If you are simply looking for a great time, there are plenty of party sites in South Beach of Miami. This hot spot is the perfect place to hit the clubs and toast your youth in one wild adventure with your friends.

## 10. Mardi Gras

![Photograph, Property, House, Tree, Beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/tx/nyuls10b5c79a4d71a335124569464_600x750.jpg)

New Orleans is a great place to visit. During Mardi Gras, people from all over the nation, and the world, come to celebrate and live it up in the deep south. If you are a party seeker, this will be the place to be.

If you are looking for the perfect adventure for you and your friends, consider any of these ten great destinations. Who would you invite on your girls trip? When are you going to plan it?
