# 8 Top City Boat Trips ...

City boat trips are just one of those trips that memories are made of; the chance to experience the sights and sounds of a city from a completely different view, almost as if you are on the outside looking in. City boat trips offer spectacular views of urban skylines away from the hustle and bustle of busy city centre traffic and pedestrians. If you are thinking about traveling to a city and wondering which urban boat tours are the best, here are 8 Top City Boat Trips to choose from.

## 1. Vancouver Ferries, Canada

This is no ordinary boat trip puttering along around the bay. These super high-tech and ultra-modern ferries glide through the crystal waters in between the islands in and around the city of Vancouver. Look out at the resplendent scenery, snow-capped mountains and rolling forest areas dotted all along the skyline.

## 2. Staten Island Ferry, New York City

This is definitely one of those epic city boat tours that take you around pinnacle landmarks, like the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island on your way to Staten Island. Look back at Manhattan as the tall city buildings become smaller and smaller as you glide away. The ferry trip is only 25 minutes long and you can hop off and explore Staten Island, or you can just go back to Manhattan. You can do this as often as you like, as it is free of charge.

## 3. San Francisco to Sausalito Ferry, California

This urban boat trip is one best done on a clear San Francisco day for the best views. If there is a lot of fog on the horizon of San Francisco Bay, it will be difficult to get the full experience as the fog blocks the scenery. The boat trip is a perfect half an hour long and you get unparalleled views of Angel Island, the legendary Alcatraz prison, the Golden Gate Bridge and the stunning San Francisco skyline.

## 4. New Orleans to Algiers Ferry, Louisiana

This historic ferry has been zipping across the Louisiana waters since 1827, and the ferry trip is completely free! The boat takes you across to Algiers Point, where you can disembark and explore the 19th century village and Hurricane Katrina survivor. Take a meander through the Jazz walk of fame, and grab a bite to eat at one of the sidewalk cafes and pubs along the way.

## 5. Star Ferry, Hong Kong, China

The legendary Star Ferry is one of those city ferryboat tours that should be on every traveler’s list. Traipsing passengers between Victoria Harbour, Hong Kong Island and Kowloon every day, the ferry company has been going strong since the 1800’s. With more than 12 different ferries taking passengers here there and everywhere, the hardworking vessels are carrying some 70,000 people a day on their various routes.

## 6. Brisbane City Cat, Australia

This state of the art, super high tech City Cat offers one of the most adrenalin charged city boat tours of your life, as the vessels almost appear to soar over the top of the waters along the Brisbane River. The super-fast boats take you zipping in and around the riveras you go past the South Bank Parklands and Story Bridge.

## 7. Bosphorus Ferry, Istanbul, Turkey

This open air ferry offers passengers the chance to glide past the distinctive skyline of romantic Istanbul. Sit back and relax, while sipping your jasmine tea as you glide past the landmarks of the jewel of Turkey, taking in the sights and scenery as the intricate turrets and spindly architecture of the temples and buildings in Istanbul pass you by.

## 8. Venice Vaporetto, Italy

Before you think that a gondola is the only way to experience this magical city, think again. Zip around like a local on the water busses or vaporettos, and experience an entirely different vibe and ambience of the city, far away from the madding crowds in the tourist areas. Taking you right into the heart of the city, the vaporettos will zip all throughout the major hotspots eventually dropping you off in the middle of St. Mark’s Square.

There are so many locations in the world that you can enjoy exciting city boat tours, exploring the city from so many different angles. City boat rides offer visitors a chance to get a unique view of the sights and sounds of the city and city ferry tours are often full of locals making their way through the town. Which one of these 8 Top City Boat Trips would you like to go on first?
