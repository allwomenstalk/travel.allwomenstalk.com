# The 9 Coolest Cities to Shop in for Fashion Addicts  ...

The best cities to shop in the USA are also among the country’s greatest cities. It seems to go hand in hand that if a city has tons of other attractions, it also fulfils the needs of any retail addict, ok, strike that, retail enthusiast. Glamanati.com has delivered their list of the best US cities for shopping and I’m delighted to share. You’re welcome!

## 1. Los Angeles

![person, clothing, human positions, lady, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n6/ao/uvmx9lti5943087fe5bb4242590772_600x600.jpg)

Los Angeles is the next most fashionable city after New York and is considered one of the best vacation spots in the U.S. Shopper will get a chance to hover in the beauty of this city as they will drive through the Beverly Hills and to the beachside of the Third Street Promenade. Reputed art galleries like Los Angeles county museum of art can fetch awesome experience with its unique beauty. La la land is another place in Los Angeles filled with movie set backdrops and popular Hollywood name brands.
Hotel suggestion:
Beverly Hills is the only place to stay if you are in it for the shopping, and the Mr C Beverly Hills Hotel is just a 15-minute walk from the Rodeo Drive Shopping District. Close enough to carry all of your bags back to your room!

## 2. Chicago

![car, vehicle, city, human settlement, skyscraper,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/xn/e2gx7dcv594309521be52188043106_600x600.jpg)

If you plan to visit Chicago this vacation, make sure you do not miss out the place Magnificent Mile. There the 13 block shopping mecca is just amazing that has almost 460 stores and once you enter the shopping complex, I am pretty sure that you cannot leave empty handed. Everything of all sizes, styles and budget are available so that all kind of shoppers can stretch their hands to get something from there. This is the third largest city in America that provides a good impression in the mind of its visitors quickly.
Hotel suggestion:
The Langham Chicago is a hotel that is a short distance from the Magnificent Mile, and the lavish accommodation boasts floor to ceiling windows, one of the classic symbols of hotel luxury!
Book at [booking.com](https://www.booking.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Houston

![building, shopping mall, airport terminal, retail, architecture,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ba/tf/mxurxv8a594309c5d52f0867218406_600x600.jpg)

Each and everything is larger in Texas and so is the shopping place. If you get a chance to drop in the largest city of the Lonestar state, do not miss the 2.4 million square foot Galleria which is one of the largest malls in the USA. This city has to offer as many as 54 shopping centers and around 16000 retails stores, so you can enter into any to get the best thing you want. You might have an idea that this city is full with sky mounting building, malls and more but there is also many grassy park, eclectic galleries and art galleries to draw your attention sometimes from that skyscrapers.
Hotel suggestion:
The amazing Royal Sonesta Houston is just a ten-minute walk from the world famous Galleria, and provides the utmost luxury for your tired feet after a day of shopping!
Book at [booking.com](https://www.booking.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Charleston

![road, town, neighbourhood, city, lane,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tq/pe/avslj3sk59430a8a27bb1219600788_600x600.jpg)

This is one of the best shopping destinations in the USA that is filled with excellent retail scenes. This picturesque city can provide a lot of good experience if you can manage to spend some more time here. The St. George Street is just amazing that is too good for shopping. King Street is another great place in the Charleston city where you will find out many consignment shops, bookstores, art galleries, and much more for a quality shopping.
Hotel suggestion:
A bit of historic beauty is always the best way to go when visiting Charleston, and the Belmond Charleston Place certainly fits the bill for luxury accommodation within reach of all the best shopping spots.
Book at [booking.com](https://www.booking.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Boston

![clothing, blue, road, snapshot, street,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xx/qi/kjzj500c59430afb4fe1b353657137_600x741.jpg)

Looking for something chic or retro? You will surely find it somewhere in the Boston city. To explore the city at your best, start from the Newbury Street that is filled with so many shops of designer apparel and used books. But if you are looking for something funky, head towards the Garment District. This city is not just the destination for baseball, bookish collegiate types and brownstones rather you will find out the first and large free municipal library of America in Boston.
Hotel suggestion:
The Eliot Suite Hotel is a wonderful hotel located inside a historic building, giving you the best of both worlds when it comes to classic Boston style and great modern shopping on Newbury Street!
Book at [booking.com](https://www.booking.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. New York

![,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b8/f3/yjxw8fv659430bcac96be663145356_600x600.jpg)

Most probably this name would come to the mind first when we mention the word USA shopping. This city is also known as the fashion capital as it provides its customers with lots of option for shopping. Grouped with London, Paris and Milan this city has a lot to offer. If you are a great fan of indie boutiques, head towards the East village and Brooklyn to find out many reputed coffee shops, trendy bakeries and antique boutiques. Also various vintage stuffs are available in the East village while retail giants like Henri Bendel and Bergdorf Goodman are situated at the Fifth avenue.
Hotel suggestion:
If you’re visiting New York to shop, then the closer you are to Fifth Avenue, the better! I would recommend the Lotte New York Palace, just five minutes away from all of the hottest retail experiences.
Book at [booking.com](https://www.booking.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Honolulu-Oahu

![boutique, retail, interior design, shopping, window covering,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bf/ga/liyebwt759430c73d8f64571747924_600x600.jpg)

Retail giant like Macy is situated in this city that is very near to the Waikiki beach. Ala Moana Center is another popular place where you will find out many high end stores. If you want to enjoy the island’s urban appeal, this city is the best option for that. Cosmopolitan luxury, breathtaking scenario and other things of the city can make you so impressed that you will feel at peace.
Hotel suggestion:
Near to the Ala Moana Centre is the delightful Modern Honolulu resort, complete with world class restaurant, 3 different bars and 2 exotic swimming pools!
Book at [booking.com](https://www.booking.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. San Francisco

![vehicle, land vehicle, transport, cable car, street,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eo/ls/ux1ri0lf59430cac140fc133911212_600x529.jpg)

You don’t know what to do in San Francisco, really? Go shopping! This is another most popular and one of the best shopping spots in the USA. All the high end boutique and reputed brand stores you will find in this city. Union square and Mission District are very popular that can show you the vibrancy of the city. It will provide you with all the options to stay busy all the day. Numbers of jaw dropping sights, excellent cuisine, snug café, etc. can blow up your mind easily. Also if you love the edge of art, this place is most probably the best one for you.
Hotel suggestion:
To make sure you are in the middle of everything, somewhere like the Axiom Hotel in Union Square is perfect. It boasts all the modern facilities along with a great onsite restaurant for when all day shopping has left you beat!
Book at [booking.com](https://www.booking.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. Las Vegas

![metropolitan area, metropolis, cityscape, skyscraper, city,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jm/t3/jpkw37ey59430d0c5ca07077323085_600x600.jpg)

Las Vegas is also known as the fantasy land for its scenic beauty. Shopping is a really a fun here as you will be flooded with huge options. Spending time in the Forum shops can fetch the best experience ever. So, do not miss out these shops in case you plan to drop to the city for a great shopping experience. Not just shopping rather this city is as good as that for dining as well.
Hotel suggestion:
When it comes to visiting Las Vegas, you may as well go big! Somewhere like the MGM Grand Hotel is always going to be a crowd pleaser, complete with famous concert and sporting arenas and tropical-themed pools!
Book at [booking.com](https://www.booking.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

So, the 9 best shopping cities in the USA are listed here. These places are not just best on quality and diversity wise rather they have something more to offer to its visitors and you will only be able to explore that once you can land into the place.
