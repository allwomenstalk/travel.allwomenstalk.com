# How to Release Your Inner Daredevil on Your Travels ...

Travel isn’t just about seeing amazing places. If you love adventure or are a bit of a daredevil, travel can be about doing some amazing things in amazing places. If you’re going to zipline or go zorbing, why do it a few miles down the road when you can do it in a place of spectacular scenery. If you’re not an adventurous kinda gal, look away now. If you are daring read on…

## 1. Ropeless Cliff Climbing in Ethiopia

![Ropeless Cliff Climbing in Ethiopia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lj/l1/tapp42dc571cb53876e61476029410_600x480.jpg)

You can visit stunning medieval monasteries in the rocks of Ethiopia, but the only snag is that visitors much a face a treacherous rope free climbing experience to get there!

## 2. Free Dive in the Bahamas

![Free Dive in the Bahamas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/n5/o6iaqoiu571cab774b14b345028692_563x480.jpg)

The practice of free diving is incredibly popular in the Bahamas area thanks to the stunning ocean beneath. Dean’s Blue Hole is the world’s deepest blue salt-water hole.

## 3. Abseiling in South Africa

If you are going to abseil on your travels, you might as well go all the way and abseil down the side of the famous Table Mountain in Cape Town!

## 4. Buggy Rollin’ in France

![Buggy Rollin’ in France](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tp/j6/sob330ud571cac49a9e4d031695030_500x400.jpg)

Popular in France, this is a crazy ‘sport’ that involves wearing a body suit covered in wheels. You end up sort of looking like a Transformer but you can hurtle around getting up to insane speeds like 100 km/h!

## 5. Highline above a Canyon in the USA

![Highline above a Canyon in the USA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yw/lu/u1knjtk2571cad34b3570825839060_499x399.jpg)

The natural landscape of the United States is blessed with some of the most beautiful canyons anywhere in the world, so test out your inner strength by taking a crazy highline walk from one to the other!

## 6. Rollercoaster in Japan

![Rollercoaster in Japan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/h1/brva22rc571cae317911d042327784_501x401.jpg)

As of 2016, the Fuji-Q Highland amusement park in Japan boasts the steepest roller coaster in the world. Including a 121-degree angle free fall, Takabisha is not for the fainthearted!

## 7. World’s Largest Cave in Malaysia

![World’s Largest Cave in Malaysia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/hj/si73mybc571cb54eda92e284968971_500x400.jpg)

Visit the Sarawak Chamber in Borneo, the largest cave on Earth. It is large enough to fit forty Boeing 747 airplanes inside!

## 8. Wing Walk in the UK

![Wing Walk in the UK](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pz/kw/ot6oyu3m571caf67a6ecc115158573_423x338.jpg)

The county of Yorkshire in England has a long history of wing walking using classic small planes. It’s completely safety checked, but it’s still a hell of a thrill!

## 9. Volcano Boarding in Nicaragua

![Volcano Boarding in Nicaragua](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ot/fa/pndlsqnp571cb01047a79693494594_500x400.jpg)

It sounds crazy, but you can actually treat the sides of Nicaragua’s volcanoes as if they were a snowy hill and you were going sledding!

## 10. Paragliding in New Zealand

![Paragliding in New Zealand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/tj/fs7hbk5z571cb0a9ca698453095942_600x450.jpg)

The country that is described as the land of adventure travel has many many options. One is to paraglide in the mountains above Queenstown, Tandem paragliding is available for novices.

## 11. CN Tower Edgewalk in Canada

![CN Tower Edgewalk in Canada](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dj/wu/o9grmsu9571cb12cf3bf3632788171_600x450.jpg)

The experience offered at the CN Tower in Toronto is the single highest external walk on a building in the world. You can explore 365m up with the safety of a harness.

## 12. Base Jumping in Venezuela

![Base Jumping in Venezuela](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nq/oj/y5z0fgl6571cb196386a1845408167_500x400.jpg)

If you have ever wanted to jump off a vertical cliff dressed in a winged suit, then Venezuela’s Angel Falls, the World's Highest Waterfall, is definitely the location for you!

## 13. Bungee Jumping in Switzerland

![Bungee Jumping in Switzerland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ph/sm/b4fy3sjo571cb238c7f38846982773_421x337.jpg)

Just like James Bond in the film Goldeneye, you can bungee jump from the Verzasca Dam, coming in at a whopping 220m high!

## 14. Cliff Diving in Mexico

![Cliff Diving in Mexico](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/wo/g7ih7mdv571cb2906b3b2240013849_563x450.jpg)

If jumping in to water is more your style, then there is no better place than La Quebrada, a famous spot for professional divers to show off their skills.

## 15. Flyboarding in France

![Flyboarding in France](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uh/id/qjmh5d0f571cb2d3a11db442378394_494x395.jpg)

The region of La Rochelle on France’s Atlantic coast has grown a reputation for the activity of flyboarding, a super fun hobby that uses hand held jets to make you feel like you are flying on the water!

## 16. Camping in the USA

If you want a truly authentic camping experience, then spending a night or two out in bear country in Wyoming will certainly do the trick.

## 17. Swimming in Zambia

The ominously named Devil’s Pools are located at the Victoria Falls in Zambia, offering you a chance to immerse yourself in nature like no other.

## 18. Skydiving in Nepal

Can you think of a more extraordinary daredevil experience than skydiving over the natural wonder that is Mount Everest? I can’t!

## 19. Cycling in Bolivia

![Cycling in Bolivia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bf/au/f83fij2p571cb515b0080599466505_479x383.jpg)

They don’t call it Death Road for nothing! Anyone who chooses to cycle this treacherous trail in the Bolivian mountains has my full respect and true daredevil status!
