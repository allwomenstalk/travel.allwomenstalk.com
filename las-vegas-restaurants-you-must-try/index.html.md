# 7 Las Vegas Restaurants You Must Try ...

Who doesn’t want to eat at one of the epic restaurants in Las Vegas? I know that I’d love to scratch each and every one of them off my list before I did. I should get started! Next time you hit the strip or the surrounding areas, here are some of the biggest and the best when it comes to making your dining experience an unforgettable experience. Your only issue will be what you want to order off the menu. Get ready for your mouth to start watering.

## 1. Foundation Room

![Foundation Room](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d5/q4/msc0gh2c54c9635f4da3c300162760_600x480.jpg)

Located at the top of Mandalay Bay, this restaurant is one you definitely don’t want to miss. The menu is loaded with yummy things like duck and lobster at prices that you’ll be pleasantly surprised with. While you enjoy the meal of a lifetime, you can take in the expansive view out the big windows of the restaurant.

## 2. Aureole

![Aureole](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/h9/eladmpq854c96390da6c3002708184_600x399.jpg)

Not sure where the name comes from, but this epic Las Vegas restaurant serves only the best in American cuisine. That includes things like pork chops, fish, rack of lamb and a wine list that can’t be beat. Chef Carlie Palmer puts together both prix fixe and tasting menus that allow you to sample all the best he has to offer. Make sure you’re hungry.

## 3. N9NE

![N9NE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/um/gk/ye7qhefk54c963c71b7b2996375533_600x400.jpg)

If you’re looking for steak, N9NE is the place to go. Not only can you count on the perfectly seared cut of beef here, but you can pair it with wonderful sides that include mushrooms, French fries and wonderful salads. You can also enjoy the best seafood that Vegas has to offer here. Yum!

## 4. Il Mulino New York

![Il Mulino New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rd/do/nca6z06u54c963e5d1f9c008607991_600x382.jpg)

When pasta and other Italian favorites is what you crave, Il Mulino won’t let you down. Located inside Caesar’s Palace, this fabulous dining location will invite you in and you’ll never want to leave. The restaurant wins top honors for having great service and the number of options you have to choose from is just perfect. A variety of risottos, salads made right at your table and antipasti are some things not to pass up.

## 5. Andre’s at the Monte Carlo

![Andre’s at the Monte Carlo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tm/aj/rr4vcxtx54c964265cc76468326273_600x452.jpg)

Take a break from the slots and head over to Andre’s next time you’re at the Monte Carlo. Chef Andre Rochat has earned the prestigious AAA Four Diamond award and ranks high when it comes to the best food in Vegas. It won’t come cheap, but the flavors of dishes like beef carpaccio, caviar, calamari, rabbit, duck breast and salmon will make it totally worth it.

## 6. Alize

![Alize](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n1/xo/k2zea4ea54c96458040cd808192342_600x436.jpg)

Sitting on top of the Palms Casino Resort, Alize is everything you could want and more. It’s true to be a culinary experience, but you’ll also satisfy your appetite and gather the energy you need to head back down and win some cash. The wine list alone is something to write home about. Pair your favorite bottle with smoked fish, foie gras, salmon, veal or bison. The sides are just as good as the main course.

## 7. Cravings Buffet

![Cravings Buffet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cq/sb/c4dli1wx54c9646da03ea987937556_600x354.jpg)

If you want to hit up one of the all you can eat options, there are loads to choose from. Obviously, some are better than others. Head to the Mirage if you want one of the best. Load your plate with anything from breakfast to brunch to dinner. Offerings change all the time so there’s always something new to try.
