# 7 Things to do in Amsterdam in the Rain ...

When you go on a vacation to Amsterdam and it starts to rain, you should not let the rain spoil the fun. There are things that you can do in Amsterdam when it is raining. Below, I am going to give you 7 things to do in Amsterdam in the rain …

## 7. The Movies

Going to the movies in Amsterdam when it is raining does not sound like such a bad idea, especially if you go to The Netherlands oldest operating cinema that has been up in operating since the year 1912. This is a perfect place to go and watch a movie.

## 6. Shop

No one wants to try on clothes in the wet. However, there are some shops that you can shop in that will keep you dry – look around the department store on Dam Square. Be warned, this area may keep you busy for hours – long after the rain has passed. I know this is one that many of you girls wanted to hear on my list – well, now you have it!

## 5. Museums

There are some pretty good museums in Amsterdam. During those rainy days, you should go out and visit them. Learn a bit about the history and the art in Amsterdam – you might end up learning something.

## 4. Koan Float

This is a floating and massage center that offers maximum relaxation. During those rainy days, try it out. It is a good method used in order to get rid of stress. You should definitely look into it.

## 3. Interspehre Lasergames

Oh yes, this one is right up my alley. Go play some lasergames the next time you are in Amsterdam, whether it is raining or not.

## 2. Home of Anne Frank

I would want to go here, even if it was not raining. Visiting the home of Anne Frank will give you a chance to look over the tragic history of the Jewish community. This is where her family was in hiding for two years during the Second World War.

## 1. Music

Yes, music keeps you alive, right? Well, in Amsterdam, the Eastern Docklands has a grand music theatre just for you. It has been designed by Danish architects and is the state-of-the-art music complex. For all of you music lovers out there, I believe this would be a great place for you to go. Even if you are not the “party type,” you should still check it out.

There you have 7 things to do in Amsterdam in the rain. Remember, just because black clouds have moved over your head and has started dumping water on you, that does not mean that it is the end of the day. There are still things you can do in the rain and sometimes, I think it is even more fun. Amsterdam is such a lovely place and there is so many activities that you can enjoy, whether the sun is shining or not. What is your favorite thing that you enjoy doing in Amsterdam when it is raining outside?
