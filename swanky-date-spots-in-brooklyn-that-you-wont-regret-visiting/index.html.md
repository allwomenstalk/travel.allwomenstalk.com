# 7 Swanky Date Spots in Brooklyn That You Wont Regret Visiting ...

If you are looking for ways to break the ice in a new relationship, this list of swanky date spots in Brooklyn will have your wallet sighing with relief. Conversely, these ideas can also be used for simple but fun outings with your best friends. The best part about these places is that they are inexpensive but still manage to maintain a certain element of beauty. These marvelous date spots in Brooklyn will put the best features that this city has to offer on display.

## 1. Picnic at the Brooklyn Heights Promenade

![Picnic at the Brooklyn Heights Promenade](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23220653_4053_600x446.jpg)

The Brooklyn Heights promenade is one of the most stunning views in this historical area, making it one of the biggest contenders for diverse date spots in Brooklyn. The countless amount of benches littered throughout the promenade make it easy and convenient to enjoy a nice lunch or fulfilling dinner. Your date is guaranteed to be filled with awe-inducing moments as you will be blown away by the gorgeous open waterfront. Is there any other free event that could amount to this beautiful sight?

## 2. BAM Café

![BAM Café](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23212149_2571_600x453.jpg)

While the drinks can be a bit expensive, the greatest reason to visit this popular and artsy café with your prospective date is the different forms of entertainment available. To be specific, it sometimes hosts performances by talented and diverse artists. These events (which usually take place on Fridays or Saturdays) are opened to the public with no cover charge. After a romantic afternoon at the promenade, hop on the train to Fort Greene for some more entertainment in the evening.

## 3. Walk the Brooklyn Bridge

![Walk the Brooklyn Bridge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23221442_6146_600x480.jpg)

This is one of the most soothing and comfortable ways to spend your time getting to know someone. Whether you guys opt to ride your bikes or would prefer walking, the overall point is that the pure ambiance of the Brooklyn Bridge will get you and your date to open up. The scene alone (both during the day and at night) is truly mesmerizing.

## 4. Kayaking and Bicycle Ride in Red Hook

![Kayaking and Bicycle Ride in Red Hook](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23221516_8452_600x400.jpg)

Speaking of bike riding, did you know that the Red Hook waterfront is only a bike ride away from Van Brunt Street? The Valentino Pier is an alternate option from the Brooklyn Heights pier that boasts a view that is just as amazing. Get a hold of one of the many City Bikes stationed throughout Brooklyn and ride through the adjacent park. This waterfront route (as well as the view of the Statue of Liberty) is astonishing. Additionally, if you don't want to take your bike for a spin, Red Hook Boaters sponsors free kayak rides!

## 5. D&D Coffee Shop

![D&D Coffee Shop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23221549_2102_600x450.jpg)

Often hailed as one of the best diners in Brooklyn by locals and tourists alike, D&D Coffee Shop's menu consists of a variety of delicious items. Located in the Borough Park area, the food and staff are definitely "something to write home about." With its wide array of extremely affordable options, fellow foodies will definitely be inclined to check this place out!

## 6. Comedy Club

![Comedy Club](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23221619_5322_600x450.jpg)

What better way is there to start a date on a great note than sharing a few laughs together? Boasting cheap tickets and multiple comedians, the Comedy Cellar in Greenwich Village will give you guys the perfect excuse to smile. Another perk is that this comedy club sometimes features famous performers such as Chris Rock!

## 7. Bowling Alley

![Bowling Alley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23221705_8526_600x402.jpg)

The iconic Williamsburg is home to the Gutter, one of Brooklyn's remaining bowling alleys. With eight vintage lanes and a fully stocked bar at hand, there is no way that you and your date won't have a great time!

There are so many possibilities when it comes to optimum places for dates in this amazing city. Trust me when I say that your dating life is sure to be eventful. What is your favorite date spot in your city?
