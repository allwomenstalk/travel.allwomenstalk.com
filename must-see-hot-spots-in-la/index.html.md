# 10 Must\-See Hot Spots in LA ...

Are you taking a trip to Los Angeles soon and you’re looking for some must-see hot spots in LA? Look no further because I’ve rounded up 10 of LA’s must-see landmarks, museums and just plain cool hang out spots for you to explore. Even if you live in LA, take a peek at this list to see if you’ve visited some of the greatest hot spots in town!

## 1. The Getty Center, Los Angeles

![The Getty Center, Los Angeles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/12_750x461.jpg)

If you love European art, gardens and cool architecture, the Getty is a must-see hot spot in LA for you! Admission is free-does it get better than that?! If you’re headed down to SoCal before August, make sure you check out the awesome exhibit featuring famed fashion photog Herb Ritts! If not, don’t worry, the exhibits are always exciting and always changing so you’re bound to see something inspiring!

## 2. Pantages Theater, Hollywood

![Pantages Theater, Hollywood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/22_1024x680.jpg)

This theater is one of the top must-see hot spots in LA for ladies who love musicals and performance art! Feeling romantic? How about seeing Beauty and the Beast minus the animation or if you’re taking a road trip with girlfriends why not see the wildly popular Priscilla Queen of the Desert? Whatever you see, you’ll fall in love with this magnificent Hollywood landmark!

## 3. Runyon Canyon Park, Hollywood

![Runyon Canyon Park, Hollywood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/32_1024x680.jpg)

If you’re the type who likes to stay active on trips, this park is one of the top things to do in LA. Runyon Canyon offers a scenic hike around Hollywood not to mention some celebrity sightings! Feel free to bring Fido along as it’s also a dog park and your pet will love mingling with other dogs and going through the various paths. Admission is free but finding parking can be tough to find so be sure you plan ahead to hunt for parking!

## 4. LA County Museum of Art, Los Angeles

![LA County Museum of Art, Los Angeles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/42_690x520.jpg)

Also called LACMA, this museum is another stop on the list of things to do in LA! This LA landmark is a perfect place to get away from the congested LA traffic to see an exhibition, watch a film or see a concert. Admission is $15 with separate prices for exhibitions and other events so be sure to plan out your stop ahead to get the most out of your visit!

## 5. Paramount Studios, Hollywood

![Paramount Studios, Hollywood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/52_1575x1046.jpg)

Paramount is the last studio standing in Hollywood so you know this is a must-see hot spot in LA! Take a tour of the famous studio where shows like Glee, American Horror Story, Dr. Phil and The Doctors are filmed! Paramount offers fun and interactive 2-hour and 4.5 hour tours around the studio plus you’re bound to see some celebrities on the lot! Take in some old Hollywood history while admiring the future of TV and film!

## 6. Fashion District, Downtown Los Angeles

![Fashion District, Downtown Los Angeles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/62_1600x1063.jpg)

This is one of the most popular must-see hot spots in LA for the shopaholic! The Fashion District which is home to the infamous Santee Alley offers fashion and accessories for the entire family at low prices. If you don’t like to spend a lot on trendy pieces, the Fashion District is the perfect spot to get some basics or get some stylish outfits. There is no admission fee and it gets pretty crowded, so the earlier you get there, the better!

## 7. Venice Beach Boardwalk, Venice Beach

![Venice Beach Boardwalk, Venice Beach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/72_1024x768.jpg)

You can’t take a trip to LA without stopping at this popular hangout in LA! The boardwalk is home to the famous coastline, shopping, dining and you can always find some interesting characters strolling around which should make for an interesting afternoon! Don’t forget to admire the Venice Art Walls and look out for the monthly art walk!

## 8. Rodeo Drive, Beverly Hills

![Rodeo Drive, Beverly Hills](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/81_800x485.jpg)

If you’re feeling rich and famous or just want to mingle with them, Rodeo Drive is one of the must-see hot spots in LA! Take a leisurely walk past three blocks of designer stores like Dior, Gucci, Harry Winston and Badgley Mischka to name a few and then stop at one of the local eateries for a bite. Many celebs have been spotted shopping here so make sure you take your camera!

## 9. Sunset Boulevard, Los Angeles

![Sunset Boulevard, Los Angeles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/91_962x1024.jpg)

Take an excursion at this popular hangout in LA for a strip filled with nightclubs, restaurants and boutiques. Sunset is also home to the Chateau Marmont and the Mondrian hotels which boast fabulous bars and scenic views overlooking Hollywood. If you’re looking for a hotel, check out the Chateau Marmont which is full of glamour and rich with Hollywood history!

## 10. Santa Monica Pier, Santa Monica

![Santa Monica Pier, Santa Monica](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/travel/2012/07/10_4752x3168.jpg)

The Santa Monica Pier is another one of the must-see hot spots in LA for the beach and all the old-school games and rides. Bring out your inner child and hop on the carousel or try your hand at some of the games. If you visit, make sure you stop at one of the amazing seafood restaurants and stop to visit with one of the many street performers performing at the pier!

These are just 10 of the many must-see hot spots in LA! There’s something for everyone, so check out some or all of these destinations to get a taste of LA culture. There’s always something interesting to be found in every nook and cranny in Southern California so make sure you come by for a visit!
