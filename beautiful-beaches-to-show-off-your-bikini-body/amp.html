Frequently Asked Questions

Where are some of the best beaches to flaunt your bikini body?
Oh, there are so many! Think of places like Miami Beach, Copacabana in Brazil, or those stunning beaches in Thailand. It's all about feeling fabulous and soaking in that sun!
Is wearing a bikini allowed on all beaches in India?
Not everywhere, but there are definitely some where it's perfectly okay! Popular tourist spots like Goa and some beaches in Kerala are generally more relaxed about it.
What makes a beach perfect for bikinis?
It's all about the vibe! Somewhere warm with clear blue water, soft sand, and a place where you can relax and enjoy the sun without any worries. Throw in a good beachside bar and you're set!
Where can I find micro bikini-friendly beaches?
Europe has quite a few, like the beaches in Spain and Greece. Of course, South Beach in Miami is also known for its more daring beachwear. It's all about knowing the crowd and feeling comfortable.
If I want to see world's best beach bodies, where should I go?
Definitely check out places like Bondi Beach in Australia or Rio’s famous beaches in Brazil. They’re like hubs for athleticism and beautiful beach bodies!
