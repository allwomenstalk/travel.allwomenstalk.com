# 37 Chinatowns to Visit for Chinese New Year ...

There are few annual celebrations to rival Chinese New Year. The sights, sounds, and smells are incredible and the colors just increase the joy of the occasion. With amazing food, dragon dances and fireworks, if you are anywhere near a Chinatown at New Year, you really owe it to yourself to go and partake of an amazing spectacle. Go herald in the Chinese New Year at any of these aromatic, colorful and fascinating Chinatowns around the world.
