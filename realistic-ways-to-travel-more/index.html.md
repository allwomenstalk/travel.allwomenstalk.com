# Realistic Ways to Travel More without Spending Loads of Cash ...

If you crave adventure, [Elite Daily](https://www.elitedaily.com/life/culture/turn-traveling-to-reality/1191892) has some advice for you. After all, most of us don't have money to waste on plane tickets and hotel rooms. That's why it helps to hear some realistic advice for a change. Here are a few things that will help you get the adventure you crave:

## 1. Visit Your Friends and Family in Other States

![Visit Your Friends and Family in Other States](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b9/o3/r2tle6ey560d467aaa5e0435908484_500x500.jpg)

If you have family in Florida, use it as an excuse to hop on a plane and see the beaches.

## 2. Travel during the off Season

![Travel during the off Season](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qg/qy/od55emj7560d467b509f3144170233_467x700.jpg)

DOn't go on vacation during the summer or around the holidays, because that's when everyone has the same idea. It'll be cheaper if you go in the middle of fall or spring.

## 3. Find Seat Sales

![Find Seat Sales](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qq/qj/m41bufdz560d467b47910111377379_500x500.jpg)

Instead of picking a vacation spot and then finding deals for it, do the opposite. If there's a sale for tickets to Jamaica, then choose that for your next vacation.

## 4. Stay in a Cheap Hotel

![Stay in a Cheap Hotel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hj/qf/ip6xq3zs560d467b8fd93856350388_500x596.jpg)

You don't need a room with an ocean view. Buy a cheap hotel, because you won't be spending much time in it, anyway.

## 5. Get a Second Job

![Get a Second Job](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d6/ed/ub41c41q560d467b8a533446977373_500x582.jpg)

If you have time to work two jobs, go for it. Two paychecks are better than one.

## 6. Budget Your Money

![Budget Your Money](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/sd/x267iy6n560d467c13ab0531897842_500x700.jpg)

You can't spend your money willy nilly. You need to have a plan for spending and saving.

## 7. Live at Home

![Live at Home](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q0/qu/rqknndbg560d467c68801515072822_391x498.jpg)

The longer you live at home, the more money you'll be able to save up.

## 8. Visit Local Destinations

![Visit Local Destinations](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rk/t1/g4jnu6iz560d467cc35e7105492894_500x750.jpg)

There are exciting local landmarks minutes away from your house that you don't need a plane ticket to visit.

## 9. Pack Light

![Pack Light](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/re/vk/jz54n6o9560d467cb331a325557917_400x400.jpg)

That way, you won't have to pay extra money for bringing your baggage on a plane.

If you follow these tips, you'll be able to go on plenty of adventures. What other realistic pieces of travel advice do you have?
