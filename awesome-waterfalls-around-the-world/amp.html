By Neecey • 4 Comments

Waterfalls are one of my favorite creations of Mother Nature. I am a real water baby so the wet stuff in any form has always held a fascination for me. If there is a waterfall anywhere near where I am staying on a trip, I'll find time to visit. Big or small, I love them all. Here are some awesome waterfalls from around the world.

3 Wailua Falls, USA

Via Wailua Falls by William Waterfall ...
There are so many gorgeous waterfalls in Hawaii.

***

Wailua Falls is a gem on the island of Kauai, often catching the eye of travelers with its easily accessible beauty. Cascading at an impressive height of around 173 feet, these falls are a sight to behold as they plunge into the serene pool below. For those seeking a closer experience, a short hike can lead to the base where the vibrant hues of the tropical landscape complement the misty spray. Many visitors recognize the falls from the opening credits of the television show "Fantasy Island," adding to its iconic status and allure.

7 Gitgit Twin Waterfalls and Emerald Pool, Indonesia

Via Gitgit Waterfall (Singaraja, Indonesia): Address, ...
One of the great places to visit in Bali.

***

One of the great places to visit in Bali, Gitgit Twin Waterfalls are an awe-inspiring natural wonder. These stunning waterfalls plunge into a crystal-clear emerald pool, surrounded by lush tropical greenery, creating a serene sanctuary for visitors. Adventure enthusiasts can trek through the scenic pathways to reach the falls, capturing breathtaking views along the way. The tranquil ambiance and the invigorating cool waters make it an idyllic spot to unwind and connect with nature. Whether you're a solo traveler or exploring with loved ones, this captivating destination promises a rejuvenating experience that will leave a lasting impression.

19 Duden Waterfalls, Turkey

Via All sizes | Duden waterval ...
The Duden Waterfalls is a series of falls on the Duden River that end when the cliffs reach the Mediterranean Sea.

***

The water cascades down from a mighty 40 meters, creating a spectacle of mist and roaring sound that is both exhilarating and tranquil. Visitors can explore the surrounding gardens that offer a lush, verdant escape and peek into hidden caves behind the waterfall's veil. Duden Waterfalls is not only a feast for the eyes but also a refuge for the soul, where you can watch the river dramatically spill into the azure embrace of the Mediterranean. It’s the perfect natural backdrop for any adventurous traveler or anyone looking to add a touch of nature's poetry to their journey.

32 Dunn's River Falls, Jamaica

Via Dunn Falls _ by Hannes ...
I'm happy to describe this as a waterfall in tropical paradise.

***

I'm happy to describe this as a waterfall in tropical paradise. Located near Ocho Rios, Dunn's River Falls is one of Jamaica's most iconic landmarks. Visitors can climb the terraced giant, as it cascades 600 feet down smooth limestone rocks into the Caribbean Sea. The surrounding lush greenery and tropical flora create a breathtaking backdrop, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Whether you're looking to take a refreshing dip in the pools or simply marvel at the stunning scenery, Dunn's River Falls promises an unforgettable experience in one of the world's most beautiful settings.

34 Agua Azul Waterfall, Mexico

Via Yahoo - login
Shallow falls are just as marvelous as towering plunges.

***

Shallow falls are just as marvelous as towering plunges. The Agua Azul Waterfall in Mexico cascades through a series of stunning turquoise pools, creating a paradise-like ambiance. The vibrant blue waters result from high mineral content, reflecting the lush jungle surroundings. Visitors can swim in the serene pools, hike along the scenic paths, or simply revel in the breathtaking views. Whether admiring from afar or diving in for a closer look, Agua Azul offers a tranquil escape into nature's beauty. This natural wonder is a must-see for travelers craving a blend of adventure and relaxation in Mexico's heart.
